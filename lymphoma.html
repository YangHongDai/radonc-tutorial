何杰金淋巴瘤（HL）基本概念、臨床表現、檢查評估、分期與 Deauville 評分

一、何杰金淋巴瘤（HL）基本概念

何杰金淋巴瘤（HL）相對少見，約占美國淋巴瘤的 10%。年齡分布呈雙峰：20–29 歲與 65 歲以上；男性略多，約 1.2:1。整體預後很好，對治療高度敏感，5 年整體存活（OS）超過 85%。HL 分為經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL）與非經典型何杰金淋巴瘤。經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL）包含結節硬化型、混合細胞型、富淋巴球型、淋巴球耗竭型；非經典型何杰金淋巴瘤主要是結節性淋巴球優勢型何杰金淋巴瘤（NLPHL），目前正逐步重新分類為結節性淋巴球優勢型 B 細胞淋巴瘤（NLPBL）。經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL）的 Reed–Sternberg 細胞通常 CD15+、CD30+、CD20−、CD45−；NLPHL 的爆米花細胞則 CD20+、CD45+、CD15−、CD30−。

二、HL 臨床表現與檢查評估

最常見表現是無痛性淋巴結腫大。HL 常呈連續性淋巴結擴散，約 90% 會沿相鄰淋巴結區域擴散；器官侵犯可經直接侵犯或血行轉移，脾臟是最常見結外部位。其他非 B 症狀包括搔癢、疲倦、飲酒誘發疼痛。B 症狀約見於 1/3 病人，包括發燒 >38°C、6 個月內體重下降 >10%、大量盜汗；B 症狀是不良預後因子，也影響分期與風險分層。危險因子包括人類免疫缺乏病毒（HIV）、EBV、免疫低下與家族史。

檢查評估包括病史與理學檢查（H&P）、B 症狀評估、完整淋巴結區域理學檢查、胸腹部檢查、肝脾腫大評估，以及年輕病人治療前生育力保存討論。實驗室包括全血球計數與分類（CBC）、完整代謝檢查／基礎代謝檢查／肝功能／白蛋白（CMP／BMP／LFT／白蛋白）、乳酸脫氫酶（LDH）、紅血球沉降速率（ESR）、妊娠檢驗、人類免疫缺乏病毒（HIV）、B 型肝炎病毒（HBV）、C 型肝炎病毒（HCV）。影像以正子電腦斷層（PET/CT）為主。若考慮多柔比星，需心臟超音波或多門控心室造影（MUGA）；若使用博來黴素，需肺功能檢查（PFT）與一氧化碳擴散能力（DLCO）。病理上淋巴結切除切片最理想，因為需要看淋巴結結構，且 Reed–Sternberg 細胞只佔腫瘤體積 1–2%；粗針切片有時可接受，但細針抽吸（FNA）不足。骨髓切片只在無法解釋的血球低下或正子電腦斷層（PET/CT）懷疑骨髓侵犯時需要，因骨髓侵犯機率低於 5%。

三、HL 分期與風險分層

HL 使用 Ann Arbor／Lugano 分期。第 I 期是單一淋巴結或單一結外病灶；第 II 期是橫膈同側兩個以上淋巴結群或有限的連續性結外侵犯；第 III 期是橫膈兩側淋巴結，或橫膈上淋巴結加脾臟侵犯；第 IV 期是非連續性結外侵犯。附加標記包括 A/B、E、X，其中 B 代表 B 症狀，X 代表巨大腫塊疾病，通常 ≥10 cm 或超過胸腔直徑 1/3。

早期 HL 分有利型與不利型，但 GHSG、EORTC、NCCN 的定義略不同。常見不利因子包括紅血球沉降速率（ESR）升高、B 症狀、縱膈腫塊、淋巴結區域數目較多、結外病灶、巨大腫塊疾病。GHSG 常用 MMR >0.33、淋巴結區域 >2、任何結外病灶、紅血球沉降速率（ESR） >50 若 A 或 >30 若 B；EORTC 加入年齡 ≥50、MTR >0.35、淋巴結區域 >3；NCCN 則常用紅血球沉降速率（ESR） ≥50 或任何 B 症狀、MMR >0.33、淋巴結區域 >3、巨大腫塊 ≥10 cm。

晚期何杰金淋巴瘤（HL）使用 IPS，口訣是 SAW MEAL：第 IV 期、年齡 ≥45、WBC ≥15,000/mm³、男性、血紅素（Hgb） <10.5 g/dL、白蛋白 <4 g/dL、淋巴球 <600/mm³ 或 <8% WBC。分數越高，無惡化存活（PFS）與整體存活（OS）越差。

Part I. Hodgkin lymphoma overview

1. Basic concept

Hodgkin lymphoma is relatively uncommon, accounting for about 10% of lymphomas in the United States. Its age distribution is bimodal, with peaks at 20–29 years and above 65 years. It is slightly more common in men, with a male-to-female ratio of about 1.2:1. Overall prognosis is excellent, the disease is highly treatment-sensitive, and 5-year overall survival exceeds 85%. HL is divided into classic HL and non-classic HL. Classic HL includes nodular sclerosing, mixed cellularity, lymphocyte-rich, and lymphocyte-depleted subtypes. The major non-classic entity is NLPHL, which is increasingly being reclassified as NLPBL. Reed-Sternberg cells in classic HL are usually CD15+, CD30+, CD20−, and CD45−, whereas the popcorn cells of NLPHL are CD20+, CD45+, CD15−, and CD30−.

2. Clinical presentation and workup

The most common presentation is painless lymphadenopathy. HL often spreads contiguously through lymph-node regions; about 90% of cases spread along adjacent nodal regions. Organ involvement may occur by direct extension or hematogenous spread, and the spleen is the most common extranodal site. Non-B symptoms include pruritus, fatigue, and alcohol-induced pain. B symptoms occur in about one-third of patients and include fever above 38°C, more than 10% weight loss within 6 months, and drenching night sweats. B symptoms are adverse prognostic factors and affect staging and risk stratification. Risk factors include HIV, EBV, immunosuppression, and family history.

Workup includes history and physical examination, assessment of B symptoms, full nodal examination, chest and abdominal examination, hepatosplenomegaly assessment, and fertility preservation discussion in younger patients before treatment. Laboratory tests include CBC with differential, CMP/BMP/LFT/albumin, LDH, ESR, pregnancy test, HIV, HBV, and HCV. PET/CT is the central imaging study. If doxorubicin is being considered, echocardiography or MUGA is needed. If bleomycin is being used, PFTs and DLCO are needed. Excisional biopsy is preferred because lymph-node architecture matters and Reed-Sternberg cells represent only 1–2% of tumor volume. Core biopsy may sometimes be acceptable, but FNA is insufficient. Bone-marrow biopsy is needed only for unexplained cytopenias or PET/CT suspicion of marrow involvement, because marrow involvement is uncommon, below 5%.

3. Staging and risk stratification

HL uses Ann Arbor / Lugano staging. Stage I is a single lymph-node region or a single extranodal lesion. Stage II involves two or more nodal groups on the same side of the diaphragm or limited contiguous extranodal involvement. Stage III involves nodal disease on both sides of the diaphragm, or supradiaphragmatic nodes with splenic involvement. Stage IV is noncontiguous extranodal involvement. Modifiers include A/B, E, and X. B denotes B symptoms; X denotes bulky disease, usually ≥10 cm or greater than one-third of the thoracic diameter.

Early-stage HL is divided into favorable and unfavorable disease, although GHSG, EORTC, and NCCN definitions differ slightly. Common unfavorable factors include elevated ESR, B symptoms, mediastinal mass, more nodal sites, extranodal lesions, and bulky disease. GHSG commonly uses mediastinal mass ratio >0.33, more than 2 nodal sites, any extranodal lesion, ESR >50 without B symptoms or >30 with B symptoms. EORTC adds age ≥50, mediastinal-thoracic ratio >0.35, and more than 3 nodal sites. NCCN commonly uses ESR ≥50 or any B symptoms, mediastinal mass ratio >0.33, more than 3 nodal sites, and bulky disease ≥10 cm.

Advanced-stage HL uses the International Prognostic Score. The mnemonic is SAW MEAL: stage IV, age ≥45, WBC ≥15,000/mm³, male sex, erythrocytes/hemoglobin <10.5 g/dL, albumin <4 g/dL, and lymphocytes <600/mm³ or <8% of WBC. Higher scores predict worse PFS and OS.

一、化學治療

ABVD＝阿黴素／多柔比星、博來黴素、長春花鹼、達卡巴嗪。每個療程為 28 天，於第 1 天與第 15 天給藥。急性毒性包括噁心、嘔吐、掉髮、骨髓抑制；晚期毒性包括心臟毒性與肺毒性。為降低博來黴素肺毒性，可在 PET 反應良好後降階為 AVD。BEACOPP＝博來黴素、依托泊苷、阿黴素、環磷醯胺、長春新鹼、丙卡巴肼、潑尼松，主要用於反應不佳或不利型疾病，但骨髓抑制與掉髮明顯較高。Stanford V 治療時間較短，且多柔比星／博來黴素累積劑量較低，但只在合併放射治療（RT）的情境被研究，因此放射治療（RT）不應省略。

晚期、復發或難治性何杰金淋巴瘤（HL）可用全身治療藥物，例如布倫妥昔單抗維多汀（BV；CD30 抗體藥物複合體），可用於晚期或復發疾病，但限制是成本與嚴重周邊神經病變；PD-1 抑制劑如納武利尤單抗可用於復發／難治性疾病。若復發發生於完全緩解（CR）後 >12 個月，可考慮重複第一線全身治療；若早期復發，需換治療藥物，例如 BV 或 PD-1 抑制劑。

二、放射治療：涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）／涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）／涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）

現代 HL 放射治療（RT）標準是涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）。歷史試驗多使用涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）；涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）需要化學治療前正子電腦斷層（PET/CT）且在放射治療體位下取得影像，涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）則不需要這個條件。現代國際淋巴瘤放射腫瘤學組織（ILROG）概念接受涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）／涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）作為標準。

三、為何有時省略放射治療（RT）？

重點是：單獨化學治療在無惡化存活（PFS）上通常不是同步放化療的不劣性替代方案，但整體存活（OS）可相近。很多醫師仍可能選擇單獨化學治療，原因包括整體存活（OS）相當、使用自體幹細胞移植進行救援治療（SCT）成功率高，以及年輕 HL 病人預後佳，對放射治療（RT）長期毒性特別敏感。然而這永遠是風險效益討論；化療本身也有心臟毒性，相關資料指出，每增加一個化學治療療程，其心臟負荷約相當於增加 4 Gy 心臟平均劑量的心臟負荷，因此若病灶大小與位置適合，有時使用較少化療加放射治療（RT）反而能降低整體晚期毒性。

Part II. Hodgkin lymphoma treatment framework

1. Chemotherapy

ABVD stands for Adriamycin/doxorubicin, bleomycin, vinblastine, and dacarbazine. Each cycle lasts 28 days, with treatment on days 1 and 15. Acute toxicities include nausea, vomiting, alopecia, and bone-marrow suppression. Late toxicities include cardiac and pulmonary toxicity. To reduce bleomycin-associated pulmonary toxicity, treatment can be de-intensified to AVD after a good PET response. BEACOPP includes bleomycin, etoposide, Adriamycin, cyclophosphamide, vincristine, procarbazine, and prednisone. It is mainly used for poor response or unfavorable disease, but it has substantially greater bone-marrow suppression and alopecia. Stanford V is a shorter regimen with lower cumulative doxorubicin and bleomycin doses, but it was studied with radiotherapy; therefore radiotherapy should not be omitted from the Stanford V approach.

Advanced, relapsed, or refractory HL can be treated with systemic agents such as brentuximab vedotin, a CD30 antibody-drug conjugate. It can be used in advanced or relapsed disease, but cost and severe neuropathy are important limitations. PD-1 inhibitors such as nivolumab can be used in relapsed or refractory disease. If relapse occurs more than 12 months after complete response, repeating first-line systemic therapy can be considered. Early relapse requires changing agents, such as brentuximab vedotin or a PD-1 inhibitor.

2. Radiotherapy: ISRT, INRT, and IFRT

The modern HL radiotherapy standard is involved-site radiotherapy, or ISRT. Historical trials often used involved-field radiotherapy, or IFRT. Involved-node radiotherapy, or INRT, requires pre-chemotherapy PET/CT obtained in treatment position; ISRT does not require that condition. Modern ILROG concepts accept ISRT/INRT as the contemporary standard.

3. Why is radiotherapy sometimes omitted?

The key point is that chemotherapy alone is usually not a non-inferior alternative to combined-modality therapy in terms of PFS, although OS can be similar. Some physicians still choose chemotherapy alone because OS is comparable, salvage with autologous stem-cell transplant can be successful, and young HL patients have excellent prognosis and are especially sensitive to long-term radiotherapy toxicity. This is always a risk-benefit discussion. Chemotherapy itself also has cardiac toxicity; the teaching file notes that each additional chemotherapy cycle can add a cardiac burden analogous to about 4 Gy of mean heart dose. Therefore, when disease size and location are suitable, fewer chemotherapy cycles plus radiotherapy may sometimes reduce overall late toxicity.

一、早期有利型經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL）

德國 HD10

德國 HD10 納入第 I–II 期有利型何杰金淋巴瘤（HL），採 2×2 隨機分派，比較 ABVD ×4 對比 ABVD ×2，以及涉及區域放射治療（IFRT） 30 Gy 對比 20 Gy。結果 ABVD ×2 與 20 Gy 涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）被認為不劣於，且毒性較低。ABVD ×4 的第 3–4 級急性毒性高於 ABVD ×2，30 Gy 放射治療（RT）毒性也高於 20 Gy。結論是：符合 GHSG 有利型標準的早期有利型何杰金淋巴瘤（HL），可以用 ABVD ×2 → 20 Gy 涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）。GHSG 有利型標準包括第 I–II 期、≤2 個淋巴結區域、無結外疾病、MMR <0.33、紅血球沉降速率（ESR） <50 或有 B 症狀時紅血球沉降速率（ESR） <30。

德國 HD16

HD16 問題是接受 ABVD ×2 後 PET 陰性是否可省略放射治療（RT）。結果顯示，即使接受 2 個療程 ABVD 後 PET 陰性，省略放射治療（RT）仍未在無惡化存活（PFS）上達到不劣性。無惡化存活（PFS）約為 93%（ABVD ×2 加 20 Gy），對比單獨 ABVD ×2 的 86%；整體存活（OS）相近，因為復發後救援治療可維持整體存活（OS）。結論是：ABVD 單獨治療後觀察不能視為無惡化存活（PFS）上等同 ABVD ×2 → 20 Gy 放射治療（RT）。

EORTC H10F

EORTC H10F 的標準組是 ABVD ×3 + 30 Gy 涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）；PET 導引組若 PET 陰性則 ABVD ×4 單獨治療，若 PET 陽性則強化型 BEACOPP ×2 + 30 Gy 涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）。PET 陰性病人中，ABVD 單獨治療無惡化存活（PFS）仍較差：5 年無惡化存活（PFS） 99% ABVD ×3 加涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）對比 87% 單獨 ABVD；整體存活（OS） 99–100% 均非常好。PET 陽性病人若改用 BEACOPPesc + 涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT），5 年無惡化存活（PFS）可由 77% 改善至 91%，但血液毒性較高。

UK RAPID

UK RAPID 納入第 IA–IIA 期非巨大腫塊經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL），ABVD ×3 後做 PET；PET 陰性者隨機分派至不再接受治療對比 30 Gy 涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）。ABVD 單獨治療仍未達無惡化存活（PFS）不劣性：3 年無惡化存活（PFS） 95% 接受放射治療（RT）對比 91% 未接受放射治療（RT）；整體存活（OS） 97–99% 均優。結論同樣是：省略放射治療（RT）可維持整體存活（OS），但無惡化存活（PFS）較差。

早期有利型高頻考點：預後極佳，無惡化存活（PFS）約 90% 以上、整體存活（OS）約 99%。放射治療（RT）省略通常不影響整體存活（OS），但會犧牲約 7–10% 絕對無惡化存活（PFS）獲益。治療核心是先 ABVD ×2，再用中期 PET 決策。Deauville 1–3 可考慮 ABVD ×2 → 20 Gy、ABVD ×3 → 30 Gy、ABVD ×3–4 單獨治療，或 Deauville 3 時 ABVD ×2 → AVD ×4；Deauville 4 則常需追加 ABVD 與 30 Gy 涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）。

二、早期不利型經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL）

德國 HD11

德國 HD11 納入第 IA／IB／IIA／IIB 期且有至少一個風險因子的早期不利型何杰金淋巴瘤（HL）。比較 BEACOPP ×4 對比 ABVD ×4，以及 30 Gy 對比 20 Gy 涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）。整體存活（OS）各組都很好，約 93–95%，但 ABVD ×4 + 20 Gy 未達不劣性；5 年無惡化存活（PFS）約 82%，低於其他約 87%。BEACOPP 可維持無惡化存活（PFS），但毒性更高。因此 ABVD ×4 → 30 Gy 涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）／涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）成為早期不利型疾病的標準基礎。

德國 HD14

HD14 比較 ABVD ×4 → 30 Gy 涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）與 “2+2” 方案，也就是 BEACOPP ×2 + ABVD ×2 → 30 Gy 涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）。「2+2」方案改善免於治療失敗／無惡化存活（PFS）：5 年無惡化存活（PFS） 95.4% 對比 89%，絕對改善約 6.2%，但 5 年整體存活（OS）約 97% 無差異，且急性毒性明顯增加，第 3–4 級毒性 80% 對比 51%、第 4 級毒性 56% 對比 6%。因此歐洲/GHSG 較接受強化治療，但美國通常較少因無惡化存活（PFS）改善而接受明顯毒性增加。

EORTC H10U

EORTC H10U 的標準組是 ABVD ×4 + 30 Gy 涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）；PET 導引組若 PET 陰性則 ABVD ×6 單獨治療，若 PET 陽性則 ABVD ×2 + BEACOPPesc ×2 + 30 Gy 涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）。PET 陰性病人中 ABVD 單獨治療仍未達不劣性；5 年無惡化存活（PFS） 92% 使用 ABVD ×4 + 涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）對比 90% 單獨 ABVD，整體存活（OS） 97–98%。PET 陽性病人中 BEACOPPesc + 涉及淋巴結放射治療（INRT）可改善 5 年無惡化存活（PFS）：77% 到 91%，但第 3–4 級血液毒性較高。

早期不利型高頻考點：美國實務中通常不偏好 BEACOPP，因毒性高。治療仍以 ABVD ×2 → PET 反應評估為核心。若 Deauville 1–3，可選 ABVD ×4 → 30 Gy 或 ABVD ×2 → AVD ×4；若 Deauville 4–5，可考慮強化型 BEACOPP ×2，再依後續 PET 決定 BEACOPP ×2 或 30 Gy 涉及區域放射治療（IFRT）/涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）。

Part III. Hodgkin lymphoma evidence

1. Early-stage favorable classic Hodgkin lymphoma

German HD10

German HD10 enrolled stage I–II favorable HL and used a 2×2 randomization to compare ABVD ×4 versus ABVD ×2 and IFRT 30 Gy versus 20 Gy. ABVD ×2 and 20 Gy IFRT were considered non-inferior and less toxic. Grade 3–4 acute toxicity was higher with ABVD ×4 than ABVD ×2, and 30 Gy radiotherapy was more toxic than 20 Gy. The conclusion is that early favorable HL meeting GHSG favorable criteria can be treated with ABVD ×2 followed by 20 Gy ISRT. GHSG favorable criteria include stage I–II, ≤2 nodal areas, no extranodal disease, mediastinal mass ratio <0.33, and ESR <50 without B symptoms or <30 with B symptoms.

German HD16

HD16 asked whether radiotherapy can be omitted in patients who are PET-negative after ABVD ×2. The result showed that even after PET-negative response to 2 cycles of ABVD, omitting radiotherapy did not meet non-inferiority for PFS. PFS was approximately 93% with ABVD ×2 + 20 Gy versus 86% with ABVD ×2 alone. OS was similar because salvage therapy after relapse preserved survival. The conclusion is that ABVD alone with observation should not be considered PFS-equivalent to ABVD ×2 followed by 20 Gy radiotherapy.

EORTC H10F

The standard arm of EORTC H10F was ABVD ×3 + 30 Gy INRT. In the PET-directed arm, PET-negative patients received ABVD ×4 alone, whereas PET-positive patients received escalated BEACOPP ×2 + 30 Gy INRT. Among PET-negative patients, ABVD alone had worse PFS: 5-year PFS was 99% with ABVD ×3 + INRT versus 87% with ABVD alone. OS was excellent at 99–100% in both groups. In PET-positive patients, escalation to BEACOPPesc + INRT improved 5-year PFS from 77% to 91%, but hematologic toxicity was higher.

UK RAPID

UK RAPID enrolled stage IA–IIA non-bulky classic HL. After ABVD ×3, PET-negative patients were randomized to no further therapy versus 30 Gy IFRT. ABVD alone still failed PFS non-inferiority: 3-year PFS was 95% with RT versus 91% without RT, while OS was excellent at 97–99%. The conclusion is the same: omitting radiotherapy can preserve OS but worsens PFS.

Early-stage favorable high-yield: Prognosis is excellent, with PFS above 90% and OS around 99%. Omitting RT usually does not affect OS, but sacrifices an absolute PFS benefit of about 7–10%. The core approach is ABVD ×2 followed by interim PET decision-making. Deauville 1–3 can be managed with ABVD ×2 → 20 Gy, ABVD ×3 → 30 Gy, ABVD ×3–4 alone, or for Deauville 3, ABVD ×2 → AVD ×4. Deauville 4 often requires additional ABVD and 30 Gy ISRT.

2. Early-stage unfavorable classic Hodgkin lymphoma

German HD11

German HD11 enrolled stage IA/IB/IIA/IIB early unfavorable HL with at least one risk factor. It compared BEACOPP ×4 versus ABVD ×4 and 30 Gy versus 20 Gy IFRT. OS was excellent in all groups, about 93–95%, but ABVD ×4 + 20 Gy did not meet non-inferiority. Five-year PFS was about 82%, lower than the approximately 87% in other groups. BEACOPP maintained PFS but was more toxic. Therefore, ABVD ×4 followed by 30 Gy ISRT/IFRT became the standard foundation for early-stage unfavorable disease.

German HD14

HD14 compared ABVD ×4 → 30 Gy IFRT with a “2+2” regimen, meaning BEACOPP ×2 + ABVD ×2 → 30 Gy IFRT. The 2+2 regimen improved freedom from treatment failure and PFS: 5-year PFS was 95.4% versus 89%, an absolute improvement of about 6.2%. Five-year OS was about 97% without difference, but acute toxicity increased substantially: grade 3–4 toxicity was 80% versus 51%, and grade 4 toxicity was 56% versus 6%. European and GHSG practice is more accepting of this intensification, whereas United States practice often does not accept the toxicity for a PFS-only gain.

EORTC H10U

The standard arm of EORTC H10U was ABVD ×4 + 30 Gy INRT. In the PET-directed arm, PET-negative patients received ABVD ×6 alone, whereas PET-positive patients received ABVD ×2 + BEACOPPesc ×2 + 30 Gy INRT. In PET-negative patients, ABVD alone still did not meet non-inferiority. Five-year PFS was 92% with ABVD ×4 + INRT versus 90% with ABVD alone, and OS was 97–98%. In PET-positive patients, BEACOPPesc + INRT improved 5-year PFS from 77% to 91%, but grade 3–4 hematologic toxicity was higher.

Early-stage unfavorable high-yield: In United States practice, BEACOPP is usually not preferred because of toxicity. Treatment still centers on ABVD ×2 followed by PET response assessment. If Deauville 1–3, options include ABVD ×4 → 30 Gy or ABVD ×2 → AVD ×4. If Deauville 4–5, escalated BEACOPP ×2 can be considered, followed by additional BEACOPP or 30 Gy IFRT/ISRT based on subsequent PET response.

晚期經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL）與 PET 反應調整治療

晚期經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL）

晚期何杰金淋巴瘤（HL）的核心同樣是 ABVD ×2 → PET 反應評估。若 PET 陰性，可去掉博來黴素轉 AVD ×4；若 PET 陽性，則升階治療至以 BEACOPP 為基礎的治療。UK RATHL 顯示，晚期經典型何杰金淋巴瘤（cHL）在基線正子電腦斷層（PET/CT）與 2 療程 ABVD 後，84% 病人 PET 陰性。PET 陰性病人隨機分派至 ABVD ×4 對比 AVD ×4，無惡化存活（PFS）約 86% 對比 84%，整體存活（OS） 97–98%；雖然嚴格統計上未達不劣性，但絕對差距小，且 AVD 可降低肺毒性，因此很多中心採用 ABVD ×2 → AVD ×4。PET 陽性病人轉 BEACOPP 後，約 74% 達完全緩解（CR），3 年無惡化存活（PFS） 68%、3 年整體存活（OS） 88%。

德國 HD15 納入晚期何杰金淋巴瘤（HL），比較 BEACOPP ×8、BEACOPP ×6、劑量密集型 BEACOPP-14 ×8；所有組別對 ≥2.5 cm 且殘存 FDG 攝取的病灶給 30 Gy PET 導引放射治療（RT）。BEACOPP ×6 與 BEACOPP-14 ×8 在無惡化存活（PFS）上不劣於，且 BEACOPP ×6 相比 ×8 有整體存活（OS）獲益。5 年無惡化存活（PFS）約 84%、89%、85%；5 年整體存活（OS）約 92%、95%、95%。但美國許多機構不常規使用 BEACOPP，因嚴重毒性。

晚期何杰金淋巴瘤（HL）的證據總結：可用 ABVD ×2 → AVD ×4、ABVD ×6 並只在初始巨大腫塊 >5 cm 且 PET2+ 或 ABVD ×6 後殘存 PET 陽性時給 30 Gy 放射治療（RT），或 BV-AVD ×6。整體原則是先 ABVD ×2 做 PET 反應，多數病人會產生反應並繼續原治療路徑；若反應不佳，則升階治療。

Advanced-stage classic Hodgkin lymphoma

The core approach in advanced-stage HL is also ABVD ×2 followed by PET response assessment. If PET-negative, bleomycin can be omitted and treatment de-escalated to AVD ×4. If PET-positive, treatment is escalated to BEACOPP-based therapy. UK RATHL showed that among advanced classic HL patients with baseline PET/CT and PET after 2 cycles of ABVD, 84% were PET-negative. PET-negative patients were randomized to ABVD ×4 versus AVD ×4. PFS was approximately 86% versus 84%, and OS was 97–98%. Although the result was technically not non-inferior statistically, the absolute difference was small and AVD reduced pulmonary toxicity. Therefore, many centers use ABVD ×2 followed by AVD ×4. PET-positive patients switched to BEACOPP; about 74% achieved complete response, with 3-year PFS of 68% and 3-year OS of 88%.

German HD15 enrolled advanced-stage HL and compared BEACOPP ×8, BEACOPP ×6, and dose-dense BEACOPP-14 ×8. All groups received 30 Gy PET-guided radiotherapy to residual FDG-avid lesions ≥2.5 cm. BEACOPP ×6 and BEACOPP-14 ×8 were non-inferior in PFS, and BEACOPP ×6 had an OS benefit compared with ×8. Five-year PFS was about 84%, 89%, and 85%; 5-year OS was about 92%, 95%, and 95%. However, many institutions in the United States do not routinely use BEACOPP because of severe toxicity.

The advanced-HL evidence summary is: options include ABVD ×2 → AVD ×4; ABVD ×6 with 30 Gy RT only for initially bulky disease >5 cm with PET2 positivity or residual PET positivity after ABVD ×6; or BV-AVD ×6. The overall principle is to start with ABVD ×2 and use PET response to guide treatment. Most patients respond and continue on the de-escalated pathway; poor responders require escalation.

二、NHL 臨床表現與檢查評估

NHL 常表現為無痛性漸增大淋巴結，可伴疲倦、全身不適、低度發燒。B 症狀包括 6 個月內體重下降 >10%、發燒 >38°C、大量盜汗，較常見於侵襲性組織型或廣泛疾病。皮膚侵犯可伴皮疹或搔癢；也可有肝腫大／脾腫大。惰性淋巴瘤常有骨髓侵犯，可能以血球低下表現。

檢查評估包括病史與理學檢查（H&P）、B 症狀、淋巴結、肝脾腫大。實驗室包括全血球計數與分類（CBC）、紅血球沉降速率（ESR）、CMP、乳酸脫氫酶（LDH）、尿酸、B 型肝炎病毒（HBV）/C 型肝炎病毒（HCV）、人類免疫缺乏病毒（HIV）、妊娠檢驗；胃黏膜相關淋巴組織淋巴瘤（胃 MALT）需尿素呼氣試驗。影像以正子電腦斷層（PET/CT）為主；原發性中樞神經系統淋巴瘤或鼻型結外 NK／T 細胞淋巴瘤需磁振造影（MRI）。病理以淋巴結切除切片最理想，選最 FDG 攝取最明顯區域；粗針切片是較差替代，細針抽吸（FNA）不能進行組織學分級。需做免疫組織化學（IHC）、流式細胞分析、螢光原位雜交（FISH）主要主要基因易位與基因體分析。骨髓切片依組織型與臨床情境決定；DLBCL 若 PET/CT 已顯示瀰漫性骨髓陽性或完全無骨骼攝取，可省略；濾泡性淋巴瘤若要確認第 I–II 期則需要，但若初始策略是觀察可延後。腰椎穿刺／神經軸影像用於睪丸侵犯、鼻竇、椎旁／腦膜旁、眼部侵犯、骨髓陽性、人類免疫缺乏病毒（HIV）、伯基特淋巴瘤等中樞神經系統（CNS）高風險情境。

三、淋巴結結構與 DLBCL 生物學

淋巴結結構包含被膜、被膜下竇、皮質、副皮質、髓竇、髓索、淋巴結門。皮質是 B 細胞區，包含濾泡與生發中心；副皮質是 T 細胞區。這對理解濾泡性淋巴瘤與 DLBCL 細胞起源很重要。

DLBCL 可依基因表現分析分為生發中心 B 細胞樣（GCB）與活化 B 細胞樣／非 GCB 型。非 GCB 型預後比 GCB 差。Hans 演算法以 CD10、BCL6、MUM1 的免疫組織化學（IHC）表現模式近似分類 GCB 型與非 GCB 型。

DLBCL 重要原癌基因包括 BCL2、BCL6、MYC。BCL2 在染色體 18，抑制細胞凋亡；BCL6 在染色體 3，可抑制 DNA 損傷反應基因，例如 ATR、TP53、CDKN1A；MYC 在染色體 8，促進增殖。若 MYC 重排合併 BCL2 或 BCL6 重排，稱雙重打擊淋巴瘤；若三者同時涉及則三重打擊淋巴瘤。若 c-MYC 與 BCL 蛋白共表現但沒有基因體重排，稱雙重表現淋巴瘤。兩者都是不良預後因子，雙重打擊淋巴瘤比雙重表現淋巴瘤更差。

Part IV. Non-Hodgkin lymphoma overview

1. Basic NHL classification

NHL is a highly heterogeneous disease arising from cells that differentiate into B-cell or T-cell lymphocytes. It can originate in bone marrow or peripheral nodal tissue. Broadly, it is divided into aggressive and indolent lymphoma. Aggressive NHL accounts for about 50–60%; without treatment, survival is often measured in months. Treatment usually involves multi-agent chemotherapy ± ISRT, and DLBCL commonly uses R-CHOP. Indolent NHL is more common after age 65 and can have a course over years to decades. Limited-stage indolent NHL can often be treated with definitive radiotherapy alone.

2. Presentation and workup

NHL often presents with painless enlarging lymph nodes and may be accompanied by fatigue, malaise, or low-grade fever. B symptoms include more than 10% weight loss over 6 months, fever above 38°C, and drenching night sweats; they are more common in aggressive histologies or extensive disease. Skin involvement can present with rash or pruritus. Hepatomegaly and splenomegaly can also occur. Indolent lymphoma commonly involves the bone marrow and may present with cytopenias.

Workup includes history and physical examination, B symptoms, lymph-node examination, and hepatosplenomegaly assessment. Laboratory tests include CBC with differential, ESR, CMP, LDH, uric acid, HBV/HCV, HIV, and pregnancy test. Gastric MALT requires a urea breath test. Imaging is centered on PET/CT. Primary CNS lymphoma and nasal-type NK/T-cell lymphoma require MRI. Excisional biopsy is preferred, selecting the most FDG-avid area. Core biopsy is a less ideal substitute, and FNA cannot provide histologic grading. IHC, flow cytometry, FISH for key translocations, and genomic profiling are needed. Bone-marrow biopsy depends on histology and clinical context. In DLBCL, it can be omitted if PET/CT already shows diffuse marrow positivity or no skeletal uptake. In follicular lymphoma, it is needed if confirming stage I–II would change management, but it can be deferred if the initial strategy is observation. Lumbar puncture or neuraxial imaging is used in CNS-risk scenarios such as testicular, paranasal sinus, paravertebral or parameningeal, eye involvement, positive marrow, HIV, and Burkitt lymphoma.

3. Lymph-node structure and DLBCL biology

Lymph-node structure includes the capsule, subcapsular sinus, cortex, paracortex, medullary sinus, medullary cord, and hilus. The cortex is the B-cell zone, containing follicles and germinal centers. The paracortex is the T-cell zone. This anatomy is important for understanding follicular lymphoma and DLBCL cell of origin.

DLBCL can be classified by gene-expression profiling into germinal-center B-cell-like, or GCB, and activated B-cell-like / non-GCB. Non-GCB has worse prognosis than GCB. The Hans algorithm uses CD10, BCL6, and MUM1 IHC patterns to approximate GCB versus non-GCB classification.

Important DLBCL proto-oncogenes include BCL2, BCL6, and MYC. BCL2 is on chromosome 18 and inhibits apoptosis. BCL6 is on chromosome 3 and can repress DNA-damage response genes such as ATR, TP53, and CDKN1A. MYC is on chromosome 8 and promotes proliferation. MYC rearrangement with BCL2 or BCL6 rearrangement is called double-hit lymphoma. Rearrangement of all three is triple-hit lymphoma. Coexpression of c-MYC and BCL protein without genomic rearrangement is double-expressor lymphoma. Both are poor prognostic factors, and double-hit disease is worse than double-expressor disease.

五、常見 B 細胞與 T 細胞淋巴瘤

B 細胞淋巴瘤通常 CD19+、CD20+。DLBCL 是最常見 NHL；原發性縱膈瀰漫性大型 B 細胞淋巴瘤（PMBCL）常見於年輕女性，表現為前縱膈／胸腺腫塊，治療不同於一般 DLBCL。套細胞淋巴瘤常有 t(11;14)、細胞週期蛋白 D1（cyclin D1），常見於較高齡與晚期，且具放射敏感性。伯基特淋巴瘤常有 t(8;14)、C-MYC，典型星空狀外觀，兒童常見，非洲地方流行型常 EBV+ 與顎骨侵犯，非地方流行型可見腹部／內臟器官。濾泡性淋巴瘤（FLL）第 1–3A 級常有 t(14;18)、BCL2，骨髓侵犯常見，10 年轉化風險約 28%。MALT／結外邊緣區淋巴瘤（MZL）常局限，t(11;18) 與胃黏膜相關淋巴組織淋巴瘤（胃 MALT）三合一抗生素治療失敗相關。CLL/SLL 可有第 12 號染色體三體（trisomy 12），SLL 形態類似 CLL，但循環中白血病細胞數量低。

T 細胞淋巴瘤包括周邊 T 細胞淋巴瘤，非特定型（PTCL-NOS）、間變性大型細胞淋巴瘤、血管免疫母細胞性 T 細胞淋巴瘤、鼻型結外 NK／T 細胞淋巴瘤、蕈樣肉芽腫。周邊 T 細胞淋巴瘤，非特定型（PTCL-NOS）是最常見周邊 T 細胞淋巴瘤，多見於老人。間變性大型細胞淋巴瘤可有 t(2;5) → ALK，ALK+ 預後較好。鼻型結外 NK／T 細胞淋巴瘤與 EBV 相關，在亞洲男性較常見。蕈樣肉芽腫是美國最常見皮膚淋巴瘤，Sézary 細胞為 CD4+。

六、NHL 分期與風險分層

NHL 同樣使用 Ann Arbor／Lugano 分期：第 I 期為單一淋巴結／淋巴結群或單一結外病灶；第 II 期為橫膈同側多個淋巴結或有限的連續性結外侵犯；第 III 期為橫膈兩側淋巴結或橫膈上淋巴結 + 脾臟侵犯；第 IV 期為非連續性淋巴系統外侵犯。2014 Lugano 更新提到 NHL 不再需要常規使用 X 巨大腫塊與 A/B 附加標記；第 III–IV 期也不需要 E。

侵襲性非何杰金淋巴瘤（NHL）用 IPI，口訣 LAKES：乳酸脫氫酶（LDH）高、年齡 >60、KPS/ECOG ≥2、結外部位 ≥2、第 III–IV 期。0–1 分為低風險，2 分為低中度風險，3 分為高中度風險，4–5 分為高風險。

濾泡性淋巴瘤可用 FLIPI 與 FLIPI2。FLIPI 包括血紅素 <12、年齡 >60、淋巴結區域 >4、第 III–IV 期、乳酸脫氫酶（LDH）升高。FLIPI2 包括血紅素 <12、年齡 >60、β2 微球蛋白升高、骨髓侵犯、最大淋巴結 >6 cm。

5. Common B-cell and T-cell lymphomas

B-cell lymphomas are usually CD19+ and CD20+. DLBCL is the most common NHL. Primary mediastinal DLBCL is common in young women and presents as an anterior mediastinal or thymic mass; its treatment differs from typical DLBCL. Mantle cell lymphoma often has t(11;14) and cyclin D1, commonly presents at older age and advanced stage, and is radiosensitive. Burkitt lymphoma often has t(8;14) and C-MYC, has a classic starry-sky appearance, is common in children, and in endemic African disease is often EBV-positive with jaw involvement; nonendemic disease can involve the abdomen or visceral organs. Follicular lymphoma grade 1–3A often has t(14;18) and BCL2, marrow involvement is common, and 10-year transformation risk is about 28%. MALT/extranodal marginal-zone lymphoma is often localized, and t(11;18) is associated with failure of triple-antibiotic therapy for gastric MALT. CLL/SLL may have trisomy 12; SLL resembles CLL morphologically but has a low circulating leukemia-cell count.

T-cell lymphomas include peripheral T-cell lymphoma NOS, anaplastic large cell lymphoma, angioimmunoblastic lymphoma, extranodal NK/T-cell lymphoma nasal type, and mycosis fungoides. Peripheral T-cell lymphoma NOS is the most common peripheral T-cell lymphoma and is more common in older patients. Anaplastic large cell lymphoma can have t(2;5) leading to ALK; ALK-positive disease has better prognosis. Extranodal NK/T-cell nasal type is EBV-associated and more common in Asian men. Mycosis fungoides is the most common cutaneous lymphoma in the United States, and Sézary cells are CD4+.

6. Staging and risk stratification

NHL also uses Ann Arbor/Lugano staging. Stage I is a single node/group or single extranodal lesion. Stage II is multiple nodes on the same side of the diaphragm or limited contiguous extranodal involvement. Stage III is nodal disease on both sides of the diaphragm or supradiaphragmatic nodes plus splenic involvement. Stage IV is noncontiguous extralymphatic involvement. The 2014 Lugano update notes that NHL no longer requires routine X bulky or A/B modifiers; stage III–IV also does not require E.

Aggressive NHL uses the IPI. The mnemonic is LAKES: high LDH, age >60, KPS/ECOG ≥2, extranodal sites ≥2, and stage III–IV. Scores 0–1 are low risk, 2 is low-intermediate risk, 3 is high-intermediate risk, and 4–5 is high risk.

Follicular lymphoma can use FLIPI and FLIPI2. FLIPI includes hemoglobin <12, age >60, more than 4 nodal sites, stage III–IV, and elevated LDH. FLIPI2 includes hemoglobin <12, age >60, elevated β2-microglobulin, bone-marrow involvement, and largest lymph node >6 cm.

二、濾泡性淋巴瘤第 1–3A 級

局限期第 I–II 期濾泡性淋巴瘤的首選治療是根治性涉及部位放射治療（ISRT），通常 24–30 Gy。晚期第 III–IV 期可觀察，也可以利妥昔單抗為基礎的化學治療或緩和性放射治療（RT）。4 Gy／2 次是緩和性涉及部位放射治療（ISRT）選項，俗稱 「boom-boom」，症狀緩解可相近，但持久局部控制較差。

Part V. NHL treatment framework

1. DLBCL

DLBCL usually undergoes restaging PET/CT after 3–4 cycles of chemotherapy. GCB-type DLBCL and follicular lymphoma grade 3B can be treated similarly: R-CHOP ×4–6 ± ISRT, or R-CHOP ×3 + RT. If complete response is achieved, RT commonly uses 30–36 Gy. If partial response remains, RT can use 40–50 Gy. For stage I–II disease with CR after R-CHOP ×3, options include one additional cycle of R-CHOP or ISRT. Bulky disease ≥7.5 cm or skeletal involvement can be considered for consolidation RT. Activated B-cell type may prompt intensified chemotherapy in some settings, such as R-CHOP ×6–8 ± ISRT, R-ACVBP + MTX/leucovorin, or R-CHOP + lenalidomide. Double-hit or triple-hit lymphoma can be treated with R-EPOCH or R-Hyper-CVAD, with consideration of CNS prophylaxis or autologous stem-cell transplant. Primary mediastinal DLBCL is a distinct entity and can be treated with R-EPOCH ×6 ± ISRT or R-CHOP ×6 + ISRT.

2. Follicular lymphoma grade 1–3A

For limited-stage I–II follicular lymphoma, the preferred treatment is definitive ISRT, usually 24–30 Gy. Advanced stage III–IV disease can be observed, treated with rituximab-based chemotherapy, or treated with palliative RT. Four Gy in 2 fractions is a palliative ISRT option, sometimes called “boom-boom.” Symptom relief can be similar, but durable local control is worse.

Part VI. NHL evidence and radiotherapy dose

1. Role of RT in aggressive NHL

Pre-rituximab evidence showed that RT + CHOP can improve outcomes or at least substitute for more intensive chemotherapy. SWOG 8736 compared CHOP ×8 with CHOP ×3 + IFRT 40–55 Gy. CHOP ×3 + RT improved 5-year PFS, 77% versus 64%, and 5-year OS, 82% versus 72%, while CHOP ×8 caused more cardiac and hematologic toxicity. In ECOG 1484, patients with CR after CHOP ×8 who received 30 Gy IFRT had improved DFS, 69% versus 53%, but no OS improvement. GELA LNH 93-1 showed that intensive ACVBP alone was superior to CHOP ×3 + 40 Gy IFRT, but ACVBP is not a standard United States regimen. The overall interpretation is that lower-intensity chemotherapy + RT can approach outcomes of intensive chemotherapy alone and may have better toxicity. However, historical studies used higher RT doses and IFRT; modern practice usually uses ISRT and 30 Gy for CR.

After rituximab, RT use declined. LYSA/GOELAMS 02-03 enrolled nonbulky, <7 cm, stage I–II DLBCL. Patients with CR after R-CHOP ×4 or ×6 were randomized to IFRT versus observation, and 5-year EFS and OS were not different. Therefore, for nonbulky stage I–II DLBCL with CR after R-CHOP, consolidative RT may not add benefit, and 4–6 cycles of R-CHOP can be used.

However, selected aggressive NHL patients may still benefit from RT. RICOVER-60 NoRTh showed that omission of RT in bulky disease ≥7.5 cm worsened EFS/PFS; EFS was 66% with the RT-containing RICOVER strategy versus 40% in NoRTh. A German pooled analysis showed that adding RT improved EFS in skeletal involvement, with HR around 0.3. UNFOLDER also showed that young good-prognosis DLBCL with bulky or extranodal disease had improved 3-year EFS with RT, 84% versus 68%, although PFS/OS were not clearly improved. OPTIMAL>60 suggested that bulky disease may be able to omit RT if PET-negative after chemotherapy. The future direction is PET response-adapted therapy.

Indolent NHL, palliative intent4 Gy/2 fractions or 24 Gy/12 fractions; 4 Gy has less durable local control

The Lowry trial supports dose reduction: for aggressive NHL, 30 Gy was non-inferior to 40–45 Gy; for indolent NHL, 24 Gy was non-inferior to 40–45 Gy. The FoRT trial compared 24 Gy/12 fractions with 4 Gy/2 fractions in indolent NHL. Four Gy in 2 fractions had inferior local control, but still provided good palliation and can be repeated. Five-year PFS was approximately 90% versus 70%, showing that definitive intent should favor 24 Gy, while “boom-boom” is better suited for palliation or selected low-toxicity strategies.

3. Definitive RT in indolent lymphoma

NCDB analysis showed that RT use in early follicular lymphoma declined from 37% to 24% between 1999 and 2012. However, in stage I–II grade 1–2 follicular NHL, 10-year OS was 68% with RT versus 54% without RT. Therefore, although RT use has declined in early follicular lymphoma, it should still be considered a standard option.

Ultra-low-dose RT also has selected evidence. A phase II study of orbital indolent NHL used 4 Gy/2 fractions; among 51 patients, 88% achieved CR by the third follow-up, with median time to CR around 3.4 months. A prospective gastric MALT trial used 4 Gy/2 fractions in 24 patients with residual lymphoma after H. pylori eradication; overall CR was 83%, 3-year local control was 96%, and no grade 3+ toxicity occurred. Local relapse could be salvaged with 20 Gy. These data support 4 Gy/2 fractions to reduce toxicity in selected indolent extranodal disease, but the definitive standard remains 24–30 Gy when durable long-term control is needed.

One-sentence memory aid: HL is “ABVD ×2 → PET response → decide RT, de-escalation, or escalation.” RT improves PFS, but omission can be considered after balancing OS, salvage success, and late toxicity.
