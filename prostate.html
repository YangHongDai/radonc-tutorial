攝護腺癌流行病學、臨床表現、解剖與 androgen axis

第一部分：攝護腺癌總論

一、流行病學與臨床重要性

攝護腺癌是美國男性最常見的惡性腫瘤，若排除 non-melanoma skin cancers，男性一生罹患風險約為 1 in 8。它也是男性第二常見癌症死因，第一名是肺癌。多數攝護腺癌診斷時仍屬 localized 或 regional disease，因此治療重點不只是「能不能治癒」，更是如何根據 risk group，在癌症控制、尿路功能、性功能、腸胃毒性、ADT 副作用、病人年齡與 competing mortality 之間取得平衡。

Source file 的圖表也強調，攝護腺癌大多在 localized stage 被診斷；localized 和 regional disease 的 5-year relative survival 幾乎非常高，但 distant disease 的 survival 明顯下降。因此 localized disease 的治療強度要依風險分層決定，而 metastatic disease 則要再依 castration-sensitive vs castration-resistant、low-volume vs high-volume、PSMA status、prior therapy 來決定。

二、Clinical presentation

攝護腺癌通常沒有症狀，最常因 PSA screening 發現。若有症狀，多數是 lower urinary tract symptoms，包括 frequency、urgency、nocturia、hesitancy，也可能出現 hematuria 或 hematospermia。PSA screening 的核心是 shared decision-making，因為 randomized trials 和 meta-analyses 沒有顯示明確 overall mortality reduction，但 ERSPC 顯示 screening 可能降低 distant-stage prostate cancer incidence；同時必須考慮 over-diagnosis、over-treatment、biopsy complications、治療後尿路/性功能/腸胃毒性，以及病人焦慮。

三、攝護腺解剖

攝護腺約 1/3 是 fibromuscular stroma，主要包含 anterior zone；約 2/3 是 glandular elements，包含 central zone、transition zone、peripheral zone。Transition zone 是 BPH 常見位置；peripheral zone 是多數攝護腺癌發生位置。這點對 DRE、MRI interpretation、biopsy targeting 很重要，因為 posterior/peripheral lesions 比較容易被 DRE 或 systematic posterior biopsy 偵測。

四、攝護腺生理與 androgen axis

攝護腺參與 seminal fluid production，約 30% seminal fluid 來自 prostate，約 70% 來自 seminal vesicles、testes、bulbourethral glands。攝護腺生長與攝護腺癌存活高度依賴 androgen regulation：LH 促進 testes 製造 testosterone；ACTH 促進 adrenals 產生 DHEA，再轉成 testosterone；testosterone 經 5-α reductase 轉成 DHT，DHT 與 androgen receptor 結合後促進 prostate survival、growth、PSA secretion。這就是 ADT、AR inhibitors、CYP17 inhibitors 的生物學基礎。

Part I. Prostate Cancer Overview

1. Epidemiology and clinical importance

Prostate cancer is the most common malignancy in men in the United States if non-melanoma skin cancers are excluded. The lifetime risk is approximately 1 in 8, and it is the second leading cause of cancer death in men after lung cancer. Most cases are diagnosed as localized or regional disease, so management is not only about cure, but also about balancing cancer control against urinary function, sexual function, bowel toxicity, ADT adverse effects, patient age, and competing mortality.

Localized and regional prostate cancer have very high 5-year relative survival, whereas distant disease has substantially worse survival. Localized treatment intensity is therefore risk-adapted. Metastatic disease requires additional classification by castration-sensitive versus castration-resistant status, low-volume versus high-volume burden, PSMA status, and prior therapy.

2. Clinical presentation

Prostate cancer is usually asymptomatic and is most often detected through PSA screening. When symptoms occur, they are usually lower urinary tract symptoms such as frequency, urgency, nocturia, hesitancy, hematuria, or hematospermia. PSA screening should be based on shared decision-making because screening can reduce distant-stage prostate cancer but also causes over-diagnosis, over-treatment, biopsy complications, treatment-related urinary, sexual, and bowel toxicity, and patient anxiety.

3. Prostate anatomy

About one third of the prostate is fibromuscular stroma, mainly the anterior zone, and about two thirds are glandular elements, including the central, transition, and peripheral zones. The transition zone is the common site of BPH. The peripheral zone is the most common origin of prostate cancer, which is why posterior and peripheral lesions are more likely to be detected by DRE or systematic posterior biopsy.

4. Androgen axis

The prostate contributes roughly 30% of seminal fluid, while the seminal vesicles, testes, and bulbourethral glands contribute the rest. Prostate growth and prostate cancer survival are strongly androgen-driven. LH stimulates testicular testosterone production, ACTH stimulates adrenal DHEA production, testosterone is converted to DHT by 5-alpha reductase, and DHT activates the androgen receptor to promote prostate survival, growth, and PSA secretion. This is the biologic basis for ADT, androgen receptor inhibitors, and CYP17 inhibitors.

第三部分：Workup 細節

一、Initial clinical workup

Workup 包括 H&P、labs、imaging、pathology。H&P 要特別評估 urinary、sexual、bowel symptoms，並記錄 IPSS 和 SHIM scores。DRE 用於評估 palpable nodule、induration、T-stage suspicion。Labs 包括 PSA 和 serum testosterone；testosterone 對日後 ADT response、是否達到 castration level，以及 castration-resistant disease definition 很重要。

二、IPSS 與 SHIM

IPSS（International Prostate Symptom Score）用於量化 urinary symptoms，包括 incomplete emptying、frequency、intermittency、urgency、weak stream、straining、nocturia。

Part III. Workup Details

1. Initial clinical workup

The workup includes history and physical examination, laboratory testing, imaging, and pathology. History should specifically assess urinary, sexual, and bowel symptoms and document IPSS and SHIM scores. DRE evaluates palpable nodules, induration, and suspected T stage. Laboratory testing includes PSA and serum testosterone. Testosterone is important for later assessment of ADT response, whether castration level is achieved, and whether disease is castration-resistant.

2. IPSS and SHIM

IPSS quantifies urinary symptoms including incomplete emptying, frequency, intermittency, urgency, weak stream, straining, and nocturia.

IPSS severity
IPSSSeverity
1–7Mild
8–19Moderate
20–35Severe

IPSS is important for radiation decision-making. Patients with high baseline IPSS, especially obstructive symptoms, have higher urinary flare or retention risk after brachytherapy or SBRT and may need BPH management or careful treatment selection.

SHIM evaluates erectile function and supports pretreatment counseling. Surgery, radiation, and ADT can all affect erectile function, but with different mechanisms and timing: surgery may cause earlier dysfunction, RT-related ED tends to be gradual, and ADT reduces libido and erectile function.

3. PSA metrics

PSA above roughly 2.5–4 ng/mL increases prostate cancer risk, but normal PSA depends on age and population. False positives can occur after manipulation or biopsy, BPH, prostatitis, urinary retention, trauma, or ejaculation, so an isolated PSA value can be difficult to interpret.

Pathologic diagnosis is made by prostate biopsy. Biopsy can be transrectal ultrasound-guided via transrectal or transperineal route. The transperineal approach is preferred when feasible because of lower sepsis risk. MRI-fusion biopsy is preferred when MRI shows a PI-RADS suspicious lesion.

一、Local therapy options

Localized prostate cancer treatment 是 risk-adapted。Local therapy 包括 radical prostatectomy、EBRT alone、brachytherapy alone、EBRT + brachytherapy boost、moderate hypofractionation、SBRT、以及 focal intraprostatic boost。若 pelvic nodal involvement risk 高，手術可考慮 PLND；放療可考慮 pelvic EBRT。Systemic therapy 以 ADT 為核心：unfavorable intermediate-risk 用 ST-ADT 4–6 months；high-risk 或更高風險用 LT-ADT 18–36 months。

第六部分：攝護腺癌 radiobiology 與 fractionation

四、為什麼 prostate cancer 適合 hypofractionation？

α/β ratio 是 linear component 與 quadratic component cell killing 相等時的 dose。High α/β tissues 對 fraction size 較不敏感，通常是 rapidly proliferating tumors；low α/β tissues 對 dose per fraction 較敏感，通常是 late-responding tissues。攝護腺癌 estimated α/β ratio 約 1.5–3，比許多腫瘤低，代表較大的 fraction size 可能對腫瘤更有利。Source file 因此提出三個策略：conventional dose escalation、moderate hypofractionation、ultra-hypofractionation/SBRT。

Part V. Localized Prostate Cancer Treatment Options

1. Local therapy options

Localized prostate cancer treatment is risk-adapted. Local therapy options include radical prostatectomy, EBRT alone, brachytherapy alone, EBRT plus brachytherapy boost, moderate hypofractionation, SBRT, and focal intraprostatic boost. If pelvic nodal involvement risk is high, surgery may include pelvic lymph node dissection and RT may include pelvic nodal irradiation. Systemic therapy is centered on ADT: unfavorable intermediate-risk disease uses 4–6 months short-term ADT, while high-risk or higher-risk disease uses 18–36 months long-term ADT.

2. Surgery

Radical prostatectomy removes the prostate and seminal vesicles and often includes risk-adapted pelvic lymph node dissection. Advantages include pathologic staging, margin status, nodal status, and a clear PSA endpoint: PSA should become undetectable. Disadvantages include urinary incontinence, erectile dysfunction, perioperative risk, and possible need for salvage RT with or without ADT if adverse pathology or PSA recurrence occurs.

Part VI. Prostate Cancer Radiobiology and Fractionation

4. Why prostate cancer is suitable for hypofractionation

The alpha/beta ratio is the dose at which the linear and quadratic components of cell killing are equal. High alpha/beta tissues are less sensitive to fraction size and are often rapidly proliferating tumors. Low alpha/beta tissues are more sensitive to dose per fraction and are often late-responding tissues. Prostate cancer has an estimated alpha/beta ratio of about 1.5–3, lower than many tumors, so larger fraction sizes may be advantageous. This supports conventional dose escalation, moderate hypofractionation, and ultra-hypofractionation/SBRT.

二、FLAME trial：focal intraprostatic boost

FLAME 是 phase III superiority randomized trial，納入 intermediate- 和 high-risk prostate cancer。Standard arm 給 prostate 77 Gy/35 Fx；boost arm 給 prostate 77 Gy/35 Fx，並對 macroscopic tumor 給 up to 95 Gy simultaneous integrated boost。病人中約 85% 是 high-risk，約 15% 是 intermediate-risk；兩組約 65% 接受 ADT。

Planning concept 是 isotoxic boost：OAR constraints 優先於 95 Gy boost coverage；兩組都沒有 pelvic nodal RT；使用 IMRT 或 VMAT；放置 gold fiducials；experimental arm 的 boost volume 沒有 CTV/PTV margin；若 PTV overlap rectum，該 overlap 區域 prescribed 70 Gy。Key OAR constraints 包括 rectum V77 Gy <1 cc、bladder V80 Gy <1 cc，後續 protocols 加入 urethra V80 Gy <0.1 cc。

FLAME 結果：intraprostatic boost 改善 5-year bDFS，standard arm 85% vs boost arm 92%，且沒有顯著增加 late GU 或 GI toxicity。Late G2+ GU toxicity 約 23% vs 28%；late G2+ GI toxicity 約 12% vs 13%。OS、DMFS、PCSS 無差異。Post-hoc pattern-of-failure analysis 顯示，GTV D98% 與 local failure、regional/distant metastasis-free survival 有 dose-response relationship；focal boost dose ≥85 Gy 時，local failure 接近 zero，regional/distant metastasis <10%。

臨床解讀：FLAME 的重點不是盲目把 visible lesion 打到 95 Gy，而是 MRI-visible dominant intraprostatic lesion 的 isotoxic dose escalation。若有高品質 MRI、image guidance、良好 OAR sparing，FLAME-style boost 是很有力的 dose intensification strategy。

三、ASCENDE-RT：EBRT boost vs LDR brachytherapy boost

ASCENDE-RT 是 phase III randomized trial，納入 intermediate- 和 high-risk prostate cancer；約 69% high-risk，31% intermediate-risk，大多數 intermediate-risk 是 unfavorable intermediate-risk。所有病人接受 12 months ADT，以及 46 Gy/23 Fx to prostate、SV、pelvis。之後 randomization：dose-escalated EBRT boost arm 給 EBRT boost 至 total 78 Gy；LDR boost arm 給 I-125 LDR prostate brachytherapy boost 115 Gy minimum peripheral dose。

結果顯示 LDR prostate brachytherapy boost 明顯改善 long-term biochemical control。15-year bPFS 約 80% with LDR boost vs 53% with dose-escalated EBRT。但 OS、CSS、DMFS 沒有顯著差異。代價是 GU toxicity 增加：grade 3+ GU toxicity 約 18% vs 5%，並增加 urethral stricture、pad use、late catheterization。

臨床解讀：Brachytherapy boost 是 UIR/HR disease 最強的 biochemical intensification 方法之一，但 patient selection 非常重要。Baseline urinary function、IPSS、prostate size、median lobe、prior TURP、anatomy、病人對 GU toxicity 的接受度，都會影響是否適合。

1. Conventional dose escalation

2. FLAME: focal intraprostatic boost

FLAME was a phase III randomized superiority trial in intermediate- and high-risk prostate cancer. The standard arm delivered 77 Gy in 35 fractions to the prostate. The boost arm delivered 77 Gy to the prostate with an SIB up to 95 Gy to the macroscopic tumor. About 85% of patients were high-risk and about 65% received ADT.

The planning concept was isotoxic boost: OAR constraints were prioritized over complete 95 Gy boost coverage. There was no pelvic nodal RT. IMRT or VMAT and gold fiducials were used. The boost volume had no CTV/PTV margin, and areas overlapping rectum received a lower prescription. Key constraints included rectum V77 Gy <1 cc and bladder V80 Gy <1 cc, with later protocols adding urethra V80 Gy <0.1 cc.

FLAME improved 5-year biochemical disease-free survival from about 85% to 92% without significantly increasing late GU or GI toxicity. There was no OS, DMFS, or PCSS difference. Post-hoc pattern-of-failure analysis showed a dose-response relationship between GTV D98% and local, regional, and distant control, with near-zero local failure when focal boost dose was at least 85 Gy.

Clinical interpretation: FLAME is not about blindly escalating all visible disease to 95 Gy. It is about MRI-visible dominant intraprostatic lesion isotoxic dose escalation with high-quality MRI, image guidance, and OAR sparing.

3. ASCENDE-RT: EBRT boost versus LDR brachytherapy boost

ASCENDE-RT randomized intermediate- and high-risk prostate cancer patients, most of whom had high-risk or unfavorable intermediate-risk disease. All patients received 12 months of ADT and 46 Gy in 23 fractions to prostate, seminal vesicles, and pelvis. Patients were then randomized to EBRT boost to 78 Gy total versus I-125 LDR prostate brachytherapy boost to 115 Gy minimum peripheral dose.

LDR brachytherapy boost substantially improved long-term biochemical control. Fifteen-year bPFS was about 80% with LDR boost versus 53% with dose-escalated EBRT. There was no significant OS, CSS, or DMFS difference. The tradeoff was increased grade 3+ GU toxicity, including urethral stricture, pad use, and late catheterization.

Clinical interpretation: brachytherapy boost is one of the strongest biochemical intensification strategies for UIR/HR disease, but selection is critical. Baseline urinary function, IPSS, prostate size, median lobe, prior TURP, anatomy, and patient tolerance for GU toxicity all matter.

第八部分：Moderate hypofractionation

一、RTOG 0415

RTOG 0415 納入 low-risk prostate cancer，randomized conventional RT 73.8 Gy/41 Fx vs hypofractionated RT 70 Gy/28 Fx。結果 hypofractionated RT 對 DFS 和 biochemical failure 達到 non-inferiority；5-year DFS 約 85% vs 86%，biochemical failure 約 6% vs 8%。早期報告中 hypofractionation 的 late grade 2–3 GI/GU events 較高，但 patient-reported QoL 沒有差異，long-term grade 3+ toxicity 也沒有顯著差異。

1. RTOG 0415

3. CHHiP

Together with PROFIT, CHHiP supports 60 Gy in 20 fractions as a standard moderate hypofractionation regimen. In practice, 60 Gy in 20 fractions is common because it is shorter, radiobiologically rational, and supported by randomized evidence.

4. HYPO-RT-PC

HYPO-RT-PC randomized intermediate- to high-risk patients to 78 Gy in 39 fractions versus 42.7 Gy in 7 fractions over 1–2 weeks. ADT was not allowed. Unfavorable intermediate-risk disease 的 ST-ADT 證據

第十二部分：ST-ADT for unfavorable intermediate-risk disease

一、RTOG 9408

RTOG 9408 randomized 2028 位 cT1b–T2b N0、PSA ≤20 prostate cancer patients 至 66.6 Gy RT alone vs 66.6 Gy RT + 4 months ADT。ADT 為 flutamide + goserelin 或 leuprolide。病人約 36% low-risk、54% intermediate-risk、10% high-risk。

ST-ADT 改善 10-year OS、CSS、biochemical failure、distant metastasis 和 pathologic tumor clearance。10-year OS：RT alone 57% vs RT + ADT 62%。但 18-year OS benefit 消失，兩組 OS 均約 23%；不過 biochemical failure 和 distant metastasis benefit 仍存在。這可能因為 18 年時很多死亡來自 competing causes。

Post-hoc subgroup analysis 顯示，ADT benefit 主要集中在 intermediate-risk subgroup；low-risk disease-specific mortality 很低，因此可省略 ADT；high-risk 則可能需要更長 ADT。雖然 RTOG 9408 使用的 RT dose 比現代低，但它建立了 intermediate-risk prostate cancer 使用 short-term ADT 的重要基礎。

二、Favorable vs unfavorable intermediate-risk 的重要性

RTOG 9408 secondary analysis 將 intermediate-risk 分成 FIR 與 UIR。UIR 整體 DM、PCSM、ACM 風險較高。ADT 在 UIR 中改善 DM、PCSM、ACM，但在 FIR 中沒有明顯 benefit。這就是現代臨床上 FIR 通常不加 ADT，UIR 加 4–6 months ADT 的理由。

三、Harvard / D’Amico trial

D’Amico trial 比較 70 Gy RT alone vs 70 Gy RT + 6 months ADT，納入 cT1b–T2b、PSA ≥10 或 GS ≥7 的病人，其中約 75% 是 intermediate-risk。結果 6 months ADT 改善 OS：5-year OS 78% vs 88%，也改善 freedom from salvage ADT。Toxicity 方面 ADT group 有較多 grade 1–2 gynecomastia 和 grade 3 impotence。

四、RTOG 0815

RTOG 0815 測試 modern dose-escalated RT 79.2 Gy ± 6 months ADT in intermediate-risk prostate cancer。ST-ADT 改善 PSA failure、DM、PCSM、salvage therapy use，但未改善 OS：5-year OS 約 90% vs 91%。Acute grade 3+ toxicity 在 ADT arm 較高，2% vs 12%，但 late grade 3+ toxicity 類似。

臨床解讀：即使在 modern dose-escalated RT 下，ST-ADT 仍改善 disease-control endpoints，但 OS benefit 可能小，因此要根據 FIR vs UIR、Decipher、病人年齡、心血管風險與 preference 個案化。

五、NRG-GU010

NRG-GU010 是 ongoing trial，使用 Decipher risk score personalize UIR disease。低 Decipher score 病人測試 de-escalation：RT + 6 months ADT vs RT alone；較高 Decipher score 病人測試 escalation：RT + 6 months ADT vs RT + 6 months ADT + 6 months darolutamide。

Part XII. Short-Term ADT for Unfavorable Intermediate-Risk Disease

1. RTOG 9408

RTOG 9408 randomized 2028 patients with cT1b–T2b N0, PSA ≤20 prostate cancer to 66.6 Gy RT alone versus 66.6 Gy RT plus 4 months of ADT. ADT was flutamide plus goserelin or leuprolide. About 36% had low-risk, 54% intermediate-risk, and 10% high-risk disease.

Short-term ADT improved 10-year OS, CSS, biochemical failure, distant metastasis, and pathologic tumor clearance. Ten-year OS improved from 57% to 62%. At 18 years, the OS benefit disappeared because many deaths were due to competing causes, but biochemical failure and distant metastasis benefit persisted.

Post-hoc analysis showed that benefit was concentrated in intermediate-risk disease. Low-risk disease-specific mortality was very low, so ADT can be omitted, while high-risk disease likely requires longer ADT. Although RTOG 9408 used a lower RT dose than modern practice, it established the foundation for short-term ADT in intermediate-risk prostate cancer.

2. Favorable versus unfavorable intermediate risk

A secondary analysis of RTOG 9408 separated favorable intermediate-risk from unfavorable intermediate-risk disease. UIR had higher distant metastasis, prostate cancer-specific mortality, and all-cause mortality. ADT improved these outcomes in UIR but not FIR. This explains the modern practice of omitting ADT in FIR and adding 4–6 months of ADT in UIR.

3. Harvard / D’Amico trial

The D’Amico trial compared 70 Gy RT alone versus 70 Gy RT plus 6 months of ADT in patients with cT1b–T2b disease, PSA ≥10, or Gleason score ≥7. About 75% were intermediate-risk. Six months of ADT improved 5-year OS from 78% to 88% and improved freedom from salvage ADT. ADT caused more gynecomastia and impotence.

4. RTOG 0815

RTOG 0815 tested modern dose-escalated RT to 79.2 Gy with or without 6 months of ADT in intermediate-risk prostate cancer. Short-term ADT improved PSA failure, distant metastasis, prostate cancer-specific mortality, and salvage therapy use, but not OS. Acute grade 3+ toxicity was higher with ADT, while late grade 3+ toxicity was similar.

Clinical interpretation: even with modern dose-escalated RT, short-term ADT improves disease-control endpoints, but the OS benefit may be small. Treatment should be individualized by FIR versus UIR, Decipher, age, cardiovascular risk, and preference.

5. NRG-GU010

NRG-GU010 is an ongoing trial using Decipher to personalize UIR disease. Low Decipher score patients are tested for de-escalation with RT plus 6 months ADT versus RT alone, while higher Decipher score patients are tested for escalation with RT plus ADT versus RT plus ADT plus darolutamide.

四、PCS IV concept

PCS IV 支持許多 high-risk patients 可用 18 months 而不是 36 months ADT。36 months 沒有優於 18 months OS，且 QoL 與 testosterone recovery 較差。因此現代實務常把 LT-ADT 設為 18–24 months，雖然舊 trials 常用 24–36 months。

六、STAMPEDE abiraterone

STAMPEDE meta-analysis 測試 abiraterone ± enzalutamide in nonmetastatic high-risk patients。納入條件包括 node-positive，或至少 2 個 high-risk factors：T3–T4、GS ≥8、PSA ≥40，或 high-risk relapsing features。Treatment 比較 74 Gy RT + 3 years ADT alone vs 同樣治療加 2 years abiraterone/prednisolone，另一 trial 加入 enzalutamide。

Abiraterone 改善 DMFS、OS、BFS、PFS；加 enzalutamide 沒有額外 benefit。6-year DMFS 約 82% with abiraterone vs 69% with ADT alone。Toxicities 包括 hypertension 和 elevated LFTs。

Part XIII. Long-Term ADT for High-Risk Disease

1. RTOG 9202

RTOG 9202 randomized mostly high-risk patients to RT plus 4 months ADT versus RT plus 28 months ADT. Long-term ADT improved multiple long-term disease-control endpoints. The greatest benefit was in Gleason 8–10 disease, including an OS benefit. Late grade 3+ GI toxicity was slightly higher with long-term ADT. The source summary is: LT-ADT is superior to ST-ADT, with greatest benefit in Gleason 8–10.

2. EORTC 22961

EORTC 22961 compared 70 Gy RT plus 6 months ADT versus 70 Gy RT plus 36 months ADT in mostly high-risk patients. Short-term ADT failed to show non-inferiority for prostate cancer mortality. Long-term ADT caused more hot flashes and sexual side effects, but cardiac-related deaths were not significantly increased.

3. DART01/05 GICOR

DART01/05 GICOR tested dose-escalated RT 76–82 Gy plus 4 months versus 28 months ADT. Five-year results showed improved bDFS with long-term ADT and OS/DM benefit in the high-risk subgroup. At 10 years, the overall bDFS improvement was less durable, but high-risk patients still had improved 10-year bDFS. Dose-escalated RT does not fully replace long-term ADT in high-risk disease.

4. PCS IV concept

PCS IV supports 18 months rather than 36 months of ADT for many high-risk patients. Thirty-six months did not improve OS over 18 months and had worse QoL and testosterone recovery. Modern practice often uses 18–24 months of long-term ADT, although older trials used 24–36 months.

5. NRG-GU009 / PREDICT-RT

NRG-GU009 uses Decipher to personalize high-risk treatment. Decipher ≤0.85 patients are randomized between RT plus 24 months ADT and de-escalated RT plus 12 months ADT. Decipher >0.85 or CT/MRI node-positive patients are randomized between RT plus 24 months ADT and RT plus 24 months ADT plus apalutamide.

Part XIV. Very High-Risk / Node-Positive Localized Disease

6. STAMPEDE abiraterone

STAMPEDE tested abiraterone with or without enzalutamide in nonmetastatic high-risk patients. Eligible patients were node-positive or had at least two high-risk factors such as T3–T4 disease, Gleason score ≥8, PSA ≥40, or high-risk relapsing features. Abiraterone improved DMFS, OS, BFS, and PFS. Adding enzalutamide did not add benefit. Six-year DMFS was about 82% with abiraterone versus 69% with ADT alone. Toxicities included hypertension and elevated LFTs.

Clinical interpretation: for node-positive or very high-risk nonmetastatic patients, definitive pelvic RT plus long-term ADT plus abiraterone/prednisone is an important intensification strategy.

Salvage RT

Salvage RT 加 ADT、pelvic nodes、Decipher 與 pN1 disease

第十六部分：Salvage RT 加 ADT 與 pelvic nodes

一、RTOG 9601

RTOG 9601 randomized post-RP patients with PSA 0.2–4 ng/mL to salvage RT alone vs salvage RT + 2 years bicalutamide 150 mg daily。RT 是 prostate bed 64.8 Gy/36 Fx；因病人都有 negative LND，所以沒有 pelvic nodes。12-year results 顯示加 ADT 改善 OS 76% vs 71%、PCSM 6% vs 13%、DM 15% vs 23%、second biochemical failure 44% vs 68%。Bicalutamide toxicity 主要是 gynecomastia，70% vs 11%。

重要 nuance：secondary analysis 顯示 ADT benefit 主要在 PSA >0.6 ng/mL 的 salvage setting；若 PSA 很低時 salvage，long-course antiandrogen benefit 可能小，且可能增加 non-cancer harm。因此 salvage ADT 要根據 PSA、PSA doubling time、GG、margin、pT stage、Decipher 等 risk-adapted。

二、GETUG-AFU 16

GETUG-AFU 16 randomized PSA 0.2–2.0 ng/mL post-RP rising PSA patients to prostate fossa RT 66 Gy/33 Fx ± 6 months goserelin。10-year bPFS 和 DMFS 都因 ADT 改善：bPFS 64% vs 49%；DMFS 75% vs 69%；OS 無改變。Toxicity 和 QoL 沒有顯著差異。

臨床解讀：Short-course ADT in salvage RT 可以改善 biochemical and metastatic control，特別是 higher-risk salvage patients。

三、SPPORT / RTOG 0534

SPPORT / RTOG 0534 支持在 selected salvage setting 加入 pelvic nodal RT 與 ST-ADT。整體概念是：prostate bed RT alone < prostate bed RT + ST-ADT < prostate bed RT + pelvic nodal RT + ST-ADT，尤其 PSA 較高、nodal risk 較高者 benefit 更明顯。

RADICALS-HD 0 vs 6 months ADT 顯示，在多數 intermediate-risk postoperative RT patients 中，ST-ADT 沒有顯著改善 10-year DMFS：79% no ADT vs 80% ST-ADT。不過 secondary endpoints 如 clinical PFS 和 time to salvage ADT 有改善。

Decipher 是 22-gene genomic classifier，score 0–1。RTOG 9601 validation 顯示 Decipher 與 bPFS、DMFS、OS 相關，可協助個人化 salvage RT 是否加 ADT、以及治療強度。Source file 分類：low <0.45、intermediate 0.45–0.6、high >0.6。

六、pN1 after prostatectomy

對 pN1 disease，retrospective data 支持 selected patients 可從 adjuvant RT 受益。Abdollah et al. 顯示 cancer-specific mortality benefit 主要在兩群：1–2 nodes 且 GS 7–10 加 pT3b–4 或 positive margin；以及 3–4 positive nodes。Tilki et al. 顯示 adjuvant RT 相較 salvage RT 可降低 all-cause mortality，benefit 隨 positive nodes 數目增加而更明顯，尤其 ≥4 nodes。

Post-RP high-yield：多數 adverse pathology + undetectable PSA → observation + early salvage。PSA detectable/rising → early salvage RT。Higher-risk salvage → 加 ADT，並考慮 pelvic nodes。pN1，尤其 ≥4 nodes 或 adverse features → 更支持 adjuvant RT + systemic therapy。

Part XVI. Salvage RT with ADT and Pelvic Nodes

1. RTOG 9601

RTOG 9601 randomized post-prostatectomy patients with PSA 0.2–4 ng/mL to salvage RT alone versus salvage RT plus 2 years of bicalutamide 150 mg daily. RT was prostate bed 64.8 Gy in 36 fractions. Because patients had negative lymph node dissection, pelvic nodes were not treated. At 12 years, ADT improved OS, prostate cancer-specific mortality, distant metastasis, and second biochemical failure. The main toxicity was gynecomastia.

Important nuance: secondary analysis showed that benefit was mainly in patients with salvage PSA >0.6 ng/mL. When salvage RT is given at very low PSA, long-course antiandrogen benefit may be smaller and non-cancer harm may increase. Salvage ADT should therefore be risk-adapted by PSA, PSA doubling time, Grade Group, margin, pT stage, Decipher, and other features.

2. GETUG-AFU 16

GETUG-AFU 16 randomized post-RP patients with rising PSA 0.2–2.0 ng/mL to prostate fossa RT 66 Gy in 33 fractions with or without 6 months of goserelin. Ten-year bPFS and DMFS improved with ADT, while OS did not change. Toxicity and QoL were similar.

Clinical interpretation: short-course ADT with salvage RT improves biochemical and metastatic control, especially in higher-risk salvage patients.

3. SPPORT / RTOG 0534

SPPORT supports adding pelvic nodal RT and short-term ADT in selected salvage settings. The hierarchy is: prostate bed RT alone < prostate bed RT + ST-ADT < prostate bed RT + pelvic nodal RT + ST-ADT, especially when PSA and nodal risk are higher.

RADICALS-HD 0 versus 6 months ADT showed that, in many intermediate-risk postoperative RT patients, short-term ADT did not significantly improve 10-year DMFS, although clinical PFS and time to salvage ADT improved. Decipher is a 22-gene genomic classifier scored from 0 to 1. RTOG 9601 validation showed association with bPFS, DMFS, and OS, supporting personalized decisions about ADT and treatment intensity with salvage RT. The source classification is low <0.45, intermediate 0.45–0.6, and high >0.6.

6. pN1 after prostatectomy

For pN1 disease, retrospective data support benefit from adjuvant RT in selected patients. Benefit is strongest with higher nodal burden or adverse features such as high Gleason score, pT3b–4 disease, or positive margin. The benefit of adjuvant RT over salvage RT becomes more apparent as positive node count increases, especially with ≥4 nodes.

Post-RP high-yield: most adverse pathology with undetectable PSA → observation plus early salvage. Detectable or rising PSA → early salvage RT. Higher-risk salvage → add ADT and consider pelvic nodes. pN1, especially ≥4 nodes or adverse features → stronger support for adjuvant RT plus systemic therapy.

Prostate-directed RT in metastatic CSPC 與 oligometastatic MDT

第十八部分：Prostate-directed RT in metastatic CSPC

一、STAMPEDE

STAMPEDE 測試 de novo metastatic prostate cancer 中，對 primary prostate 加 RT 是否有 benefit。病人依 CHAARTED burden 用 CT/MRI 分層，不是 PSMA PET。High metastatic burden 定義為 ≥4 bone metastases 且至少 1 個在 vertebral bodies 或 pelvis 之外，或有 visceral metastases。Low metastatic burden 則是其餘病人，包括 ≤3 bone metastases，或 bone mets only involving vertebral bodies/pelvis。

Randomization：standard arm 為 lifelong ADT，部分病人後來接受 docetaxel；experimental arm 則在 systemic therapy 上加 prostate RT。RT regimen 有兩種：55 Gy/20 Fx daily over 4 weeks，或 36 Gy/6 Fx weekly over 6 weeks。

結果：low metastatic burden patients 有 OS benefit。5-year OS：without RT 53% vs with RT 65%。High burden patients 沒有 benefit：without RT 35% vs with RT 30%。Failure-free survival 在所有病人改善。QoL 沒有顯著差異，5 年 overall G3+ toxicity 類似，1 年 grade 3+ urinary toxicity 約 2%，grade 3+ bowel toxicity 約 3%。

二、STOPCAP meta-analysis

STOPCAP prospective meta-analysis 合併 STAMPEDE 和 HORRAD。STAMPEDE 使用 55 Gy/20 Fx 或 36 Gy/6 Fx；HORRAD 使用 70 Gy/35 Fx 或 57.76 Gy/19 Fx。STOPCAP 顯示 prostate-directed RT 在 <5 bone lesions patients 中改善 OS；3-year OS 約 70% → 77%。Unselected metastatic patients 沒有 OS benefit，但 failure-free survival 和 biochemical PFS 有約 10% benefit，且 effect size 依 metastatic burden 不同。

三、PEACE-1

PEACE-1 是 de novo mCSPC trial，採 2×2 factorial design，比較 SOC、SOC + abiraterone、SOC + prostate RT 74 Gy/37 Fx、SOC + abiraterone + prostate RT。SOC 包含 ADT ± docetaxel。Abiraterone 加到 SOC 明顯改善 rPFS 和 OS。Low-volume population 中，SOC + abiraterone + prostate RT 改善 rPFS，7.5 years with RT vs 4.4 years without RT，但未見 OS benefit。在 overall population，prostate RT 改善 time to serious GU events 和 castration-resistance-free survival，但 time to GU event 較難解讀，因為 receiving prostate RT 本身也被定義為 GU event。

Metastatic prostate RT high-yield：Low-volume de novo mCSPC → prostate RT 應強烈考慮。STAMPEDE regimen：55 Gy/20 Fx daily 或 36 Gy/6 weekly fractions。PSMA PET era 要小心：STAMPEDE 用 CT/MRI 分層，今天用 PSMA PET 可能把原本 CT low-volume 的病人 upstage。

第十九部分：Oligometastatic prostate cancer 的 MDT

四、STOMP and ORIOLE concept

Oligometastatic prostate cancer 中，MDT/SBRT 可延後 progression 和 ADT initiation。STOMP 和 ORIOLE 都支持 selected oligometastatic patients 使用 MDT。Pooled ORIOLE + STOMP analysis 顯示 median PFS 11.9 months with MDT vs 5.9 months with observation。Source file 將此總結為 MDT 對 ≤3 metastases 可改善 PFS。

五、EXTEND

EXTEND randomized oligometastatic mCSPC patients with ≤5 metastases and receiving ADT 至 continued ADT alone vs ADT + SBRT to all metastatic lesions。SBRT 改善 PFS：ADT alone median PFS 15.8 months，ADT + SBRT arm median PFS not reached。也改善 eugonadal PFS after testosterone recovery，代表 MDT 可能幫助 intermittent ADT strategy。Correlative immune assays 顯示 ADT + SBRT arm 有 increased T-cell activation、proliferation、clonal expansion。

MDT high-yield：對 ≤3–5 metastases，特別是 PSMA PET-detected oligometastatic disease，SBRT/MDT 可改善 PFS、延後 systemic escalation 或 ADT，但 OS benefit 仍未完全確立。

Part XVIII. Prostate-Directed RT in Metastatic CSPC

1. STAMPEDE

STAMPEDE tested whether adding RT to the primary prostate improves outcomes in de novo metastatic prostate cancer. Patients were classified by CHAARTED burden using CT/MRI, not PSMA PET. High metastatic burden was defined as ≥4 bone metastases with at least one outside the vertebral bodies or pelvis, or visceral metastases. Low burden was all other patients, including ≤3 bone metastases or bone metastases confined to vertebral bodies or pelvis.

The standard arm was lifelong ADT, with some later receiving docetaxel. The experimental arm added prostate RT. Two RT regimens were used: 55 Gy in 20 daily fractions over 4 weeks, or 36 Gy in 6 weekly fractions over 6 weeks.

Low metastatic burden patients had an OS benefit: 5-year OS improved from about 53% to 65%. High-burden patients did not benefit. Failure-free survival improved in all patients. QoL and overall grade 3+ toxicity were similar.

2. STOPCAP meta-analysis

STOPCAP combined STAMPEDE and HORRAD. STAMPEDE used 55 Gy/20 fractions or 36 Gy/6 weekly fractions; HORRAD used 70 Gy/35 fractions or 57.76 Gy/19 fractions. STOPCAP showed OS improvement from prostate-directed RT in patients with fewer than 5 bone lesions, while unselected metastatic patients did not have an OS benefit. Failure-free survival and biochemical PFS improved by about 10%, with effect size depending on metastatic burden.

3. PEACE-1

PEACE-1 was a de novo mCSPC 2×2 factorial trial comparing standard of care, SOC plus abiraterone, SOC plus prostate RT 74 Gy/37 fractions, and SOC plus abiraterone plus prostate RT. SOC included ADT with or without docetaxel. Abiraterone improved rPFS and OS. In low-volume disease, adding prostate RT to SOC plus abiraterone improved rPFS but not OS. Prostate RT also improved time to serious GU events and castration-resistance-free survival, but GU event endpoints are harder to interpret because receiving prostate RT itself counted as a GU event.

Metastatic prostate RT high-yield: low-volume de novo mCSPC → strongly consider prostate RT. STAMPEDE regimens: 55 Gy/20 daily fractions or 36 Gy/6 weekly fractions. In the PSMA PET era, be careful: STAMPEDE used CT/MRI staging, and PSMA PET may upstage patients who were CT low-volume.

Part XIX. MDT for Oligometastatic Prostate Cancer

4. STOMP and ORIOLE concept

In oligometastatic prostate cancer, MDT/SBRT can delay progression and ADT initiation. STOMP and ORIOLE support MDT in selected oligometastatic patients. Pooled ORIOLE + STOMP analysis showed median PFS 11.9 months with MDT versus 5.9 months with observation. The source summarizes that MDT improves PFS for ≤3 metastases.

5. EXTEND

EXTEND randomized oligometastatic mCSPC patients with ≤5 metastases receiving ADT to continued ADT alone versus ADT plus SBRT to all metastatic lesions. SBRT improved PFS, with ADT alone median PFS 15.8 months and median PFS not reached in the ADT + SBRT arm. It also improved eugonadal PFS after testosterone recovery, suggesting MDT may support intermittent ADT strategies. Correlative immune assays showed increased T-cell activation, proliferation, and clonal expansion with ADT + SBRT.

MDT high-yield: for ≤3–5 metastases, especially PSMA PET-detected oligometastatic disease, SBRT/MDT can improve PFS and delay systemic escalation or ADT, but OS benefit is not fully established.

Hanks et al, IJROBP, 2002 · N=233

Population: Low risk prostate group from trial: T1-T2a, GS 2-6

Methods: 3DCRT dose escalation: <70 Gy 70-72.5 Gy 72.5-75 Gy 75-77.5 Gy 77.5-80 Gy

Results: <70 Gy vs. 70-72 Gy vs. >72 Gy: PSA <10: 8-yr bPFS 68% vs. 74% vs. 68% (NS) PSA 10-20: 8-yr bPFS 19% vs 31% vs. 84% (p=-.003) Dose escalation gave benefit with PSA 10-20, but not PSA >20 At >9 years f/u, adverse effects similar, except increased grade 2 GI with dose escalation

Interpretation: Dose escalation improves bPFS. Results are sustained at long term f/u.

Brachytherapy boost · Brachy boost LDR vs. HDR

Crook et al, IJROBP, 2024 · N=195

Population: intermediate and high risk prostate cancer 57% high risk, 43% unfavorable intermediate risk

Methods: 46 Gy WPRT ➜ ➜ LDR 110 Gy boost vs. ➜ 🏆 HDR 15 Gy boost Primary endpoint: QOL adverse effects data was collected retrospectively

Results: GU QOL eventually was similar at 18 months. HDR GU QOL recovered by 6 months, and LDR QOL took longer, 18 monhts Bowel QOL was improved in HDR after 5 years No difference in sexual HRQOL GU grade 3 in n=1 in both arms GU grade 2 in 2% LDR vs. 7% HDR GI grade 3 in n=1 in LDR, none in HDR GI grade 2 in 11% vs. 5% Median time to nadir PSA 14.1 vs. 13.8 mos 4-yr ≥0.2 PSA 83% vs. 81% 5-yr bFFS 90% vs. 94% 8-yr bFFS 85% vs. 86%

Interpretation: HDR boost has improved bowel QOL over LDR. GU QOL is similar at 18 months, but HDR recovers sooner.

Brachytherapy boost · EBRT + LDR boost vs. LDR alone

Michalski et al, JCO, 2023 · N=588

Population: intermediate risk prostate cancer: T1c-T2b and GS 2-6/PSA 10-20 or GS7/PSA<10

Methods: ➜ 45 Gy partial pelvis EBRT/IMRT+LDR vs. ➜ LDR alone

Results: 5-yr FFF-ASTRO no change 86% vs. 83% 5-yr FFF-Phoenix 88% vs. 86% 5-yr late GU & GI grade ≥2 adverse effects 43% vs. 26% 5-yr late GU & GI grade ≥3 adverse effects 8% vs. 4%

Interpretation: There was no difference in PFS with LDR boost with EBRT vs LDR alone. LDR boost with EBRT worsened adverse effects.
