總論

乳癌總論：流行病學、分型、存活與臨床表現

乳癌是女性最常見的惡性腫瘤之一，若排除非黑色素瘤皮膚癌，女性終生風險約為 1/8。它也是女性癌症死亡的第二常見原因，第一是肺癌。乳癌分類最重要的是 HR status 與 HER2 status，因為這會直接決定全身治療策略。

  • Subtype distribution：HER2-/HR+ 約 72%、HER2+/HR+ 約 10%、HER2+/HR- 約 5%、triple-negative 約 13%。
  • 5 年存活率：stage 0 接近 100%、stage I 約 98%、stage II 約 93%、stage III 約 72%、stage IV 約 22%。
  • 臨床發現來源：約 90% 由 screening mammogram 偵測，約 10% 由自我或臨床乳房檢查發現。
  • 常見症狀：無痛性腫塊、疼痛、乳頭分泌物、乳頭凹陷，或以腋下淋巴結腫大但乳房原發灶不明的方式表現。
  • 鑑別診斷：fibroadenoma、cyst、infection、Mondor’s cord、intraductal papilloma、sclerosing adenosis、lactocele。
  • 篩檢建議：ACS 建議 40–44 歲可選擇開始 mammogram，45–54 歲每年一次，≥55 歲每兩年一次；USPSTF 在投影片中列為 40–74 歲每兩年一次，≥75 歲不建議常規篩檢。
  • 教學提醒：篩檢建議屬投影片內容，實際臨床仍應依所在地 guideline 與病人風險調整。

Breast cancer is one of the most common malignancies in women. Excluding non-melanoma skin cancer, the lifetime risk for women is approximately 1 in 8. It is also the second most common cause of cancer death in women, after lung cancer. The most important clinical classification is based on hormone receptor status and HER2 status, because these directly determine systemic therapy.

  • Subtype distribution: HER2-negative/hormone receptor-positive about 72%, HER2-positive/hormone receptor-positive about 10%, HER2-positive/hormone receptor-negative about 5%, and triple-negative about 13%.
  • Five-year survival by stage: stage 0 nearly 100%, stage I about 98%, stage II about 93%, stage III about 72%, and stage IV about 22%.
  • Mode of detection: about 90% are detected by screening mammography, and about 10% are found by self-examination or clinical breast examination.
  • Common symptoms: painless mass, pain, nipple discharge, nipple retraction, or axillary lymphadenopathy with an occult breast primary.
  • Differential diagnosis: fibroadenoma, cyst, infection, Mondor’s cord, intraductal papilloma, sclerosing adenosis, and lactocele.
  • Screening recommendations in the teaching slides: ACS suggests optional mammography at ages 40–44, annual mammography at ages 45–54, and biennial mammography at age 55 or older; USPSTF is listed as biennial screening from ages 40–74, with no routine screening recommended at age 75 or older.
  • Teaching caveat: screening should be individualized according to local guidelines and patient risk.

乳房解剖與淋巴引流

乳房分成四個象限。上外象限，upper outer quadrant, UOQ，體積最大，也是乳癌最常發生的位置；下內象限，lower inner quadrant, LIQ，是較少見的位置。腫瘤位置通常以 anatomical position 的 o’clock position 描述。

乳房淋巴引流主要到 腋下淋巴結 level I–III。內側腫瘤，尤其下內象限腫瘤，可能引流到 internal mammary nodes, IMN。Axillary levels、supraclavicular nodes、internal mammary nodes 的解剖界線，是放療 contouring 和考試都很重要的內容。

The breast is divided into four quadrants. The upper outer quadrant has the largest tissue volume and is the most common location for breast cancer; the lower inner quadrant is a less common location. Tumor position is usually described by anatomical o’clock position.

Breast lymphatic drainage is primarily to axillary nodal levels I–III. Medial tumors, especially lower-inner-quadrant tumors, may drain to the internal mammary nodes. Axillary levels, supraclavicular nodes, and internal mammary nodes are important for both radiotherapy contouring and examinations.

Workup

Workup：診斷與分期檢查

  • 基本 workup：完整病史與理學檢查，尤其是雙側乳房檢查、腋下與鎖骨上淋巴結檢查。
  • 乳房影像：mammography、ultrasound，必要時 breast MRI。
  • 病理診斷：以 core needle biopsy 為首選，因為 fine needle aspiration 通常無法充分區分 DCIS 與 invasive carcinoma。
  • Systemic staging：若是 clinical stage IIIA 以上，例如 T3N1、T4 或 N2，需要 CT chest/abdomen/pelvis、bone scan 或 PET/CT，必要時考慮 brain MRI。
  • 心臟功能：若預計使用 anthracycline-based chemotherapy，需先做 echocardiogram 評估心臟功能。
  • Basic workup: complete history and physical examination, especially bilateral breast examination and axillary and supraclavicular nodal examination.
  • Breast imaging: mammography, ultrasound, and breast MRI when indicated.
  • Pathologic diagnosis: core needle biopsy is preferred, because fine needle aspiration usually cannot adequately distinguish DCIS from invasive carcinoma.
  • Systemic staging: clinical stage IIIA or higher, such as T3N1, T4, or N2 disease, requires CT chest/abdomen/pelvis, bone scan or PET/CT, and brain MRI when clinically indicated.
  • Cardiac function: obtain echocardiography before anthracycline-based chemotherapy.

  • Tis：DCIS。
  • T1mi：≤1 mm；T1a：>1–5 mm；T1b：>5–10 mm；T1c：>10–20 mm。
  • T2：>2–5 cm；T3：>5 cm。
  • T4a：chest wall invasion，不包括單純 pectoralis invasion。
  • T4b：skin ulceration、satellite skin nodules 或 edema；T4d：inflammatory carcinoma。
  • cN1：可活動的 ipsilateral level I/II axillary nodes。
  • cN2：fixed/matted level I/II nodes 或 clinically positive IMN without axillary nodes。

DCIS

DCIS：乳管原位癌、RT benefit 與 omission

DCIS 約占所有乳癌的 20%。若不治療，部分 DCIS 可在長期追蹤中進展為 invasive malignancy，投影片中提到 30 年內可達 30%。大部分 DCIS 為 hormone-positive，約 80%。DCIS 通常由 screening mammogram 發現，最常見影像表現是 microcalcifications；linear branching calcifications 較支持 high-grade DCIS，fine granular calcifications 較支持 low-grade DCIS。DCIS 屬於 stage 0 breast cancer, pTis。

DCIS lumpectomy 後，adjuvant WBI 大約可使 local recurrence 風險減半，包括 DCIS recurrence 與 invasive recurrence，但沒有 OS benefit。投影片強調：surgery alone 後 DCIS recurrence 可高達 30%，而 recurrence 中約一半是 invasive recurrence。

DCIS accounts for about 20% of all breast cancers. If untreated, some DCIS can progress to invasive malignancy over long-term follow-up; the slides note a rate up to 30% over 30 years. Most DCIS is hormone-positive, about 80%. DCIS is usually detected by screening mammography, most commonly as microcalcifications; linear branching calcifications suggest high-grade DCIS, while fine granular calcifications suggest low-grade DCIS. DCIS is stage 0 breast cancer, pTis.

After lumpectomy for DCIS, adjuvant whole-breast irradiation approximately halves local recurrence, including both DCIS recurrence and invasive recurrence, but does not improve overall survival. The slides emphasize that DCIS recurrence after surgery alone can be as high as 30%, and about half of recurrences are invasive.

早期乳癌

Early-stage invasive breast cancer：保乳手術後 RT 與 boost

早期乳癌接受保乳手術後，adjuvant RT 可大幅降低同側乳房復發。NSABP B-06 納入 stage I–II breast cancer，randomized total mastectomy + ALND、lumpectomy + ALND、lumpectomy + ALND + RT。20 年結果顯示，RT 將 ipsilateral breast recurrence 從 39% 降到 14%，且不受 nodal status 影響；三組 DFS 與 OS 沒有顯著差異。

  • EBCTCG meta-analysis：納入 17 個 trials、10,801 位病人，顯示 BCS 後 RT 將 10 年 recurrence 從 35% 降到 19%。整體概念是：每避免 4 個 10 年內 recurrence，約可避免 1 個 15 年 breast cancer death。
  • 現代 recurrence risk：過去 BCS alone 復發約 30%，加 RT 降至約 15%；現代大約是無 RT 15%、有 RT 8%、RT + ET 2–3%。原因包括 mammographic screening、image-guided surgery、adequate margins、radiation boost、以及更好的 systemic therapy。
  • Tumor bed boost：可降低 IBTR，但代價是 fibrosis 增加。
  • EORTC 22881：margin-negative patients 中，50 Gy/25 Fx 後加 15–16 Gy boost，可將 10 年 LR 從 10% 降到 6%，20 年 LR 從 16% 降到 12%；但 severe fibrosis 也增加。
  • Boost benefit 較大的族群：age <50、high grade、adjacent DCIS。

After breast-conserving surgery for early-stage invasive breast cancer, adjuvant radiotherapy substantially reduces ipsilateral breast recurrence. NSABP B-06 enrolled stage I–II breast cancer and randomized patients to total mastectomy plus axillary lymph node dissection, lumpectomy plus axillary lymph node dissection, or lumpectomy plus axillary lymph node dissection plus radiotherapy. At 20 years, radiotherapy reduced ipsilateral breast recurrence from 39% to 14%, independent of nodal status, with no significant difference in disease-free survival or overall survival among the three groups.

  • EBCTCG meta-analysis: included 17 trials and 10,801 patients; radiotherapy after breast-conserving surgery reduced 10-year recurrence from 35% to 19%. The general concept is that for every 4 recurrences avoided by year 10, about 1 breast cancer death is avoided by year 15.
  • Modern recurrence risk: older studies showed recurrence around 30% after breast-conserving surgery alone and 15% after radiotherapy; modern estimates are approximately 15% without radiotherapy, 8% with radiotherapy, and 2–3% with radiotherapy plus endocrine therapy. Reasons include mammographic screening, image-guided surgery, adequate margins, radiotherapy boost, and improved systemic therapy.
  • Tumor bed boost: reduces ipsilateral breast tumor recurrence but increases fibrosis.
  • EORTC 22881: in margin-negative patients, adding a 15–16 Gy boost after 50 Gy in 25 fractions reduced 10-year local recurrence from 10% to 6% and 20-year local recurrence from 16% to 12%, but also increased severe fibrosis.
  • Groups with greater boost benefit: age below 50, high grade, and adjacent DCIS.

Axilla/RNI

Tamoxifen、SLNB/ALND 與 RNI

  • NSABP B-21：比較 tamoxifen、RT、RT + tamoxifen。14 年 IBTR 約為 tamoxifen alone 29%、RT alone 11%、RT + tamoxifen 10%，OS 類似。意思是：RT 對 IBTR 的降低幅度比 tamoxifen alone 更明顯；tamoxifen 則可降低 contralateral breast cancer risk。
  • ALND morbidity：ALND historically 可帶來約 25% lymphedema risk，因此現代多以 SLNB 取代。
  • NSABP B-32：SLNB-guided management 有良好準確性，且 OS、DFS、locoregional control 與 ALND 相近，但 morbidity 較少。
  • AMAROS：axillary RT/RNI 可替代 ALND，10 年 LRR 類似，但 lymphedema 較低。
  • ACOSOG Z0011：支持在 selected cT1–2N0、1–2 positive SLNs、接受 BCS + WBI 的病人中，可省略 completion ALND。
  • RNI：在 node-positive 或 high-risk node-negative 病人中可改善 DFS，但會增加 lymphedema 與 pneumonitis。
  • MA.20：WBI + RNI 可改善 10 年 DFS，但 OS 沒有明顯改善。
  • EORTC 22922：IMN/medial SCV irradiation 可改善 DFS，長期追蹤也顯示 breast cancer mortality 下降。
  • NSABP B-21: compared tamoxifen, radiotherapy, and radiotherapy plus tamoxifen. Fourteen-year ipsilateral breast tumor recurrence was about 29% with tamoxifen alone, 11% with radiotherapy alone, and 10% with radiotherapy plus tamoxifen, with similar overall survival. Radiotherapy reduces ipsilateral breast recurrence more than tamoxifen alone, while tamoxifen reduces contralateral breast cancer risk.
  • Axillary lymph node dissection morbidity: historically causes about 25% lymphedema risk, so modern management often replaces it with sentinel lymph node biopsy.
  • NSABP B-32: sentinel-node-guided management has good accuracy and similar overall survival, disease-free survival, and locoregional control compared with axillary dissection, with less morbidity.
  • AMAROS: axillary radiotherapy/regional nodal irradiation can replace axillary dissection, with similar 10-year locoregional recurrence and lower lymphedema.
  • ACOSOG Z0011: supports omission of completion axillary dissection in selected cT1–2N0 patients with 1–2 positive sentinel nodes who receive breast-conserving surgery and whole-breast irradiation.
  • Regional nodal irradiation: improves disease-free survival in node-positive or high-risk node-negative patients, but increases lymphedema and pneumonitis.
  • MA.20: whole-breast irradiation plus regional nodal irradiation improved 10-year disease-free survival, without clear overall-survival improvement.
  • EORTC 22922: internal mammary and medial supraclavicular irradiation improved disease-free survival, and long-term follow-up showed reduced breast cancer mortality.
省略 RT

何時可省略乳房 RT？

CALGB 9343 與 PRIME II 支持在特定 elderly low-risk HR+ patients 中可省略 RT，但前提是病人願意接受 endocrine therapy，且能接受較高 local recurrence risk。

  • CALGB 9343：納入 ≥70 歲、stage I、ER+，lumpectomy 後 randomized tamoxifen ± RT；RT 將 10 年 LRR 從 10% 降到 2%，但 OS、DFS、mastectomy-free survival 無差異。
  • PRIME II：納入 ≥65 歲、stage I、ER/PR+、tumor ≤3 cm、negative margin；10 年 LR 約為 ET alone 10% vs ET + RT 1%，同樣沒有 OS benefit。
  • 臨床重點：省略 RT 不是因為 RT 沒有效，而是因為在高齡、低風險、HR+、願意吃 ET 的病人中，local recurrence benefit 可能不轉化成 OS benefit。
  • 通常不適合省略 RT：年輕、high grade、TNBC、HER2+、positive margin、需要 boost 的族群。

CALGB 9343 and PRIME II support radiotherapy omission in selected elderly, low-risk, hormone receptor-positive patients, provided that patients are willing to take endocrine therapy and accept a higher local-recurrence risk.

  • CALGB 9343: enrolled patients aged 70 or older with stage I estrogen receptor-positive disease after lumpectomy, randomized to tamoxifen with or without radiotherapy. Radiotherapy reduced 10-year locoregional recurrence from 10% to 2%, but overall survival, disease-free survival, and mastectomy-free survival were similar.
  • PRIME II: enrolled patients aged 65 or older with stage I estrogen receptor/progesterone receptor-positive tumors, tumor size ≤3 cm, and negative margins. Ten-year local recurrence was about 10% with endocrine therapy alone versus 1% with endocrine therapy plus radiotherapy, with no overall-survival benefit.
  • Clinical point: radiotherapy is not omitted because it is ineffective; it is omitted because in elderly, low-risk, hormone receptor-positive patients willing to take endocrine therapy, local-recurrence benefit may not translate into overall-survival benefit.
  • Usually not appropriate for omission: young age, high-grade disease, triple-negative disease, HER2-positive disease, positive margin, or boost-indicated disease.

NAC

NAC：術前輔助化療與 residual disease escalation

局部晚期乳癌多數會接受 NAC。NAC 的目的不是單純改善 OS，而是 downstage tumor and nodes、增加保乳可能、觀察 treatment response、並根據 residual disease 決定 adjuvant escalation。

  • NSABP B-18：比較 preoperative vs postoperative AC，DFS 與 OS 沒有顯著差異，但 preoperative arm 有較高 BCS rate。
  • NSABP B-27：加入 preoperative docetaxel 可使 pCR 約加倍，從 13% 到 26%，但 OS/DFS 沒有顯著差異。
  • pCR：是重要 prognostic marker，但不應被簡單當作 universal surrogate endpoint。

NAC 後 PMRT

NAC 後 PMRT

  • 實用規則：clinical stage III 或 ypN+ disease 通常需要 PMRT/RNI。
  • cN1 → ypN0：NSABP B-51 是關鍵試驗；投影片摘要指出 B-51 abstract 結果顯示 RNI vs no RNI 在 IBTR、DMFS、OS 沒有明顯差異，因此這部分 practice 正在演變。
  • MDACC retrospective data：NAC 後 PMRT 可降低 10 年 LRR，尤其在 stage III/IV 或 pCR 但初始風險很高的病人中，PMRT 仍可能有價值。
  • 投影片重點：PMRT 在 cT4、pN2–3、clinical stage IIIB 以上病人中可改善 cancer-specific survival。
  • PMRT 範圍：通常包括 chest wall / reconstructed breast 與 RNI，也就是 SCV、未廓清的 axillary nodes、以及 IMN。
  • PMRT indication：包括 ≥4 positive nodes、T3 with positive nodes、stage III、ypN+ after NAC；ypN0 after NAC 則依 B-51 與個案風險討論。
  • Practical rule: clinical stage III disease or ypN+ disease generally requires postmastectomy radiotherapy and regional nodal irradiation.
  • cN1 → ypN0: NSABP B-51 is the key trial; the slide summary states that the B-51 abstract showed no clear difference in ipsilateral breast tumor recurrence, distant metastasis-free survival, or overall survival between RNI and no RNI, so practice is evolving.
  • MD Anderson retrospective data: PMRT after NAC can reduce 10-year locoregional recurrence, especially in stage III/IV disease or in patients with pathologic complete response but very high initial risk.
  • Teaching point: PMRT can improve cancer-specific survival in cT4, pN2–3, and clinical stage IIIB or higher disease.
  • PMRT volume: usually includes the chest wall or reconstructed breast plus regional nodal irradiation, including the supraclavicular region, undissected axillary nodes, and internal mammary nodes.
  • PMRT indications: include ≥4 positive nodes, T3 with positive nodes, stage III disease, and ypN+ after NAC; ypN0 after NAC should be discussed according to B-51 and individual risk.
Bolus/IMNI

Bolus 與 IMN irradiation

  • PMRT bolus：會增加皮膚劑量與皮膚毒性，因此應 selective 使用。
  • EORTC bolus guideline：建議在皮膚復發風險高且 skin 未被 95–100% isodose 覆蓋時考慮，例如 positive anterior margin 且未切除皮膚、skin involvement、inflammatory stage T4b–d / ypT4、chest wall recurrence、inoperable 或 fungating breast cancer。
  • IMN irradiation：benefit 較細緻。DBCG 82b/82c、British Columbia、MA.20、EORTC 22922 均包含 IMN 或 medial SCV coverage 且顯示整體 benefit，但很難單獨歸因於 IMNI。
  • Dedicated IMN trials：French trial 未顯示 10-year OS benefit；DBCG-IMN 顯示 right-sided IMNI 可小幅改善 OS 與 breast cancer mortality。
  • 臨床考量：node-positive、medial/central tumor、較高風險 biology 時，IMNI 更值得考慮，但必須平衡 heart/lung dose。
  • PMRT bolus: increases skin dose and skin toxicity, so it should be used selectively.
  • EORTC bolus guideline: consider bolus when skin recurrence risk is high and skin is not covered by the 95–100% isodose, such as positive anterior margin without skin resection, skin involvement, inflammatory stage T4b–d / ypT4 disease, chest-wall recurrence, inoperable disease, or fungating breast cancer.
  • Internal mammary nodal irradiation: benefit is nuanced. DBCG 82b/82c, British Columbia, MA.20, and EORTC 22922 all included internal mammary or medial supraclavicular coverage and showed overall benefit, but the benefit is difficult to attribute to IMNI alone.
  • Dedicated internal mammary trials: the French trial did not show a 10-year overall-survival benefit; DBCG-IMN showed that right-sided IMNI slightly improved overall survival and breast cancer mortality.
  • Clinical consideration: IMNI is more worth considering for node-positive disease, medial/central tumors, and higher-risk biology, but heart and lung dose must be balanced.

重點

最重要 take-home messages

  • Subtype-driven management：乳癌治療的核心是 subtype-driven management。HR+/HER2-、HER2+、TNBC 的 systemic therapy、pCR rate、NAC 後 adjuvant escalation 都不同，因此 core biopsy、ER/PR/HER2/Ki67 是治療決策的起點。
  • DCIS：DCIS lumpectomy 後 RT 可使 local recurrence 風險約減半，包括 invasive recurrence，但沒有 OS benefit。即使 low-risk DCIS，復發風險仍會隨時間累積，因此省略 RT 必須是 patient-specific decision。Mastectomy 後 DCIS 通常不需 PMRT。
  • Early-stage invasive breast cancer：接受 BCS 後，RT 是降低 IBTR 的核心工具。Boost 可進一步降低局部復發，尤其年輕與 high-risk 病人，但會增加 fibrosis。Elderly low-risk HR+ 病人可以考慮省略 RT，但必須理解 RT 仍能把 10 年 IBTR 從約 10% 降到 1–2%。
  • Locally advanced 或 high-risk breast cancer：PMRT + RNI 可改善 LRR，且在 node-positive、T3–T4、skin/pectoral fascia invasion、clinical stage III 等族群可改善 OS。DBCG 82b/82c、British Columbia、EBCTCG 是 PMRT/RNI 的核心證據。
  • 現代乳癌放療趨勢：40 Gy/15 Fx 或 42.56 Gy/16 Fx 已是成熟方案；26 Gy/5 Fx 有 FAST Forward 支持；SIB 可 3 週完成 WBI + boost；PBI 適合 selected low-risk patients；selected ipsilateral recurrence 可考慮 partial breast reirradiation。
  • Subtype-driven management: breast cancer treatment is driven by subtype. Hormone receptor-positive/HER2-negative, HER2-positive, and triple-negative breast cancers differ in systemic therapy, pCR rate, and adjuvant escalation after neoadjuvant chemotherapy. Core biopsy with estrogen receptor, progesterone receptor, HER2, and Ki67 is the starting point for treatment decisions.
  • DCIS: radiotherapy after DCIS lumpectomy approximately halves local recurrence, including invasive recurrence, but does not improve overall survival. Even low-risk DCIS has recurrence risk that accumulates over time, so radiotherapy omission must be patient-specific. DCIS after mastectomy usually does not require postmastectomy radiotherapy.
  • Early-stage invasive breast cancer: after breast-conserving surgery, radiotherapy is the core tool for reducing ipsilateral breast tumor recurrence. Boost further reduces local recurrence, especially in young and high-risk patients, but increases fibrosis. Elderly low-risk hormone receptor-positive patients can consider radiotherapy omission, but must understand that radiotherapy still reduces 10-year ipsilateral breast tumor recurrence from about 10% to 1–2%.
  • Locally advanced or high-risk breast cancer: postmastectomy radiotherapy plus regional nodal irradiation improves locoregional recurrence and can improve overall survival in node-positive disease, T3–T4 disease, skin or pectoral fascia invasion, and clinical stage III disease. DBCG 82b/82c, British Columbia, and EBCTCG are core PMRT/RNI evidence.
  • Modern breast radiotherapy trend: treatment courses are shorter. 40 Gy in 15 fractions or 42.56 Gy in 16 fractions are mature regimens; 26 Gy in 5 fractions is supported by FAST Forward; simultaneous integrated boost can complete whole-breast irradiation plus boost in 3 weeks; partial-breast irradiation is appropriate for selected low-risk patients; selected ipsilateral recurrences can be considered for partial breast reirradiation.
