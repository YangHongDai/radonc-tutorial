👤 使用者管理

同一台電腦可以建立多個使用者，作答進度互不干擾。

💡 進度儲存在瀏覽器 localStorage；換瀏覽器/裝置或清除快取會遺失。可使用「📤 匯入／匯出」備份。

💡 Progress is saved in browser localStorage; switching browsers/devices or clearing cache will lose it. Use "📤 Import / Export" to back up.

💡 Practice Mode is "retry mode": instant feedback per question; correct answers clear from the wrong-list automatically.
📋 Exam Mode: no feedback during answering; submit at end to see results.
