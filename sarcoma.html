總論

Sarcoma 總論

Sarcoma 是一群源自 mesenchymal tissue 的異質性腫瘤，整體非常少見，約佔所有惡性腫瘤 1%。Sarcoma 有超過 100 種 histologic subtypes，約 80% 起源於 soft tissue，約 20% 起源於 bone。

成人常見 soft tissue sarcoma 包括 liposarcoma、leiomyosarcoma、undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma, synovial sarcoma、GIST、angiosarcoma。常見 bone sarcoma 包括 osteosarcoma、chondrosarcoma、Ewing sarcoma。

Sarcoma is a heterogeneous group of malignancies arising from mesenchymal tissue. It is rare, accounting for about 1% of all malignant tumors. There are more than 100 histologic subtypes; approximately 80% arise from soft tissue and approximately 20% arise from bone.

Common adult soft-tissue sarcomas include liposarcoma, leiomyosarcoma, undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma, synovial sarcoma, GIST, and angiosarcoma. Common bone sarcomas include osteosarcoma, chondrosarcoma, and Ewing sarcoma.

Workup

Risk factors、presentation 與 workup

STS 的 risk factors 包括 male gender、genetic predisposition、prior radiotherapy or chemotherapy、chemical carcinogens、chronic irritation 與 lymphedema。FAP / Gardner syndrome 與 desmoid tumor 風險增加相關。RT-induced sarcoma 常見 histology 包括 osteosarcoma、UPS、angiosarcoma，中位 latency 約 10 年。

典型 presentation 是 逐漸變大的無痛性腫塊。Retroperitoneal sarcoma 可能造成 lower extremity swelling 或 DVT-like presentation。

任何 soft tissue mass 若有 >5 cm、deep to fascia、rapid growth、painful、recurrent after excision，都應高度懷疑 sarcoma，避免 unplanned excision。

Biopsy 應由未來要開刀的 surgical / orthopedic oncologist 規劃。Biopsy tract 或 open biopsy incision 應 longitudinally along the same plane as tumor，避免污染 uninvolved compartments。Retroperitoneal sarcoma 可用 CT-guided retroperitoneal biopsy，以避免 peritoneal seeding。

Risk factors include male sex, genetic predisposition, prior radiotherapy or chemotherapy, chemical carcinogens, chronic irritation, and lymphedema. FAP / Gardner syndrome is associated with desmoid tumors. Radiation-induced sarcoma commonly includes osteosarcoma, UPS, and angiosarcoma, with median latency around 10 years.

The classic presentation is a gradually enlarging painless mass. Retroperitoneal sarcoma may present with lower-extremity swelling or a DVT-like syndrome.

Any soft-tissue mass that is >5 cm, deep to fascia, rapidly growing, painful, or recurrent after excision should be suspicious for sarcoma. Avoid unplanned excision.

Biopsy should ideally be planned by the surgical or orthopedic oncologist who will perform definitive resection. The biopsy tract should run longitudinally in the same plane as the tumor and avoid contaminating uninvolved compartments. Retroperitoneal sarcoma can be biopsied using a CT-guided retroperitoneal approach to reduce peritoneal seeding risk.

處置

局部處置

肢體 / trunk STS: 目標是 functional limb preservation + en bloc resection + negative margins。Grade 1 通常 surgery alone；grade 2–3 或 stage II–III 通常 RT + surgery。

Unplanned excision: 若 positive margin，優先 re-excision；若無法 re-excise，考慮 adjuvant RT。

Preop vs postop RT: Preop RT 與 postop RT 的 local control 大致相當，約 85–95%。Preop RT dose 較低、volume 較小、長期 fibrosis / edema / stiffness 較少，但 wound complication 較高。Postop RT wound complication 較低，但 dose 較高、volume 較大、late toxicity 較多。

ASTRO 2021 concept: 對 trunk、extremity、retroperitoneum STS，若適合 RT，通常 strongly favor preop RT over postop RT。

Extremity / trunk STS: The goal is functional limb preservation plus en bloc resection with negative margins. Grade 1 disease is usually treated with surgery alone; grade 2–3 or stage II–III disease usually receives RT + surgery.

Unplanned excision: If margin is positive, re-excision is preferred when feasible. If re-excision is not feasible, adjuvant RT is considered.

Preop vs postop RT: Preop and postop RT have broadly similar local control, approximately 85–95%. Preop RT uses a lower dose and smaller volume with less long-term fibrosis, edema, and stiffness, but more wound complications. Postop RT has fewer acute wound complications but requires higher dose and larger volume, with more late toxicity.

全身治療

Systemic therapy evidence

STS chemotherapy benefit 整體是 marginal，不應 routine 給所有病人。通常保留給 high-risk disease，例如 large、deep、high-grade、unfavorable histology、locally advanced 或 metastatic disease。常用 regimen 包括 doxorubicin + ifosfamide，例如 AIM。

Evidence重點
Rosenberg chemotherapy protocol早期小型研究；chemotherapy 改善 3-year DFS/OS，但時代與現代不同
SMAC meta-analysisDoxorubicin-based chemotherapy 改善 local recurrence、distant metastasis、overall recurrence；benefit marginal，toxicity 需權衡
SU2C-SARC032Stage III >5 cm UPS / DDLPS / pleomorphic LPS；preop + postop pembrolizumab 加 RT + surgery 改善 2-year DFS，52% vs 67%，但 G3+ toxicity 較高
SARC032 使 stage III、>5 cm、grade 2–3 UPS / dedifferentiated or pleomorphic liposarcoma 的 perioperative pembrolizumab 成為重要新方向，但應在 MDT 中依 toxicity 與 risk-benefit 討論。

The benefit of chemotherapy in STS is generally marginal, so it should not be routine for all patients. It is usually reserved for high-risk disease: large, deep, high-grade, unfavorable histology, locally advanced, or metastatic disease. A common regimen is doxorubicin + ifosfamide, such as AIM.

後腹腔

Retroperitoneal STS

Retroperitoneal sarcoma 與 extremity STS 不同：常很大，且接近 bowel、kidney、major vessels、spine、psoas。Surgical margin 常受解剖限制。Postop RT 通常不理想，因為術後 bowel 會掉入 tumor bed，OAR toxicity 高；若要 RT，理論上多偏向 preop RT。

Study重點
STRASSPreop RT 50.4 Gy / 28 Fx + surgery vs surgery alone；整體 abdominal RFS 無 benefit；serious adverse events 較高
STRASS subgroupLiposarcoma subgroup 可能有 signal，但不是 definitive
STRASS / STREXIT pooledWDLPS 與 G1–2 DDLPS 可能有 ARFS benefit；G3 DDLPS 與 LMS 無明顯 benefit
STRASS-2Ongoing；針對 G3 DDLPS / LMS 測試 neoadjuvant chemotherapy → surgery vs surgery alone
RPS 是否做 preop RT 必須 histology-specific：WDLPS 或 G1–2 DDLPS 可考慮；G3 DDLPS 或 LMS 不應假設有同樣 RT benefit，主要問題常是 systemic failure。

Retroperitoneal sarcoma differs from extremity STS. Tumors are often large and close to bowel, kidney, major vessels, spine, and psoas. Surgical margins are anatomically constrained. Postop RT is usually problematic because bowel can fall into the tumor bed, increasing OAR toxicity. If RT is used, it is generally more logical preoperatively.

Extremity/trunk grade 2–3 disease usually uses limb-sparing surgery + RT; RT mainly improves local control; preop RT is usually favored because of lower dose, smaller volume, and better long-term function; systemic therapy has limited overall benefit but matters for selected high-risk disease; retroperitoneal STS must be histology-specific.

RTOG 0630

Extremity · IGRT

Wang et al, JCO, 2015 Wang et al, JAMA Oncol, 2023 · N=98

Population: Extremity soft tissue sarcoma

Methods: ➜ Cohort A: Pre-op IGRT with chemotherapy (arm closed early and not reported) ➜ Cohort B: IGRT without chemotherapy IMRT or 3DCRT allowed. 50 Gy. IMRT used in 75% patients

Interpretation: Pre-op RT with IGRT leads to less late adverse effects compared to historical control. pCR is associated with improved OS and reduced LF.

STRASS/STREXIT

Retroperitoneal · pre-op RT vs. no RT

Callegaro et al, Ann Surg, 2022 · N=408

Population: "STREXIT" cohort: Sarcoma patients at 10 STRASS recruiting centers that were not enrolled in STRASS, s/p curative intent surgery, with or without RT

Methods: Propensity matched analysis of STRASS and STREXIT cohorts ➜ pre-op RT to 50.4 Gy then en bloc surgery in 4-8 weeks vs. ➜ en bloc surgery Endpoint: abdominal RFS

Results: RT improved abdominal RFS in well-differentiated liposarcoma and grade 1-2 dedifferentiated liposarcoma No improvement in abdominal RFS with RT in grade 3 dedifferentiated liposarcoma or leiomyosarcoma

Interpretation: In this propensity matched analysis, pre-op RT was associated with improved abdominal RFS in well-differentiated liposarcoma and grade 1-2 dedifferentiated liposarcoma. A randomized trial focused on this subgroup may be warranted.
