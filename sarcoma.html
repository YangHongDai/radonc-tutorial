軟組織肉瘤總論

肉瘤是一群源自間葉組織的高度異質性惡性腫瘤，整體非常少見，約占所有惡性腫瘤的 1%。肉瘤包含超過 100 種組織學亞型，其中約 80% 起源於軟組織，約 20% 起源於骨骼。

成人常見的軟組織肉瘤包括脂肪肉瘤、平滑肌肉瘤、未分化多形性肉瘤（UPS）、滑膜肉瘤、胃腸道基質瘤（GIST）與血管肉瘤。常見骨肉瘤則包括骨肉瘤、軟骨肉瘤與 Ewing 肉瘤。

臨床記憶：肉瘤不是單一疾病，而是由組織學類型主導治療決策的疾病。治療前最重要的是正確規劃切片、確認病理亞型與分級、記錄腫瘤大小與深度、預先規劃手術切緣，並由肉瘤多專科團隊（MDT）共同討論。

Sarcoma is a heterogeneous group of malignancies arising from mesenchymal tissue. It is rare, accounting for about 1% of all malignant tumors. There are more than 100 histologic subtypes; approximately 80% arise from soft tissue and approximately 20% arise from bone.

Common adult soft-tissue sarcomas include liposarcoma, leiomyosarcoma, undifferentiated pleomorphic sarcoma, synovial sarcoma, GIST, and angiosarcoma. Common bone sarcomas include osteosarcoma, chondrosarcoma, and Ewing sarcoma.

危險因子、臨床表現與檢查評估

軟組織肉瘤的危險因子包括男性、遺傳易感性、既往接受放射治療或化學治療、化學致癌物、慢性刺激與淋巴水腫。家族性腺瘤性瘜肉症（FAP）／Gardner 症候群與硬纖維瘤風險增加相關。放射線誘發肉瘤常見的組織學類型包括骨肉瘤、未分化多形性肉瘤（UPS）與血管肉瘤，中位潛伏期約 10 年。

典型臨床表現是逐漸變大的無痛性腫塊。後腹腔肉瘤也可能因壓迫造成下肢腫脹，或呈現類似深層靜脈栓塞的症狀。

任何軟組織腫塊若具有>5 cm、位於筋膜深層、快速增大、疼痛，或切除後復發等特徵，都應高度懷疑肉瘤，並避免未經規劃的切除。

Risk factors include male sex, genetic predisposition, prior radiotherapy or chemotherapy, chemical carcinogens, chronic irritation, and lymphedema. FAP / Gardner syndrome is associated with desmoid tumors. Radiation-induced sarcoma commonly includes osteosarcoma, UPS, and angiosarcoma, with median latency around 10 years.

The classic presentation is a gradually enlarging painless mass. Retroperitoneal sarcoma may present with lower-extremity swelling or a DVT-like syndrome.

Any soft-tissue mass that is >5 cm, deep to fascia, rapidly growing, painful, or recurrent after excision should be suspicious for sarcoma. Avoid unplanned excision.

Biopsy should ideally be planned by the surgical or orthopedic oncologist who will perform definitive resection. 局部治療策略

肢體／軀幹軟組織肉瘤：治療目標是保留肢體功能、整塊切除腫瘤並取得陰性切緣。第 1 級疾病通常可單獨手術；第 2–3 級或第 II–III 期通常採放射治療合併手術。

未經規劃的切除：若切緣陽性，應優先再次切除；若無法再次切除，則考慮輔助放射治療。

術前與術後放射治療：兩者的局部控制率大致相當，約 85–95%。術前放射治療所需劑量較低、照射體積較小，長期纖維化、水腫與關節僵硬較少，但傷口併發症較高。術後放射治療的急性傷口併發症較低，但所需劑量與照射體積較大，晚期毒性較多。

ASTRO 2021 概念：對軀幹、肢體與後腹腔軟組織肉瘤，若需要放射治療，通常強烈優先採術前放射治療，而非術後放射治療。

Extremity / trunk STS: The goal is functional limb preservation plus en bloc resection with negative margins. Grade 1 disease is usually treated with surgery alone; grade 2–3 or stage II–III disease usually receives RT + surgery.

Unplanned excision: If margin is positive, re-excision is preferred when feasible. If re-excision is not feasible, adjuvant RT is considered.

Preop vs postop RT: Preop and postop RT have broadly similar local control, approximately 85–95%. Preop RT uses a lower dose and smaller volume with less long-term fibrosis, edema, and stiffness, but more wound complications. Postop RT has fewer acute wound complications but requires higher dose and larger volume, with more late toxicity.

全身治療證據

軟組織肉瘤化學治療的整體效益有限，不應常規用於所有病人。通常保留給高風險疾病，例如腫瘤體積大、位於深層、惡性度高、不利組織學類型、局部晚期或已轉移。常用方案包括多柔比星加異環磷醯胺，例如 AIM。

證據重點
Rosenberg 化學治療方案早期小型研究；化學治療改善 3 年無病存活（DFS）與整體存活（OS），但研究年代與現代治療不同
SMAC 統合分析以多柔比星為基礎的化學治療降低局部復發、遠端轉移與整體復發；效益有限，必須與毒性權衡
SU2C-SARC032納入第 III 期、>5 cm 的 UPS／DDLPS／多形性脂肪肉瘤；術前及術後帕博利珠單抗合併放射治療與手術，使2 年無病存活由 52% 提升至 67%，但第 3 級以上毒性較高
SARC032 使第 III 期、>5 cm、第 2–3 級 UPS／去分化或多形性脂肪肉瘤的圍手術期帕博利珠單抗成為重要新方向，但應由多專科團隊依毒性與效益個別討論。

The benefit of chemotherapy in STS is generally marginal, so it should not be routine for all patients. It is usually reserved for high-risk disease: large, deep, high-grade, unfavorable histology, locally advanced, or metastatic disease. A common regimen is doxorubicin + ifosfamide, such as AIM.

SARC032 makes perioperative pembrolizumab an important new direction for stage III, >5 cm, grade 2–3 UPS and dedifferentiated or pleomorphic liposarcoma, but it requires MDT discussion because toxicity is higher.
後腹腔軟組織肉瘤

後腹腔肉瘤與肢體軟組織肉瘤不同：腫瘤通常很大，且緊鄰腸道、腎臟、大血管、脊椎與腰大肌，手術切緣常受解剖限制。術後放射治療通常不理想，因為術後腸道會落入原腫瘤床，增加正常器官毒性；若需要放射治療，理論上通常偏向術前治療。

研究重點
STRASS術前放射治療 50.4 Gy／28 次加手術，對比單獨手術；整體腹部無復發存活未改善，嚴重不良事件較高
STRASS 亞組分析脂肪肉瘤亞組可能有獲益訊號，但尚非確定性結論
STRASS／STREXIT 整合分析WDLPS 與第 1–2 級 DDLPS 可能改善腹部無復發存活；第 3 級 DDLPS 與平滑肌肉瘤未見明顯效益
STRASS-2進行中；針對第 3 級 DDLPS／平滑肌肉瘤，比較術前化學治療後手術與單獨手術
後腹腔肉瘤是否接受術前放射治療必須依組織學類型個別決策：WDLPS 或第 1–2 級 DDLPS 可考慮；第 3 級 DDLPS 或平滑肌肉瘤不可假設有相同放射治療效益，因其主要問題常是全身性失敗。

Retroperitoneal sarcoma differs from extremity STS. Tumors are often large and close to bowel, kidney, major vessels, spine, and psoas. Surgical margins are anatomically constrained. Postop RT is usually problematic because bowel can fall into the tumor bed, increasing OAR toxicity. If RT is used, it is generally more logical preoperatively.

StudyKey point
STRASSPreop RT 50.4 Gy / 28 Fx + surgery vs surgery alone; no overall abdominal RFS benefit; more serious adverse events
STRASS subgroupLiposarcoma subgroup showed a possible signal, but not definitive
STRASS / STREXIT pooledWDLPS and G1–2 DDLPS may have ARFS benefit; G3 DDLPS and LMS did not show clear benefit
STRASS-2Ongoing; tests neoadjuvant chemotherapy → surgery vs surgery alone for G3 DDLPS / LMS
Preop RT in RPS must be histology-specific. WDLPS or G1–2 DDLPS can be considered; G3 DDLPS or LMS should not be assumed to have the same RT benefit because systemic failure is often the dominant problem.

高頻考點整理

主題最重要結論
肉瘤生物學肉瘤罕見，約占惡性腫瘤 1%，包含 >100 種亞型；約 80% 為軟組織、20% 為骨骼來源
臨床表現常見逐漸增大的無痛性腫塊；後腹腔腫瘤可因壓迫造成水腫或類深層靜脈栓塞症狀
檢查評估顯影 MRI 評估局部範圍；胸部 CT 排除肺轉移；切片路徑必須能被未來手術切除
病理分級取決於分化程度、有絲分裂數目與壞死比例
預後分級、腫瘤大小與轉移最重要；肺是最常見遠端轉移位置
淋巴結轉移一般少見，但 CARE 組織學類型風險較高：透明細胞肉瘤、血管肉瘤、橫紋肌肉瘤、上皮樣肉瘤
手術保留肢體功能＋整塊切除＋陰性切緣
放射治療角色主要改善局部控制，不一定改善整體存活（OS）
術前與術後放射治療術前傷口併發症較高；術後晚期纖維化、水腫與僵硬較高；現代治療通常偏好術前放射治療
標準術前放射治療50 Gy／25 次
黏液型脂肪肉瘤對放射線敏感；DOREMY 支持術前 36 Gy／18 次的降階治療
RTOG 0630影像導引時代的縮小靶區可降低晚期毒性，且未增加邊緣復發
化學治療整體效益有限，主要用於高風險疾病；常用多柔比星加異環磷醯胺
帕博利珠單抗SARC032 支持第 III 期、>5 cm 的 UPS／DDLPS／多形性脂肪肉瘤加入帕博利珠單抗，可改善無病存活但增加毒性
後腹腔肉瘤STRASS 不支持所有後腹腔肉瘤常規接受術前放射治療；WDLPS／第 1–2 級 DDLPS 可能獲益；第 3 級 DDLPS／平滑肌肉瘤等待 STRASS-2
一句話總結：成人軟組織肉瘤的核心是依組織學類型與風險調整的多模式治療。肢體或軀幹第 2–3 級疾病通常以保肢手術加放射治療為標準；放射治療主要改善局部控制；術前放射治療因劑量較低、照射體積較小且長期功能較佳，通常優於術後放射治療；全身治療的整體效益有限，但可用於特定高風險病人；後腹腔軟組織肉瘤必須依組織學類型個別決策。

One-sentence memory: Adult soft-tissue sarcoma requires histology- and risk-adapted multimodality treatment. Extremity/trunk grade 2–3 disease usually uses limb-sparing surgery + RT; RT mainly improves local control; preop RT is usually favored because of lower dose, smaller volume, and better long-term function; systemic therapy has limited overall benefit but matters for selected high-risk disease; retroperitoneal STS must be histology-specific.

RTOG 0630

Extremity · IGRT

Wang et al, JCO, 2015 Wang et al, JAMA Oncol, 2023 · N=98

Population: Extremity soft tissue sarcoma

Methods: ➜ Cohort A: Pre-op IGRT with chemotherapy (arm closed early and not reported) ➜ Cohort B: IGRT without chemotherapy IMRT or 3DCRT allowed. 50 Gy. IMRT used in 75% patients

Results: 2-yr Grade 2+ adverse effects 11% vs. 37% in historical NCIC trial. LF 6% pCR 19% LF with pCR 0% vs. 9% in <pCR 5-yr OS with pCR 100% vs. 56% if <pCR fibrosis 5% [vs. 32% in NCIC] stiffness 4% [vs. 18%] edema 5% [vs. 15%] wound complications 37% [same] lower extremity wound complications 42% [vs. 45%]

Interpretation: Pre-op RT with IGRT leads to less late adverse effects compared to historical control. pCR is associated with improved OS and reduced LF.

STRASS/STREXIT

Retroperitoneal · pre-op RT vs. no RT

Callegaro et al, Ann Surg, 2022 · N=408

Population: "STREXIT" cohort: Sarcoma patients at 10 STRASS recruiting centers that were not enrolled in STRASS, s/p curative intent surgery, with or without RT

Methods: Propensity matched analysis of STRASS and STREXIT cohorts ➜ pre-op RT to 50.4 Gy then en bloc surgery in 4-8 weeks vs. ➜ en bloc surgery Endpoint: abdominal RFS
