腦轉移：基本概念、診斷與初始處理

腦轉移是成人最常見的顱內腫瘤，佔成人腦腫瘤超過 50%。約 20–40% 癌症病人會發生顱內轉移，且因 MRI 偵測能力提升與全身性治療改善使病人活得更久，臨床發生率變得更常見。約 80% 病人會有多發腦轉移。成人最常見原發腫瘤包括肺癌、乳癌、腎癌、大腸直腸癌、黑色素瘤；兒童則較常見肉瘤、神經母細胞瘤、生殖細胞腫瘤。

  • 致病機轉：腦轉移最常透過血行性轉移到腦部。
  • 典型位置：灰白質交界，因為血管在此口徑變小，容易讓腫瘤細胞團塊被困住。
  • 分布：也常出現在動脈循環的終末分水嶺區。分布大致隨腦部重量與血流：約 80% 大腦半球、15% 小腦、5% 腦幹。
  • 症狀來源：症狀通常來自逐漸增大的腫瘤與腫瘤周圍水腫。
  • 常見表現：頭痛 40–50%、局部神經功能缺損 20–40%、認知功能障礙 30–50%、癲癇發作 10–20%、類中風表現 5–10%。
  • 少見但重要情況：腫瘤內出血、阻塞性水腦症、腫瘤栓塞。

診斷：初始評估應包含完整病史、理學檢查與神經學檢查。影像上，無對比劑頭部電腦斷層主要用於排除腦出血；真正的黃金標準是 含與不含對比劑的腦部磁振造影。典型腦轉移在對比劑前 T1 影像可為等至低訊號；若為出血性病灶可有內在高訊號。對比劑後 T1 影像可呈均勻、點狀或環狀增強；T2／T2-FLAIR 影像通常呈高訊號，可顯示水腫。

病理 原則上需要癌症的組織學診斷。如果病人已有明確轉移性癌症病史，例如已知轉移性肺癌的病人出現新發癲癇，若影像表現相符，即可合理診斷為腦轉移；若沒有癌症病史，則應接受胸腹骨盆電腦斷層、腦部影像檢查，並考慮對其他轉移部位進行切片，或對腦腫瘤進行最大安全切除。

軟腦膜癌病 是癌細胞侵犯腦脊髓液並附著於腦膜。症狀可包括疼痛、癲癇發作、噁心／嘔吐、頭暈、無力、共濟失調、失禁、感覺異常、顱神經功能缺損。診斷依靠 MRI 顯示結節狀腦膜腫瘤、腦膜增厚、明顯對比增強，以及腦脊髓液細胞學；腦脊髓液也可見開放壓升高、白血球增加、蛋白質升高。

Brain metastases are the most common intracranial tumors in adults, accounting for more than 50% of adult brain tumors. Approximately 20–40% of patients with cancer develop intracranial metastases, and the clinical incidence has increased because MRI detects smaller lesions and systemic therapy allows patients to live longer. About 80% of patients have multiple brain metastases. The most common adult primary tumors are lung, breast, kidney, colorectal, and melanoma; in children, sarcoma, neuroblastoma, and germ-cell tumors are more common.

  • Pathogenesis: brain metastases most commonly spread hematogenously to the brain.
  • Typical location: the gray-white matter junction, where vessel caliber narrows and tumor emboli can be trapped.
  • Distribution: they also commonly occur in terminal watershed areas of arterial circulation. Distribution roughly follows brain weight and blood flow: about 80% in cerebral hemispheres, 15% in the cerebellum, and 5% in the brainstem.
  • Source of symptoms: symptoms usually arise from tumor growth and peritumoral edema.
  • Common presentations: headache 40–50%, focal neurologic dysfunction 20–40%, cognitive dysfunction 30–50%, seizures 10–20%, and stroke-like presentation 5–10%.
  • Less common but important situations: intratumoral hemorrhage, obstructive hydrocephalus, and tumor embolization.

Diagnosis: initial evaluation should include complete history, physical examination, and neurologic examination. Non-contrast head CT is mainly used to exclude brain bleeding; the gold standard is MRI brain with and without contrast. Typical brain metastases can be iso- to hypointense on pre-contrast T1; hemorrhagic lesions may have intrinsic high signal. Post-contrast T1 may show uniform, punctate, or ring enhancement; T2/T2 FLAIR is usually hyperintense and shows edema.

Pathology generally requires tissue diagnosis of cancer. If a patient has a clear history of metastatic cancer, such as metastatic lung cancer followed by new-onset seizure, compatible imaging can reasonably establish the diagnosis of brain metastasis. If there is no cancer history, obtain CT chest/abdomen/pelvis, brain imaging, and consider biopsy of another metastatic site or maximal safe resection of the brain tumor.

Leptomeningeal carcinomatosis occurs when cancer cells invade the CSF and attach to the meninges. Symptoms may include pain, seizures, nausea/vomiting, lightheadedness, weakness, ataxia, incontinence, sensory abnormalities, and cranial nerve deficits. Diagnosis relies on MRI showing nodular meningeal tumor, meningeal thickening, strong contrast enhancement, and CSF cytology; CSF may also show increased opening pressure, leukocytes, and elevated protein.

腦轉移的初始管理與治療選擇

支持性照護應優先進行。腦轉移的急性管理先處理症狀。可使用抗癲癇藥物、止痛藥物等。Dexamethasone（地塞米松）對水腫相關症狀很重要，教材指出，類固醇可在 3 天內改善約 75% 病人的症狀。常見做法是靜脈注射 dexamethasone 10 mg 負荷劑量，之後每 6 小時 4 mg，待腫瘤導向治療開始後再逐漸減量。使用類固醇時要給 PPI 做腸胃道預防，處理高血糖，並提醒類固醇副作用。

Supportive care comes first. Acute management of brain metastases begins with symptom control. Anti-seizure medication and pain medication may be needed. Dexamethasone is important for edema-related symptoms; the teaching template notes that steroids can improve symptoms in about 75% of patients within 3 days. A common approach is IV dexamethasone 10 mg loading dose, followed by 4 mg every 6 hours, then tapering after tumor-directed treatment begins. When steroids are used, give a proton-pump inhibitor for gastrointestinal prophylaxis, manage hyperglycemia, and counsel about steroid side effects.

全腦放射治療：定義、劑量、臨床角色與證據

WBRT 是照射整個腦部，用於顯性與微小顱內疾病。常見劑量是 30 Gy / 10 次；預後較好且希望降低晚期毒性時可用 35 Gy / 14 次；預後較差或需要短療程可用 20 Gy / 5 次。小細胞肺癌的預防性顱腦照射（PCI）常見劑量是 25 Gy / 10 次。

WBRT 可以單獨使用，也可以作為手術或 SRS 後的輔助治療。單獨 WBRT 適合不適合 SRS 或手術的病人，例如體能狀態不佳、病灶太多、疾病總體積過大，或有軟腦膜疾病。作為輔助治療時，WBRT 可降低手術腔復發、遠端腦內復發與神經學死亡，但代價是神經認知毒性。

WBRT irradiates the entire brain to treat macroscopic and microscopic intracranial disease. The common dose is 30 Gy in 10 fractions. For patients with better prognosis and a desire to reduce late toxicity, 35 Gy in 14 fractions can be used. For poorer prognosis or a need for a short course, 20 Gy in 5 fractions can be used. For prophylactic cranial irradiation in small-cell lung cancer, a common dose is 25 Gy in 10 fractions.

WBRT can be used alone or as an adjunct after surgery or SRS. WBRT alone is appropriate for patients who are not candidates for SRS or surgery, such as those with poor performance status, too many lesions, excessive total disease volume, or leptomeningeal disease. As an adjunct, WBRT reduces surgical cavity recurrence, distant brain failure, and neurologic death, but at the cost of neurocognitive toxicity.

SRS 是高精準、高單次劑量、低正常腦劑量的局部治療。腦部 SRS 使用高度聚焦的光子射束，對一個或多個腦內標的給予比傳統體外放射治療更高的每次劑量，同時保護正常腦實質與 OAR。它通常由神經外科醫師與放射腫瘤科醫師共同參與，雖名為放射手術，但實際上沒有手術切口，屬微侵入性局部治療。SRS／SBRT 通常定義為 1–5 次。

SRS 可治療腦轉移、腦膜瘤、前庭神經鞘瘤、腦下垂體腺瘤、動靜脈畸形、三叉神經痛等；這份投影片聚焦腦轉移。

SRS is a highly precise local therapy with high dose per fraction and low normal brain dose. Brain SRS uses highly focused photon beams to deliver a higher dose per fraction than conventional EBRT to one or more brain targets while protecting normal brain parenchyma and organs at risk. It usually involves both a neurosurgeon and a radiation oncologist. Despite the word radiosurgery, there is no incision; it is a minimally invasive local therapy. SRS/SBRT is usually defined as 1–5 fractions.

SRS can treat brain metastases, meningiomas, vestibular schwannomas, pituitary adenomas, arteriovenous malformations, trigeminal neuralgia, and other conditions; this teaching page focuses on brain metastases.

追蹤、放射性壞死及全腦放療與 SRS 臨床決策

SRS 後需連續腦部磁振造影，每 2–3 個月追蹤；2 年後可逐漸拉長，例如每 4 個月、每 6 個月。最重要的延遲性毒性是放射性壞死。風險因子包括劑量－體積參數與 PTV 邊界、既往放射線暴露、同步全身性治療；大型腫瘤應採分次治療以降低放射性壞死風險。

放射性壞死與復發的影像鑑別可使用連續磁振造影、進展速度、T1／T2 影像模式。T1／T2 不匹配比較偏向放射性壞死，而 T1／T2 匹配比較偏向復發腫瘤。CTCAE v5.0 中樞神經系統壞死分級：第 1 級：無症狀，觀察；第 2 級：中度症狀，需要皮質類固醇；第 3 級：嚴重症狀，需要醫療介入；第 4 級：危及生命，需要緊急介入；第 5 級：死亡。

症狀性放射性壞死的第一線治療是皮質類固醇，例如 dexamethasone 4–8 mg／日並逐漸減量。其他選項包括 bevacizumab（貝伐珠單抗）、pentoxifylline 合併維生素 E；投影片列出 Trental 400 mg + 維生素 E 400 IU 每日兩次 ×6 個月。

After SRS, serial brain MRI is required every 2–3 months; after 2 years, the interval can gradually lengthen, such as every 4 months and then every 6 months. The most important delayed toxicity is radiation necrosis. Risk factors include dose-volume / PTV margin, prior radiation exposure, and concurrent systemic therapy; large tumors should be fractionated to reduce radiation necrosis risk.

Radiation necrosis and recurrence can be differentiated using serial MRIs, tempo of progression, and T1/T2 patterns. T1/T2 mismatch favors radiation necrosis, while T1/T2 match favors recurrent tumor. CTCAE v5.0 CNS necrosis grading: Grade 1 asymptomatic observation; Grade 2 moderate symptoms requiring corticosteroids; Grade 3 severe symptoms requiring medical intervention; Grade 4 life-threatening urgent intervention; Grade 5 death.

First-line treatment for symptomatic radiation necrosis is corticosteroids, such as dexamethasone 4–8 mg/day with gradual taper. Other options include bevacizumab and pentoxifylline + vitamin E; the slides list Trental 400 mg + Vitamin E 400 IU twice daily for 6 months.

腦轉移治療最重要重點整理

  • 腦轉移的治療核心不是「WBRT 對 SRS」二選一，而是根據體能狀態、預期存活期、全身疾病控制、病灶數目、顱內腫瘤總體積、病灶大小、症狀、軟腦膜疾病狀態制定個別化策略。
  • 支持性照護應優先進行，尤其 dexamethasone 對水腫相關症狀很重要。
  • WBRT 的標準劑量是 30 Gy / 10 次；20 Gy / 5 次 適合較差預後，35 Gy / 14 次 可用於較好預後以降低晚期毒性。
  • WBRT 的優點是治療全腦的顯性與微小病灶，改善顱內控制；缺點是 4–6 個月後的神經認知下降，尤其延遲回憶／短期記憶。
  • Patchell 試驗的教學重點是：對單一腦轉移，手術合併 WBRT 可改善總生存期與體能狀態；手術後加入 WBRT 可改善局部及遠端腦內控制並降低神經學死亡，但不改善總生存期。
  • QUARTZ 試驗則提醒，對預後不佳的非小細胞肺癌腦轉移，WBRT 不一定有意義，最佳支持性照護或安寧緩和醫療可能才是正確治療。
  • 若必須給 WBRT 且病人預期活過數月，應考慮 memantine 及／或海馬迴迴避。RTOG 0614 支持 memantine 延緩認知功能下降；RTOG 0933 與 NRG CC001 支持 HA-WBRT，尤其 HA-WBRT + memantine 改善 4–6 個月認知結果。
  • SRS 是適用於總體積有限腦轉移、可保留認知功能的局部治療。傳統單次劑量依 RTOG 90-05：≤2 cm 24 Gy、2–3 cm 18 Gy、3–4 cm 15 Gy。
  • >2 cm 病灶接受單次治療局部控制下降且放射性壞死增加，因此常用 27 Gy / 3 次。
  • 對多發腦轉移，現代重點逐漸從數目轉向腫瘤總體積與正常腦劑量；JLGK0901 支持在經選擇病人中 5–10 顆轉移病灶也可採單獨 SRS。
  • 單獨 SRS 與 WBRT 的取捨很穩定：單獨 SRS 的認知功能較佳，總生存期通常相近，但局部或遠端腦內復發較多；WBRT 則顱內控制較佳，但認知功能較差，通常不改善總生存期。
  • 對體能狀態良好、預期存活期 >4 個月、有限數目腦轉移的病人，現代策略通常優先採用 SRS 並密切進行磁振造影追蹤，而不是常規前置全腦放療。
  • The core of brain metastases treatment is not a binary choice between WBRT and SRS; strategy should be individualized according to performance status, expected survival, systemic disease control, number of lesions, total intracranial tumor volume, lesion size, symptoms, and leptomeningeal disease status.
  • Supportive care comes first, especially dexamethasone for edema-related symptoms.
  • The standard WBRT dose is 30 Gy in 10 fractions. 20 Gy in 5 fractions is appropriate for poorer prognosis, and 35 Gy in 14 fractions can be used for better prognosis to reduce late toxicity.
  • The advantage of WBRT is treatment of macroscopic and microscopic disease throughout the whole brain, improving intracranial control; the disadvantage is neurocognitive decline at 4–6 months, especially delayed recall and short-term memory.
  • The teaching answer from the Patchell trials is: surgery added to WBRT improves overall survival and performance status for solitary metastasis; postoperative WBRT added after surgery improves local and distant brain control and neurologic death, but not overall survival.
  • The QUARTZ trial reminds us that for poor-prognosis NSCLC brain metastases, WBRT is not always meaningful, and best supportive care or hospice can be the correct treatment.
  • If WBRT is required and the patient is expected to live several months, memantine and/or hippocampal avoidance should be considered. RTOG 0614 supports memantine to delay cognitive decline; RTOG 0933 and NRG CC001 support HA-WBRT, especially HA-WBRT plus memantine, for improving 4–6 month cognitive outcomes.
  • SRS is a cognition-sparing local therapy for limited-volume brain metastases. Traditional single-fraction dose follows RTOG 90-05: 24 Gy for ≤2 cm, 18 Gy for 2–3 cm, and 15 Gy for 3–4 cm.
  • For lesions >2 cm, single-fraction local control decreases and radiation necrosis increases; therefore 27 Gy in 3 fractions is commonly used.
  • For multiple brain metastases, modern emphasis has shifted from number to total volume and normal brain dose; JLGK0901 supports SRS alone for 5–10 metastases in selected patients.
  • The trade-off between SRS alone and WBRT is stable: SRS alone has better cognition and generally similar overall survival, but higher local and distant brain failure; WBRT has better intracranial control, worse cognition, and usually no overall-survival improvement.
  • For good-performance-status patients with expected survival >4 months and limited brain metastases, modern strategy usually favors SRS with close MRI surveillance rather than routine upfront WBRT.

Results: No change in OS: 15.7 vs. 16.1 mos PFS longer with bev: 10.7 mos vs. 7.3 mos •Adverse effects higher in bev group: hypertension, thromboembolism, intenstinal perf, neutropenia, decline in QOL and cognitive function •Those with Pro-B type (indicative of proneural) had improved PFS, but not OS.
