膀胱總論

膀胱癌總論、流行病學與 NMIBC / MIBC 分界

膀胱癌是第二常見的 genitourinary malignancy，診斷年齡中位數約 70 歲，約 76% 為男性，發生率在 North America / Western Europe 較高。約 90% 是 urothelial carcinoma。約 70% 病人初診時屬於 superficial disease，也就是 stage I / NMIBC，通常以 TURBT ± intravesical therapy 處理；MIBC 則以 radical cystectomy + perioperative systemic therapy，或 bladder preservation with chemoRT 治療。

  • 美國癌症比例：投影片圖表顯示 bladder cancer 約占美國新癌症的 4.2%。
  • 5 年 relative survival：in situ 97.2%、localized 71.7%、regional 39.5%、distant 8.8%。
  • 最重要臨床分界：NMIBC vs MIBC。NMIBC 包括 Ta、Tis、T1，尚未侵犯 muscularis propria；MIBC 從 T2 開始，代表侵犯 detrusor muscle。
  • 治療邏輯：NMIBC 主要是內視鏡切除與膀胱內灌注治療；MIBC 則進入根除性手術、全身治療或器官保留 chemoRT 的決策。

Bladder cancer is the second most common genitourinary malignancy. The median age at diagnosis is about 70 years, about 76% of patients are male, and incidence is higher in North America and Western Europe. About 90% are urothelial carcinoma. About 70% present with superficial disease, or stage I / NMIBC, usually managed with TURBT ± intravesical therapy. MIBC is managed with radical cystectomy plus perioperative systemic therapy, or bladder preservation with chemoradiotherapy.

  • United States cancer burden: the slide figure lists bladder cancer as about 4.2% of new cancers in the United States.
  • Five-year relative survival: in situ 97.2%, localized 71.7%, regional 39.5%, and distant 8.8%.
  • Key clinical distinction: NMIBC versus MIBC. NMIBC includes Ta, Tis, and T1 disease without invasion of the muscularis propria; MIBC begins at T2, indicating invasion of the detrusor muscle.
  • Treatment logic: NMIBC is mainly managed with endoscopic resection and intravesical therapy, whereas MIBC requires decisions between radical surgery, systemic therapy, and organ-preserving chemoradiotherapy.
表現／風險

臨床表現與危險因子

最典型表現是 hematuria，可以是 gross hematuria 或 microscopic hematuria。投影片指出 gross hematuria 約有 10–20% 風險與 bladder tumor 相關。其他表現包括 obstructive 或 irritative bladder symptoms，例如頻尿、急尿、排尿疼痛、排尿不完全感；若為 locally advanced disease，可能出現 pelvic pain 或 back pain。

解剖／Workup

膀胱解剖、壁層、淋巴引流與診斷分期檢查

膀胱可分為三個解剖區域：body 位於 ureteral orifices 以上；trigone 位於兩側 ureteral openings 與 bladder neck 之間；neck 是膀胱最下方區域。膀胱由 urachus 固定於 anterior abdominal wall。主要淋巴引流包括 external iliac、internal iliac、obturator、perivesical、presacral nodes。

  • Urine cytology：sensitivity 低，約 34%；specificity 高，>98%。陰性不能排除癌症，但陽性很有意義。
  • Office cystoscopy：可在無麻醉下進行；真正取得病理與分期依賴 EUA + cystoscopy with maximal TURBT。

The bladder can be divided into three anatomic regions: the body above the ureteral orifices; the trigone between the ureteral openings and bladder neck; and the neck, the most inferior region. The bladder is attached to the anterior abdominal wall by the urachus. Major lymphatic drainage includes the external iliac, internal iliac, obturator, perivesical, and presacral nodes.

NMIBC

NMIBC risk-adapted management 與手術角色

所有病人一開始都需要 TURBT。後續依風險分層決定 observation、intravesical chemotherapy、BCG，或 early cystectomy。

NMIBC 風險分層與後續治療

Intermediate riskLow-grade T1、>3 cm、multifocal、1 年內 recurrence；或 high-grade Ta、≤3 cm、solitaryIntravesical therapy：CHT vs BCG
High riskHigh-grade CIS、T1、>3 cm、multifocalBCG preferred

  • 臨床重點：NMIBC 並不等於 low-risk。尤其 high-grade T1、CIS、BCG-unresponsive disease、variant histology、LVSI 或 prostatic urethral involvement，行為可能很 aggressive，應考慮 early radical cystectomy。
  • TURBT：所有病人的 initial diagnostic procedure，也可治療 CIS、Ta、T1 primary tumors。
  • Partial cystectomy：可用於非常選擇性的 cT2 MIBC，例如 solitary lesion、位置適合 segmental resection 且可取得 adequate margins；也可用於 bladder diverticulum cancer，但需同時做 bilateral pelvic lymphadenectomy。
  • Radical cystectomy in NMIBC：適用於 residual high-grade cT1、aggressive histopathologic subtypes、LVSI、concomitant CIS、BCG-unresponsive disease，且同樣需 bilateral pelvic lymphadenectomy，包括 obturator、external iliac、internal iliac、common iliac nodes。

All patients initially require TURBT. Subsequent management depends on risk stratification and may include observation, intravesical chemotherapy, BCG, or early cystectomy.

  • Partial cystectomy: can be used in highly selected cT2 MIBC, such as a solitary lesion in a location suitable for segmental resection with adequate margins; it can also be used for bladder diverticulum cancer, but must include bilateral pelvic lymphadenectomy.

MIBC

MIBC 治療總架構、systemic therapy、radical cystectomy 與 urinary diversion

MIBC 一開始也需要 TURBT。若病人是 surgical candidate，現代標準逐漸轉向 perioperative chemoIO + radical cystectomy；若病人不適合手術或拒絕手術，則可選擇 bladder preservation with maximal safe TURBT + chemoRT。

  • 歷史 systemic therapy 標準：neoadjuvant cisplatin-based chemotherapy，例如 DD-MVAC 或 gemcitabine/cisplatin，術前給予後再做 radical cystectomy。
  • 若術前未接受 systemic therapy：可考慮 adjuvant DD-MVAC 3–6 cycles 或 gemcitabine/cisplatin 4 cycles。
  • NIAGARA trial 新標準：gemcitabine/cisplatin + durvalumab ×4 cycles q3 weeks → radical cystectomy → adjuvant durvalumab q4 weeks ×8 cycles。
  • NIAGARA 結果：加入 durvalumab 改善 2-year EFS，68% vs 60%，也改善 2-year OS，82% vs 75%，且 grade 3–4 toxicity 約 40%，兩組沒有顯著差異。
  • Radical cystectomy 切除範圍：移除 bladder、peritoneal covering、urachus、perivesical fat、lower ureters。男性通常還包括 proximal urethra、prostate、seminal vesicles、vas deferens；女性則包括 entire urethra、uterus、fallopian tubes、ovaries、cervix、vaginal cuff。
  • PLND：所有病人都應做 bilateral PLND，至少包含 obturator、external iliac、internal iliac、common iliac nodes；SWOG 8710 中移除至少 10 nodes 與較佳 OS 相關。

膀胱保留

Selective bladder preservation：候選人、response assessment、chemoRT 劑量與 volume

Selective bladder preservation, SBP 的標準三步驟是 maximal safe TURBT → chemoRT → response assessment / surveillance。理想候選人包括 unifocal tumor <5 cm after complete TURBT、no associated CIS、cT2–T3 或 selected T4a、cN0、adequate bladder function、無 hydronephrosis、無 IBD、無 prior pelvic RT，且能配合嚴格 surveillance。若 unilateral hydronephrosis，投影片指出可在 ureteral stenting 後考慮 SBP。

  • SBP 後 2–3 個月 re-assess：若 complete response 則 surveillance；若 T1/Ta/CIS 可 TURBT；若 T2 residual disease，則建議 salvage cystectomy。
  • Definitive chemoRT 前：必須先做 maximal safe TURBT。
  • Concurrent chemotherapy：常用 5-FU + MMC、cisplatin alone、low-dose gemcitabine。
  • 5-FU/MMC regimen：5-FU 500 mg/m² days 1–5 and 16–20，加 MMC 12 mg/m² day 1。
  • 放療劑量：64 Gy/32 Fx 或 55 Gy/20 Fx。
  • Volume：通常照 whole bladder；partial bladder boost 可考慮用於達成 bowel constraints，但 BC2001 顯示 whole bladder RT 與 partial bladder boost 在 outcome 和 toxicity 上沒有明顯差異。
  • Elective nodal irradiation：具爭議，institution-dependent；若 high-risk，例如 small cell，或 cN+，可考慮。
  • Small pelvis：包含 distal internal/external iliac、presacral、perivesical、obturator nodes；若 N+，可加 common iliacs 並 boost gross nodes，視 bowel tolerance 調整。
  • Interim cystoscopy：對 operable patients，可考慮在 conventionally fractionated RT 至 40–45 Gy 後做 restaging cystoscopy：若 cT0/Ta/Tis，繼續 boost 至 64 Gy；若 ≥T1，考慮 salvage cystectomy。非手術候選人通常不需要 interim cystoscopy。
  • Locally advanced / metastatic first-line：enfortumab vedotin + pembrolizumab q3 weeks。EV 是 directed against nectin-4 的 antibody–drug conjugate；pembrolizumab 是 PD-1 inhibitor。若為 locally advanced disease，可在 2–3 cycles 後評估 response，再重新考慮 definitive local treatment。

Selective bladder preservation, SBP, consists of three standard steps: maximal safe TURBT → chemoradiotherapy → response assessment / surveillance. Ideal candidates include unifocal tumor <5 cm after complete TURBT, no associated CIS, cT2–T3 or selected T4a disease, cN0, adequate bladder function, no hydronephrosis, no inflammatory bowel disease, no prior pelvic radiotherapy, and ability to comply with strict surveillance. If unilateral hydronephrosis is present, the slide notes that SBP can be considered after ureteral stenting.

  • Reassess 2–3 months after SBP: if complete response, proceed with surveillance; if T1/Ta/CIS, TURBT can be performed; if T2 residual disease, salvage cystectomy is recommended.
  • Before definitive chemoradiotherapy: maximal safe TURBT is required.
  • Concurrent chemotherapy: common options include 5-FU + mitomycin-C, cisplatin alone, and low-dose gemcitabine.
  • 5-FU/MMC regimen: 5-FU 500 mg/m² on days 1–5 and 16–20 plus mitomycin-C 12 mg/m² on day 1.
  • Radiotherapy dose: 64 Gy in 32 fractions or 55 Gy in 20 fractions.
  • Volume: usually whole bladder; partial bladder boost can be considered to meet bowel constraints, but BC2001 showed no clear outcome or toxicity difference between whole bladder RT and partial bladder boost.
  • Elective nodal irradiation: controversial and institution-dependent; consider for high-risk disease such as small-cell carcinoma or cN+ disease.
  • Small pelvis: includes distal internal/external iliac, presacral, perivesical, and obturator nodes. If N+, add common iliac coverage and boost gross nodes as bowel tolerance allows.
  • Interim cystoscopy: in operable patients, consider restaging cystoscopy after conventionally fractionated RT to 40–45 Gy. If cT0/Ta/Tis, continue boost to 64 Gy; if ≥T1, consider salvage cystectomy. Non-surgical candidates usually do not need interim cystoscopy.
  • Locally advanced / metastatic first-line: enfortumab vedotin + pembrolizumab every 3 weeks. EV is an antibody–drug conjugate directed against nectin-4; pembrolizumab is a PD-1 inhibitor. In locally advanced disease, response can be assessed after 2–3 cycles and definitive local treatment reconsidered.
膀胱證據

膀胱癌證據：術後放療、RTOG pooled analysis、BC2001、RTOG 0712

  • 術後 RT 歷史毒性：Reisinger 1992 中部分病人接受 cystectomy 前 5 Gy ×1，術後 high-risk 再給 whole pelvis 40–45 Gy。5 年 OS 約 67%，但 RT 組 bowel obstruction 37%，非 RT 組 8%，顯示舊時代 postoperative RT 毒性不可接受。
  • 術後復發風險因子：SWOG 8710 確立 T3–T4 disease、N+ disease、positive surgical margin。
  • Christodouleas model：≤pT2 為 low-risk，LRF 約 8%；≥pT3 且 ≥10 nodes sampled 且 negative margins 為 intermediate-risk，LRF 約 19–20%；≥pT3 且 <10 nodes sampled 或 positive margins 為 high-risk，LRF 約 41%。
  • NRG GU-001：pT3–T4 pN0–2 urothelial bladder cancer radical cystectomy 後，randomized no RT vs postoperative IMRT 50.4 Gy/28 Fx；primary endpoint pelvic recurrence-free survival；因收案不佳提早終止。
  • Egyptian phase II/III RCT：radical cystectomy 後至少一個 risk factor，包括 stage ≥pT3b、grade 3、LVSI 或 pN+；ART 組 50 Gy/25 Fx IMRT 至 cystectomy bed + pelvic nodes。3-year LRFS 81% vs 71%，p=0.045；DMFS/OS 無顯著差異；late GU/GI toxicity 無顯著差異。
  • BART trial：urothelial MIBC radical cystectomy 後 ≥1 risk factor；ART 50.4 Gy/28 Fx 至 cystectomy bed + pelvic LNs，IMRT + daily CBCT。主要報告 toxicity：acute grade 3+ toxicity 4.2% vs 1.6%，late grade 2+ toxicity 23.3% vs 17.5%，未見顯著增加；2-year LRFS pending。
  • 術後 RT 解讀：post-cystectomy ART 在舊技術下毒性高，但 IMRT 時代 toxicity 可接受；randomized efficacy evidence 仍有限，且現代 chemoIO 標準已改變。
  • RTOG pooled analysis：Mak et al. JCO 2014 整合 6 個 RTOG bladder preservation studies，共 468 位 MIBC 病人。5 年 OS 57%、DSS 71%、muscle-invasive local failure 13%、non–muscle-invasive local failure 31%、distant metastasis 31%；10 年 OS 36%、DSS 65%。Late grade 3+ GU toxicity 6%，GI toxicity 2%。
  • RTOG pooled conclusion：long-term results comparable to radical cystectomy，definitive chemoRT 是可行替代方案，尤其適合 elderly 或 poor KPS patients。
  • RTOG 8903：T2–T4a NX M0 bladder cancer，2 cycles MCV → 39.6 Gy concurrent cisplatin vs 無 neoadjuvant therapy 直接 39.6 Gy concurrent cisplatin。5 年 OS 48% vs 49%，distant metastasis 33% vs 39%，5 年 cystectomy-free survival 36% vs 40%，均無顯著改善。
  • BC2001：T2–T4a N0 M0，2×2 factorial design 比較 RT alone vs RT + concurrent 5-FU/MMC，以及 whole bladder RT vs partial bladder boost。RT 可用 64 Gy/32 Fx 或 55 Gy/20 Fx；5-FU 500 mg/m² fractions 1–5 and 16–20，MMC 12 mg/m² day 1。
  • BC2001 outcome：concurrent CHT 改善 2-year LRFS，67% vs 54%，p=0.03；5-year cystectomy rate 15% vs 22%，p=0.034。Whole bladder vs partial boost 無顯著 LRFS 差異，也沒有降低 grade 3–4 acute 或 late toxicity。
  • BC2001 結論：RT 加 concurrent 5-FU/MMC 是標準 bladder preservation regimen；partial bladder boost 不會明顯改善 outcome 或 toxicity。
  • RTOG 0712：phase II，70 位 MIBC，FCT arm 使用 5-FU/cisplatin + twice-daily RT，總劑量 pelvis 44.8 Gy、bladder 52.3 Gy、tumor 64.3 Gy；GD arm 使用 gemcitabine + once-daily RT，總劑量 pelvis 44 Gy、bladder 52 Gy、tumor 64 Gy。
  • RTOG 0712 outcome：3-year DMFS 78% with FCT、84% with GD；bladder-intact DMFS 67% vs 72%。此試驗不是直接比較兩組，而是顯示 5-FU/cisplatin 與 gemcitabine 都可作 concurrent chemoRT option。
  • Historical postoperative RT toxicity: in Reisinger 1992, some patients received 5 Gy ×1 before cystectomy and high-risk patients received postoperative whole pelvis 40–45 Gy. Five-year OS was about 67%, but bowel obstruction was 37% in the RT group versus 8% without RT, showing that historical postoperative RT toxicity was unacceptable.
  • Postoperative recurrence risk factors: SWOG 8710 established T3–T4 disease, node-positive disease, and positive surgical margin as risk factors.
  • Christodouleas model: ≤pT2 was low-risk with LRF about 8%; ≥pT3 with ≥10 nodes sampled and negative margins was intermediate-risk with LRF about 19–20%; ≥pT3 with <10 nodes sampled or positive margins was high-risk with LRF about 41%.
  • NRG GU-001: after radical cystectomy for pT3–T4 pN0–2 urothelial bladder cancer, patients were randomized to no RT versus postoperative IMRT 50.4 Gy in 28 fractions; primary endpoint was pelvic recurrence-free survival; the trial closed early due to poor accrual.
  • Egyptian phase II/III RCT: after radical cystectomy with at least one risk factor, including stage ≥pT3b, grade 3, LVSI, or pN+, the ART arm received 50 Gy in 25 fractions by IMRT to cystectomy bed plus pelvic nodes. Three-year LRFS was 81% vs 71%, p=0.045; DMFS and OS were not significantly different; late GU/GI toxicity was not significantly different.
  • BART trial: urothelial MIBC after radical cystectomy with ≥1 risk factor; ART was 50.4 Gy in 28 fractions to cystectomy bed plus pelvic LNs with IMRT and daily CBCT. Main report was toxicity: acute grade 3+ toxicity 4.2% vs 1.6%, late grade 2+ toxicity 23.3% vs 17.5%, without significant increase; 2-year LRFS remains pending.
  • Postoperative RT interpretation: post-cystectomy ART was toxic with older techniques, but toxicity appears acceptable in the IMRT era. Randomized efficacy evidence remains limited, and modern chemoimmunotherapy standards have changed.
  • RTOG pooled analysis: Mak et al. JCO 2014 integrated 6 RTOG bladder-preservation studies, with 468 MIBC patients. Five-year OS 57%, DSS 71%, muscle-invasive local failure 13%, non–muscle-invasive local failure 31%, distant metastasis 31%; 10-year OS 36%, DSS 65%. Late grade 3+ GU toxicity was 6% and GI toxicity 2%.
  • RTOG pooled conclusion: long-term results were comparable to radical cystectomy, and definitive chemoradiotherapy is a feasible alternative, especially for elderly or poor-KPS patients.
  • RTOG 8903: T2–T4a NX M0 bladder cancer, 2 cycles of MCV → 39.6 Gy with concurrent cisplatin versus no neoadjuvant therapy and direct 39.6 Gy with concurrent cisplatin. Five-year OS 48% vs 49%, distant metastasis 33% vs 39%, and 5-year cystectomy-free survival 36% vs 40%, with no significant improvement.
  • BC2001: T2–T4a N0 M0, 2×2 factorial design comparing RT alone versus RT + concurrent 5-FU/MMC, and whole bladder RT versus partial bladder boost. RT was 64 Gy in 32 fractions or 55 Gy in 20 fractions; 5-FU 500 mg/m² on fractions 1–5 and 16–20, and MMC 12 mg/m² day 1.
  • BC2001 outcome: concurrent chemotherapy improved 2-year LRFS, 67% vs 54%, p=0.03; 5-year cystectomy rate 15% vs 22%, p=0.034. Whole bladder versus partial boost showed no significant LRFS difference and did not reduce grade 3–4 acute or late toxicity.
  • BC2001 conclusion: RT plus concurrent 5-FU/MMC is a standard bladder-preservation regimen; partial bladder boost does not clearly improve outcome or toxicity.
  • RTOG 0712: phase II, 70 MIBC patients. The FCT arm used 5-FU/cisplatin plus twice-daily RT, with total dose pelvis 44.8 Gy, bladder 52.3 Gy, tumor 64.3 Gy. The GD arm used gemcitabine plus once-daily RT, with total dose pelvis 44 Gy, bladder 52 Gy, tumor 64 Gy.
  • RTOG 0712 outcome: 3-year DMFS was 78% with FCT and 84% with GD; bladder-intact DMFS was 67% vs 72%. The study was not designed to directly compare arms, but showed that both 5-FU/cisplatin and gemcitabine can be concurrent chemoradiotherapy options.
Seminoma 總論

睪丸精原細胞瘤總論、表現、危險因子與 workup

睪丸癌約占男性癌症 1%，最常見於 15–34 歲。約 95% 睪丸癌是 germ cell tumors，其中 50–60% 是 seminoma，40–50% 是 NSGCT。NSGCT 包括 embryonal carcinoma、teratoma、choriocarcinoma、yolk sac tumor。Seminoma 多見於 30–40 歲，約 85% 初診為 stage I disease。整體預後極佳，10-year OS >95%。60 歲以上若出現 testicular mass，testicular lymphoma 反而最常見。

  • 放療敏感性：Seminoma 高度 radiosensitive，因此 RT 在 stage I–II seminoma 仍有角色；NSGCT 則 RT 只在 salvage / palliation 中有角色。
  • 臨床表現：painless testicular mass、testicular swelling、scrotal fullness、dull ache 或 heaviness，可放射到 lower abdomen 或 perianal area。
  • 鑑別診斷：testicular torsion、epididymitis、hydrocele、varicocele、hernia、hematoma、spermatocele。
  • 理學檢查：重點是 scrotal examination；firm 或 fixed mass 應視為 cancer until proven otherwise。也要 palpate abdomen 評估 nodal disease 或 visceral involvement，檢查 chest 是否有 gynecomastia，並 palpate SCV nodes。
  • 最重要危險因子：cryptorchidism / undescended testis。腹腔內 undescended testis 風險約 1/20，即 5%；inguinal undescended testis 風險約 1/80，即 1.3%。應在青春期前做 orchipexy；越晚發現或矯正，癌症風險越高。
  • Contralateral risk：有 cryptorchidism history 的 GCT 病人中，約 20% 發生在 contralateral normally descended testicle。
  • Carcinoma in situ：untreated carcinoma in situ of testis 在 5 年內約有 50% 進展為 invasive malignancy。
  • 其他危險因子：hypospadias、androgen insensitivity syndrome、gonadal dysgenesis、previous contralateral testicular cancer、extragonadal GCT、family history、white race、HIV、marijuana use、Peutz–Jeghers syndrome。
  • Labs：CBC、CMP 與 serum tumor markers：AFP、β-hCG、LDH。術後 tumor markers 用於 staging，因此 orchiectomy 後要重測。
  • Marker interpretation：AFP 升高通常代表 NSGCT 或 mixed NSGCT component；seminoma 幾乎不會使 AFP 升高。β-hCG 可見於 seminoma，但更常提示 NSGCT。
  • Imaging：bilateral scrotal Doppler ultrasound，典型為 hypoechoic mass；分期需 CXR / CT chest、CT abdomen/pelvis。PET/CT utility limited；若有症狀或 β-hCG 很高，可考慮 brain imaging。
  • 取得病理：radical inguinal orchiectomy with high ligation of spermatic cord。
  • 禁忌：trans-scrotal biopsy 或 trans-scrotal orchiectomy 是絕對禁忌，因為會造成 scrotal seeding、lymphatic disruption，並增加 inguinal nodal spread 風險。
  • Fertility：治療前應做 fertility counseling，提供 semen analysis 與 sperm banking。

Testicular cancer accounts for about 1% of male cancers and is most common from ages 15–34. About 95% are germ-cell tumors; 50–60% are seminoma and 40–50% are NSGCT. NSGCT includes embryonal carcinoma, teratoma, choriocarcinoma, and yolk-sac tumor. Seminoma is most common at ages 30–40, and about 85% present as stage I disease. Overall prognosis is excellent, with 10-year OS >95%. In patients older than 60 with a testicular mass, testicular lymphoma is more common.

  • Radiosensitivity: seminoma is highly radiosensitive, so RT still has a role in stage I–II seminoma; for NSGCT, RT is mainly used for salvage or palliation.
  • Presentation: painless testicular mass, testicular swelling, scrotal fullness, dull ache, or heaviness, sometimes radiating to lower abdomen or perianal area.
  • Differential diagnosis: testicular torsion, epididymitis, hydrocele, varicocele, hernia, hematoma, spermatocele.
  • Physical exam: focus on scrotal examination; a firm or fixed mass should be treated as cancer until proven otherwise. Palpate abdomen for nodal or visceral disease, examine chest for gynecomastia, and palpate supraclavicular nodes.
  • Most important risk factor: cryptorchidism / undescended testis. Intra-abdominal undescended testis carries about 1/20 risk, or 5%; inguinal undescended testis about 1/80, or 1.3%. Orchipexy should be done before puberty; later detection or correction increases cancer risk.
  • Contralateral risk: among GCT patients with cryptorchidism history, about 20% occur in the contralateral normally descended testicle.
  • Carcinoma in situ: untreated carcinoma in situ of testis progresses to invasive malignancy in about 50% within 5 years.
  • Other risk factors: hypospadias, androgen insensitivity syndrome, gonadal dysgenesis, prior contralateral testicular cancer, extragonadal GCT, family history, white race, HIV, marijuana use, and Peutz–Jeghers syndrome.
  • Labs: CBC, CMP, and serum tumor markers AFP, β-hCG, LDH. Postoperative tumor markers are used for staging, so markers should be repeated after orchiectomy.
  • Marker interpretation: elevated AFP usually indicates NSGCT or a mixed NSGCT component; seminoma almost never elevates AFP. β-hCG can be seen in seminoma but more often suggests NSGCT.
  • Imaging: bilateral scrotal Doppler ultrasound, typically showing a hypoechoic mass; staging requires CXR / CT chest and CT abdomen/pelvis. PET/CT has limited utility; brain imaging can be considered for symptoms or very high β-hCG.
  • Pathologic diagnosis: radical inguinal orchiectomy with high ligation of the spermatic cord.
  • Contraindication: trans-scrotal biopsy or trans-scrotal orchiectomy is absolutely contraindicated because it can cause scrotal seeding, lymphatic disruption, and increased risk of inguinal nodal spread.
  • Fertility: fertility counseling should be done before treatment, with semen analysis and sperm banking offered.

睪丸淋巴血管解剖、病理與 staging

  • 血管解剖：testicular arteries 直接來自 abdominal aorta；right testicular vein 匯入 IVC，位於 right renal vein 下方；left testicular vein 匯入 left renal vein。

Seminoma 證據

Seminoma 證據：Stage I surveillance、MRC TE10/18/19、Stage II RT 與 de-escalation

  • Stage I surveillance：Toronto systematic review 顯示 stage I seminoma orchiectomy 後 surveillance，約 17% relapse，多數在第一年；9% relapse 發生在 >2 年後。
  • Danish cohort：1964 位病人中 369 位 relapse，約 18.8%；median time to relapse 13.7 months；73.4% 在前 2 年 relapse，22.2% 在第 3–5 年 relapse，4.3% 在 5 年後 relapse；15-year DSS 99.3%，OS 91.6%。
  • Sunnybrook study：88 位 stage I seminoma orchiectomy 後 surveillance；10 年與 15 年 actuarial relapse-free rate 皆約 80%。17 位 relapse 中 15 位在 diaphragm 以下；salvage 很有效。
  • Stage I conclusion：active surveillance relapse 約 15–20%，但因 salvage therapy 高效，DSS 幾乎接近 100%。
  • MRC TE10：478 位 stage I seminoma，dogleg field 30 Gy/15 Fx vs PAS 30 Gy/15 Fx。3-year RFS 96.6% vs 96.0%，3-year OS 100% vs 99.3%，無顯著差異。PAS 組 pelvic relapse 約 2%，但急性 GI/heme/gonadal toxicity 較低，azoospermia 11% vs 35%。結論：Stage I RT 用 PAS，不需 routine dogleg。
  • MRC TE18：625 位 stage I seminoma，30 Gy/15 Fx vs 20 Gy/10 Fx to PAS。2-year RFS 97.7% vs 97.0%，無顯著差異；20 Gy 組急性 fatigue、lethargy 與 inability to conduct normal work 較少。結論：20 Gy/10 Fx noninferior to 30 Gy/15 Fx，且急性毒性較低。
  • MRC TE19：1477 位 stage I seminoma，carboplatin AUC 7 ×2 cycles q3 weeks vs RT。2-year RFS 98% vs 97%，5-year RFS 95% vs 96%，無顯著差異。Carboplatin 有較多 para-aortic relapses，但 pelvic / mediastinal / SCV relapses 較少；急性 dyspepsia、lethargy、inability to work 較少，但 thrombocytopenia 較多。Contralateral GCT 較少，2% vs 15%。
  • TE10/TE18/TE19 pooled analysis：3049 位病人 overall case-specific survival 99.8%；98 relapses，只有 4 個 relapse 發生在 3 年後。失敗模式依治療不同：dogleg RT 後較多 mediastinum/neck failure；PAS RT 後較多 pelvis 與 mediastinum/neck failure；carboplatin 後 67% failure 在 retroperitoneum。
  • German Classen study：94 位 stage IIA/B seminoma，nodes <5 cm，modified dogleg field。Stage IIA 30 Gy/15 Fx，Stage IIB 36 Gy/18 Fx。6-year RFS：Stage IIA 95%，Stage IIB 89%；DSS 100%。
  • Stage II treatment：Stage IIA/B seminoma 可安全使用 reduced-field modified dogleg RT；Spanish prospective observational study 使用 EP ×4 或 BEP ×3 治療 stage IIA/B seminoma，5-year PFS 和 OS 良好。若 nodes >3 cm，prefer chemotherapy。
  • SAKK 01/10：single-arm phase II，Stage IIA/B seminoma 使用 carboplatin AUC 7 ×1 cycle → involved node RT 30–36 Gy。目標 3-year PFS 95% 未達成，實際 3-year PFS 93.7%，但 grade 3–4 toxicity 低。
  • SAKK 01/18：ongoing。Stage IIA 或 surveillance 後 recurrent IIA：carboplatin AUC 7 ×1 → 24 Gy/12 Fx involved nodal RT。Stage IIB 或 recurrent IIB：1 cycle EP → 30 Gy/15 Fx involved nodal RT。
  • SEMS：phase II，follow-up 仍短，將 RPLND 作為 Stage II seminoma 的選項之一。現階段仍不能取代成熟標準，但代表 seminoma de-escalation 與 surgery-based strategy 正在被重新探索。
  • Stage I surveillance: Toronto systematic review showed about 17% relapse after orchiectomy and surveillance for stage I seminoma, mostly in the first year; 9% of relapses occurred after 2 years.
  • Danish cohort: among 1964 patients, 369 relapsed, about 18.8%; median time to relapse 13.7 months; 73.4% relapsed in the first 2 years, 22.2% in years 3–5, and 4.3% after 5 years; 15-year DSS 99.3%, OS 91.6%.
  • Sunnybrook study: 88 patients with stage I seminoma after orchiectomy underwent surveillance; 10- and 15-year actuarial relapse-free rates were about 80%. Among 17 relapses, 15 were below the diaphragm; salvage was highly effective.
  • Stage I conclusion: active surveillance relapse risk is about 15–20%, but disease-specific survival is nearly 100% because salvage therapy is highly effective.
  • MRC TE10: 478 patients with stage I seminoma, dogleg field 30 Gy/15 fractions vs PAS 30 Gy/15 fractions. Three-year RFS 96.6% vs 96.0%, 3-year OS 100% vs 99.3%, no significant difference. PAS had about 2% pelvic relapse but lower acute GI/hematologic/gonadal toxicity and less azoospermia, 11% vs 35%. Conclusion: for stage I RT, use PAS rather than routine dogleg.
  • MRC TE18: 625 patients with stage I seminoma, 30 Gy/15 fractions vs 20 Gy/10 fractions to PAS. Two-year RFS 97.7% vs 97.0%, no significant difference; 20 Gy caused less acute fatigue, lethargy, and inability to conduct normal work. Conclusion: 20 Gy/10 fractions is noninferior to 30 Gy/15 fractions and has less acute toxicity.
  • MRC TE19: 1477 patients with stage I seminoma, carboplatin AUC 7 ×2 cycles every 3 weeks vs RT. Two-year RFS 98% vs 97%, 5-year RFS 95% vs 96%, no significant difference. Carboplatin had more para-aortic relapses but fewer pelvic / mediastinal / SCV relapses; less acute dyspepsia, lethargy, and inability to work, but more thrombocytopenia. Contralateral GCT was lower, 2% vs 15%.
  • TE10/TE18/TE19 pooled analysis: 3049 patients had overall case-specific survival 99.8%; there were 98 relapses, with only 4 relapses after 3 years. Failure patterns differed by therapy: dogleg RT had more mediastinum/neck failure; PAS RT had more pelvic and mediastinum/neck failure; carboplatin had 67% failure in the retroperitoneum.
  • German Classen study: 94 stage IIA/B seminoma patients, nodes <5 cm, treated with modified dogleg field. Stage IIA received 30 Gy/15 fractions; stage IIB received 36 Gy/18 fractions. Six-year RFS: stage IIA 95%, stage IIB 89%; DSS 100%.
  • Stage II treatment: stage IIA/B seminoma can be safely treated with reduced-field modified dogleg RT. A Spanish prospective observational study using EP ×4 or BEP ×3 for stage IIA/B seminoma showed good 5-year PFS and OS. If nodes are >3 cm, chemotherapy is preferred.
  • SAKK 01/10: single-arm phase II, stage IIA/B seminoma using carboplatin AUC 7 ×1 cycle → involved-node RT 30–36 Gy. Target 3-year PFS of 95% was not met; actual 3-year PFS 93.7%, with low grade 3–4 toxicity.
  • SAKK 01/18: ongoing. Stage IIA or recurrent IIA after surveillance: carboplatin AUC 7 ×1 → 24 Gy/12 fractions involved-nodal RT. Stage IIB or recurrent IIB: 1 cycle EP → 30 Gy/15 fractions involved-nodal RT.
  • SEMS: phase II with short follow-up; explores RPLND as an option for stage II seminoma. It does not yet replace mature standards, but reflects ongoing seminoma de-escalation and renewed interest in surgery-based strategies.

High-yield take-home messages

  • 膀胱癌的第一個核心分界是 NMIBC vs MIBC。NMIBC 以 TURBT + risk-adapted intravesical therapy 為主；MIBC 則在 radical cystectomy + perioperative systemic therapy 與 selective bladder preservation chemoRT 之間選擇。
  • TURBT 品質非常重要：specimen 要包含 muscle，high-grade、T1、多發、大於 3 cm 或切除不完全者常需 repeat TURBT。若要做 bladder preservation，必須先做到 maximal safe / visually complete TURBT。
  • MIBC systemic therapy 正在改變：過去是 cisplatin-based neoadjuvant chemotherapy；NIAGARA 後，gem/cis + durvalumab → cystectomy → adjuvant durvalumab 成為重要新標準。
  • Bladder preservation 證據：核心來自 RTOG pooled analysis、BC2001、RTOG 0712。BC2001 支持 RT 加 concurrent 5-FU/MMC 可改善 LRFS 與 cystectomy-free survival；RTOG 0712 支持 5-FU/cisplatin 或 gemcitabine 皆可作為 concurrent option。
  • Seminoma 第一原則：先做 radical inguinal orchiectomy with high ligation，不做 trans-scrotal biopsy。術後 AFP、β-hCG、LDH 用於 staging；AFP 升高幾乎代表 NSGCT component，不能當純 seminoma 處理。
  • Stage I seminoma：preferred strategy 是 active surveillance，因為約 80–85% 不會復發，即使復發 salvage therapy 也非常有效。若選 RT，MRC TE10 支持 PAS 而非 dogleg，MRC TE18 支持 20 Gy/10 Fx 而非 30 Gy/15 Fx；MRC TE19 支持 carboplatin AUC 7 作為 RT 替代。
  • Stage II seminoma：IIA 可用 modified dogleg RT；IIB 尤其 bulky 或 >3 cm，通常偏向 EP ×4 或 BEP ×3。整體治療趨勢是 de-escalation：降低 chemo intensity、縮小 RT field、降低 RT dose，並探索 RPLND，但標準治療仍需依 stage 與 disease bulk 選擇。
  • The first core distinction in bladder cancer is NMIBC versus MIBC. NMIBC is mainly managed with TURBT plus risk-adapted intravesical therapy; MIBC requires choosing between radical cystectomy plus perioperative systemic therapy and selective bladder-preservation chemoradiotherapy.
  • TURBT quality is critical: the specimen must contain muscle. High-grade disease, T1 disease, multifocal disease, tumors larger than 3 cm, or incomplete resection often require repeat TURBT. If bladder preservation is planned, maximal safe / visually complete TURBT is required first.
  • MIBC systemic therapy is changing: the historical standard was cisplatin-based neoadjuvant chemotherapy; after NIAGARA, gemcitabine/cisplatin + durvalumab → cystectomy → adjuvant durvalumab has become an important new standard.
  • Bladder-preservation evidence: core evidence comes from RTOG pooled analysis, BC2001, and RTOG 0712. BC2001 supports RT plus concurrent 5-FU/MMC to improve LRFS and cystectomy-free survival; RTOG 0712 supports either 5-FU/cisplatin or gemcitabine as concurrent options.
  • Seminoma first principle: perform radical inguinal orchiectomy with high ligation and avoid trans-scrotal biopsy. Postoperative AFP, β-hCG, and LDH are used for staging; elevated AFP almost always indicates an NSGCT component and should not be treated as pure seminoma.
  • Stage I seminoma: the preferred strategy is active surveillance because about 80–85% do not relapse, and salvage therapy is highly effective. If RT is chosen, MRC TE10 supports PAS rather than dogleg, MRC TE18 supports 20 Gy/10 fractions rather than 30 Gy/15 fractions, and MRC TE19 supports carboplatin AUC 7 as an alternative to RT.
  • Stage II seminoma: IIA can be treated with modified dogleg RT; IIB, especially bulky disease or nodes >3 cm, usually favors EP ×4 or BEP ×3. The overall trend is de-escalation: less chemotherapy intensity, smaller RT fields, lower RT dose, and exploration of RPLND, although standard treatment still depends on stage and disease bulk.
