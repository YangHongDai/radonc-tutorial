肺癌基本分類、危險因子、篩檢、檢查流程與分子檢測

一、肺癌基本分類與臨床意義

肺癌是美國第二常見癌症，也是癌症死亡的主要原因。約 85% 為非小細胞肺癌（NSCLC），約 10–15% 為小細胞肺癌（SCLC）。NSCLC 中常見組織型包括腺癌、鱗狀細胞癌與大細胞癌；SCLC 則是高惡性度神經內分泌癌，幾乎都與吸菸相關，且遠端轉移與腦轉移傾向更高。約 15–20% NSCLC 可發生於非吸菸者，特別是非吸菸女性腺癌常有 EGFR 突變。

二、危險因子與臨床表現

肺癌危險因子包括吸菸、氡氣、石棉、家族史、肺纖維化與職業暴露，例如矽塵、鎘、砷、鈹、柴油廢氣與煤煙。症狀依腫瘤位置與分期而異，可能包括咳嗽、呼吸喘、聲音沙啞、胸痛、體重下降、疼痛、肋膜積液與阻塞性肺炎。若有轉移，可因腦轉移出現頭痛或神經學症狀。副腫瘤症候群方面，鱗癌可造成副甲狀腺素相關蛋白（PTHrP）導致高血鈣；SCLC 可造成 SIADH、Lambert–Eaton 症候群或小腦共濟失調。

三、篩檢與肺結節處理

USPSTF 篩檢條件：50–80 歲、20 包年以上吸菸史、目前仍吸菸或戒菸未超過 15 年者，每年接受低劑量 CT（LDCT）篩檢。National Lung Screening 試驗（NLST）顯示，相較胸部 X 光，LDCT 可降低肺癌死亡率。

四、肺癌檢查流程

標準檢查流程包括完整病史與理學檢查、風險因子評估、戒菸諮詢、CBC/CMP、肺功能檢查、胸部 CT、FDG-PET/CT 與腦部 MRI。醫學上可手術性通常至少需要 FEV1 >40%、DLCO >40%。腦部 MRI 對所有 SCLC 皆必要；NSCLC 則第 II 期以上、腫瘤 >4 cm 或 N+ 為必要，第 IB 期 >3 cm 可考慮。

1. Basic classification and clinical meaning

Lung cancer is the second most common cancer in the United States and remains the leading cause of cancer death. About 85% are non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC), and about 10–15% are small cell lung cancer (SCLC). NSCLC commonly includes adenocarcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma, and large-cell carcinoma. SCLC is a high-grade neuroendocrine carcinoma, is strongly associated with smoking, and has a high tendency for distant and brain metastases. About 15–20% of NSCLC can occur in never-smokers, particularly adenocarcinoma in never-smoking women, in whom EGFR mutation is common.

2. Risk factors and clinical presentation

Risk factors include smoking, radon, asbestos, family history, pulmonary fibrosis, and occupational exposures such as silica, cadmium, arsenic, beryllium, diesel exhaust, and coal soot. Symptoms vary by location and stage and may include cough, dyspnea, hoarseness, chest pain, weight loss, pain, pleural effusion, and obstructive pneumonia. Metastatic disease may present with headache or neurologic symptoms from brain metastases. Paraneoplastic syndromes include PTHrP-mediated hypercalcemia in squamous cell carcinoma, and SIADH, Lambert-Eaton syndrome, or cerebellar ataxia in SCLC.

3. Screening and pulmonary nodule management

USPSTF screening criteria: age 50–80 years, at least 20 pack-years of smoking, and currently smoking or having quit within the past 15 years. These patients should receive annual low-dose CT screening. The National Lung Cancer Screening Trial showed that low-dose CT reduces lung-cancer mortality compared with chest radiography.

4. Lung cancer workup

Standard workup includes complete history and physical examination, risk-factor assessment, smoking-cessation counseling, CBC/CMP, pulmonary function testing, chest CT, FDG-PET/CT, and brain MRI. Medical operability usually requires at least FEV1 >40% and DLCO >40%. Brain MRI is required for all SCLC patients. For NSCLC, brain MRI is required for stage II or higher, tumor >4 cm, or node-positive disease, and can be considered for stage IB tumors >3 cm.

5. PD-L1, NGS, and ctDNA

All advanced or metastatic NSCLC patients require PD-L1 and molecular testing. PD-L1 TPS ≥1% is positive; TPS ≥50% can support consideration of immune-checkpoint inhibitor monotherapy. Tissue NGS is preferred. ctDNA liquid biopsy can help with rapid decisions but cannot replace tissue diagnosis.

早期 NSCLC：手術、圍手術期全身治療與 SBRT

一、手術：肺葉切除與次肺葉切除

早期 NSCLC 可手術病人，手術一直是標準治療。傳統標準是肺葉切除加淋巴結評估，且肺葉切除優於全肺切除。對於 ≤2 cm 腫瘤，現代證據支持肺段切除或楔狀切除可不劣於肺葉切除；對 >2 cm 腫瘤，仍偏向肺葉切除。VATS 或機器人輔助微創手術優先，因為疼痛少、恢復快、併發症較少。

二、早期 NSCLC 的圍手術期全身治療

系統性治療通常適用於第 II 期以上，也就是腫瘤 ≥4 cm 或 N+。第 IB 期，尤其 >3 cm 或有高風險特徵，可轉介腫瘤內科討論。第 IA 期通常不需輔助化學治療。

LACE 合併分析顯示，術後以 cisplatin 為基礎的化學治療可帶來約 5.4% 的 5 年 OS 絕對獲益，最大獲益在第 II–III 期；第 IB 期不顯著。現代策略則加入標靶治療與免疫治療。

三、SBRT 在早期 NSCLC 的定位

SBRT 標準上保留給醫學上不可手術、高手術風險，或拒絕手術的第 I–IIA 期 NSCLC。它尚未在大型完成的隨機試驗中證明可取代手術，因此可手術且標準風險病人，SBRT 不應在試驗外常規取代手術。

六、SBRT 合併全身治療

目前早期 NSCLC SBRT 後加化學治療或 ICI 不是標準。多個第 III 期試驗正在或曾經進行，例如 PACIFIC-4（SBRT + durvalumab vs placebo，另有 SBRT + osimertinib 組）、SWOG/NRG S1914（SBRT + atezolizumab vs placebo，暫停）、KEYNOTE-867（SBRT + pembrolizumab vs placebo，因無效性早停）。目前沒有前瞻性資料支持早期 SBRT 常規加化學治療。

1. Surgery: lobectomy versus sublobar resection

Surgery remains the standard for operable early-stage NSCLC. Historically, lobectomy with lymph-node assessment was the gold standard and lobectomy was preferred over pneumonectomy. For tumors ≤2 cm, modern evidence supports segmentectomy or wedge resection as non-inferior to lobectomy. For tumors >2 cm, lobectomy remains preferred. VATS or robotic minimally invasive surgery is preferred when feasible because it reduces pain, speeds recovery, and lowers complications.

Systemic therapy is generally used for stage II or higher disease, meaning tumors ≥4 cm or node-positive disease. 五、腦轉移

腦轉移不等於寡轉移灶；寡轉移通常指顱外寡轉移灶。NSCLC 腦轉移治療依腫瘤負荷與症狀決定：有限腫瘤負荷可考慮 SRS、手術 + 術前／術後 SRS；廣泛腫瘤負荷或軟腦膜轉移可考慮 WBRT、HA-WBRT + memantine、或全身治療。TURBO-NSCLC 回溯性研究評估 TKI ± 腦部 SRS，提示 SRS 組病灶體積較大但可有顱內控制意義；在驅動基因變異型 NSCLC 中，TKI 與 SRS 的治療順序仍需個案化。

2. Oligometastatic disease concept

Oligometastatic disease lies between localized cancer and widespread metastatic disease and may benefit from aggressive local therapy. SBRT is commonly used because it is noninvasive, effective, and convenient. Oligometastatic disease includes de novo oligometastatic disease, metachronous oligorecurrence, oligoprogression, and oligopersistence; these should not be treated as the same entity.

Favorable features include adenocarcinoma, T1a primary, low nodal burden, single metastasis, ECOG 0–1, metachronous oligometastases, driver mutation, and undetectable pre-RT ctDNA.

ASTRO palliative RT guidance suggests that good-performance-status patients can receive higher-dose palliative thoracic EBRT, such as 30 Gy/10 fx or higher, which may provide modest OS benefit but more esophageal toxicity. 5. Brain metastases

Brain metastases are not the same as oligometastases; oligometastatic disease usually refers to extracranial oligometastases. NSCLC brain-metastasis treatment depends on burden and symptoms. Limited burden can be treated with SRS or surgery plus preoperative/postoperative SRS. Extensive burden or leptomeningeal disease can be treated with WBRT, HA-WBRT + memantine, or systemic therapy. In driver-mutated NSCLC, sequencing of TKI and SRS should be individualized.

最終高分重點

肺癌總論的核心是：NSCLC 要做完整分期與分子檢測；SCLC 要特別注意腦轉移、快速全身進展與 PCI/MRI 監測的選擇。 檢查流程包括胸部 CT、PET/CT、腦部 MRI、肺功能檢查（PFT）、病理診斷、縱膈分期與分子檢測。

早期 NSCLC 的核心是：能手術者以手術為標準；≤2 cm 可次肺葉切除，>2 cm 肺葉切除；不可手術或拒絕手術者 SBRT。 周邊型 SBRT 以高 BED 短療程效果佳；中央型/超中央型必須拉長次並嚴格保護支氣管樹、食道、pulmonary vessels。

局部晚期 NSCLC 的核心是：不可手術第 III 期用同步化學放射治療 60 Gy/30 次，再接 durvalumab；EGFR 突變型則接 osimertinib。 不要常規劑量提升到 74 Gy；PORT 只在 R1/R2 最明確，pN2 不常規 PORT。

轉移性 NSCLC 的核心是：標靶治療 > ICI > 化學治療；寡轉移/寡進展可考慮 SBRT/MDT，但免疫治療/targeted era 資料混合，需嚴格選病人。 多發轉移有症狀的疾病則以緩和放射治療改善症狀與生活品質。

Limited-stage SCLC 的核心是：含鉑藥物／etoposide + early 同步胸部放射治療，首選 45 Gy/30 次每日兩次（BID）；化學放射治療後 durvalumab 已成標準；PCI 25 Gy/10 次只給治療反應好且 PS/認知適合者。

Extensive-stage SCLC 的核心是：含鉑藥物／etoposide + atezolizumab 或 durvalumab，之後免疫維持治療；治療反應後可考慮胸部放射治療 30 Gy/10 次；PCI 與 MRI 監測需個案化。

High-yield take-home messages

The core of lung cancer overview is complete staging and molecular testing for NSCLC; SCLC requires special attention to brain metastases, rapid systemic progression, and PCI versus MRI surveillance. Workup includes CT chest, PET/CT, brain MRI, PFT, pathology, mediastinal staging, and molecular testing.

For early-stage NSCLC, surgery is the standard if operable: ≤2 cm can accept sublobar resection, >2 cm generally needs lobectomy. If medically inoperable or refusing surgery, use SBRT. Peripheral SBRT works well with high-BED short courses; central/ultracentral disease requires more fractions and strict protection of bronchial tree, esophagus, and pulmonary vessels.

For locally advanced NSCLC, unresectable stage III uses concurrent chemoRT 60 Gy/30 fx followed by durvalumab; EGFR-mutant disease is followed by osimertinib. Do not routinely escalate to 74 Gy. PORT is clearest for R1/R2 resection; pN2 alone does not mandate PORT.

For metastatic NSCLC, targeted therapy takes priority over immunotherapy, which takes priority over chemotherapy-based decisions when no driver is present. Oligometastatic or oligoprogressive disease can be considered for SBRT/MDT, but evidence in the immunotherapy and targeted-therapy era is heterogeneous and patient selection is critical. Polymetastatic symptomatic disease should receive palliative RT for symptom relief and quality of life.

For limited-stage SCLC, use platinum/etoposide plus early concurrent thoracic RT, preferably 45 Gy/30 fx BID; durvalumab after chemoRT is now standard. PCI 25 Gy/10 fx should be considered only for responders with good performance status and cognition.

For extensive-stage SCLC, use platinum/etoposide plus atezolizumab or durvalumab followed by maintenance immunotherapy. Thoracic RT 30 Gy/10 fx can be considered after response. PCI versus MRI surveillance remains individualized.
