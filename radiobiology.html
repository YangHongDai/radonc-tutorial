總論

Radiobiology 核心總論

放射治療的核心目標是：maximize TCP，minimize NTCP，也就是最大化腫瘤控制、同時最小化正常組織併發症。

有效放射生物劑量不只取決於 total dose，也取決於 dose per fraction、α/β ratio、treatment time，以及正常組織是 serial organ 還是 parallel organ。

一句話理解：RT 殺癌細胞靠 DNA damage；fractionation 的意義是利用正常組織 repair、腫瘤 reoxygenation / redistribution 的差異，讓 TCP 上升但 NTCP 不爆掉。
核心概念臨床意義
TCPTumor control probability；腫瘤控制機率
NTCPNormal tissue complication probability；正常組織併發症機率
Fractionation利用 repair、reoxygenation、redistribution、repopulation 的差異
BED / EQD2把不同 fractionation 轉換成 biological equivalent dose
Serial vs parallel organ決定 constraint 應重視 Dmax / point dose 還是 mean / volume dose

The central goal of radiotherapy is to maximize TCP and minimize NTCP: maximize tumor control while minimizing normal-tissue complications.

The effective biological radiation dose depends not only on total dose, but also on dose per fraction, α/β ratio, total treatment time, and whether the normal tissue behaves as a serial organ or a parallel organ.

One-sentence memory: RT kills cancer cells through DNA damage. Fractionation exploits differences in normal-tissue repair, tumor reoxygenation, redistribution, and repopulation so that TCP rises without unacceptable NTCP.

分割/劑量

Dose、Fractionation 與 Toxicity

Radiation dose 的生物效果取決於四大因素：total dose、dose per fraction、α/β ratio、total treatment time。BED 和 EQD2 可以把不同 fractionation 轉換成可比較的 biological dose，但公式本身不直接處理 treatment time。

因素意義
Total dose總劑量，例如 60 Gy
Dose per fraction每次劑量，例如 2 Gy/Fx 或 10 Gy/Fx
α/β ratio組織對 fraction size 的敏感度
Total treatment time總療程時間，與 repopulation 有關

Linear-quadratic model

SF(D) = e−αD − βD²
參數意義
αlinear component；single-hit cell kill
βquadratic component；double-hit cell kill
α/βαD = βD² 時的 dose，也就是 linear killing 和 quadratic killing 相等的劑量

α/β ratio 本質上代表組織對 fractionation 的敏感度。α/β 越低，代表 survival curve 越彎、越容易受到 fraction size 影響。

Early vs late responding tissues

組織類型α/β ratio對 fraction size 敏感度例子
Early responding tissues約 10較不敏感多數腫瘤、skin、intestinal cells、bone marrow
Late responding tissues約 3較敏感lung、kidney、liver、bladder
Spinal cord約 2非常敏感spinal cord
注意：late-responding tissues = low α/β ratio，且對 fraction size 更敏感。若有 slide 寫 late tissue high α/β，應視為筆誤。

BED 與 EQD2

BED = nd[1 + d/(α/β)]
EQD2 = D[(d + α/β)/(2 + α/β)] = BED / [1 + 2/(α/β)]

Dose per fraction 越大，對低 α/β 組織的 BED / EQD2 增幅越明顯。

Acute vs late toxicity

Toxicity特徵主要相關因素
Acute toxicityinflammationtotal dose
Late toxicityfibrosis、sclerosisdose per fraction；需要用 BED / EQD2 評估
簡單記法：Acute 看總量，Late 看單次量。

As dose per fraction increases, BED/EQD2 rises especially strongly for low-α/β tissues.

DNA 傷害

DNA damage：RT 如何造成細胞死亡？

RT 的主要作用機制是造成 DNA damage，尤其是 DNA double-strand breaks, DSBs。

Damage type每 1 Gy 大約數量生物意義
Single-strand breaks, SSBs約 1000多數容易修復，通常不致命
Double-strand breaks, DSBs約 40最重要的 lethal lesion，可導致 chromosome / chromatid aberrations 和 cell death

直接與間接 DNA damage

機制說明代表 radiation
Direct DNA damageionization 直接打到 DNAhigh LET radiation 較重要，例如 α-particles、carbon ions
Indirect DNA damage水分子被 ionized 產生 free radicals，再攻擊 DNAlow LET photons 較重要，例如 x-rays、γ-rays

Radiation tracks：spurs、blobs、clustered lesions

Radiation typeTrack structure特徵
Low LET：x-rays、γ-raysSpurs約 3 ion pairs、100 eV、直徑約 4 nm
High LET：neutrons 等Blobs約 12 ion pairs、100–500 eV、直徑約 7 nm

多個 spurs / blobs 可形成 clustered lesions。Clustered DNA damage 比單一 SSB 更難修復，因此 high LET radiation 通常有較高 biological effectiveness。

DSB signaling

DSB 發生後，細胞怎麼感知？

DSB 發生後的核心 signaling cascade：

  1. DNA DSB occurs
  2. MRN complex：MRE11 / RAD50 / NBS1 被 recruit 到 damage site
  3. ATM 被 recruit 並 autophosphorylation
  4. ATM phosphorylates H2AX → γ-H2AX
  5. γ-H2AX 作為平台 recruit repair proteins
  6. ATM phosphorylates CHK2，讓 cell cycle arrest，提供修復時間
  7. 53BP1 促進 NHEJ
  8. BRCA1 促進 HR

Chromosome & chromatid aberrations

DSB 的致死效應常不是「斷了就死」，而是錯誤修復後造成 chromosome / chromatid aberrations。某些 chromosome / chromatid aberrations 是 lethal，導致 cell death。

Aberration發生時機例子
Chromosome aberrationsEarly interphase，DNA 尚未 replicatedDicentric chromosome、ring chromosome
Chromatid aberrationsLate interphase，DNA 已 replicatedAnaphase bridge
臨床思維：DSB → misrepair → chromosome aberration → mitotic catastrophe / cell death。

The lethal effect of DSBs is often not simply the break itself, but incorrect repair that produces chromosome or chromatid aberrations. Some aberrations are lethal and lead to cell death.

AberrationTimingExamples
Chromosome aberrationsEarly interphase, before DNA replicationDicentric chromosome, ring chromosome
Chromatid aberrationsLate interphase, after DNA replicationAnaphase bridge
Clinical logic: DSB → misrepair → chromosome aberration → mitotic catastrophe / cell death.

OER

OER，Oxygen Enhancement Ratio

OER = Dose under hypoxic condition / Dose under aerobic condition

OER 代表達到相同 biological effect 時，hypoxic condition 需要多少倍 dose。OER 越高，代表 oxygen 越能增強 radiation cell kill。

Oxygen fixation hypothesis

Oxygen 的作用是把 free radicals 造成的 DNA damage fix 成永久損傷。沒有 oxygen 時，indirect DNA damage 較可能被修復。

概念數值
Oxygen 必須存在時間radiation exposure 當下或 microseconds 內
Free radical lifespan約 10⁻⁵ 到 10⁻⁹ 秒

OER is the dose multiplier required under hypoxic conditions to achieve the same biological effect. Higher OER means oxygen more strongly enhances radiation cell kill.

Oxygen fixation hypothesis

Oxygen fixes free-radical DNA damage into permanent lesions. Without oxygen, indirect DNA damage is more likely to be repaired.

Hypoxia

Tumor hypoxia

Oxygen 只能擴散約 70–200 μm，因此只有接近血管的 tumor cells 能維持較好 oxygenation。遠離血管的區域容易 hypoxic 或 necrotic。

Hypoxia type成因特徵Reoxygenation
Acute hypoxiaTemporary vessel closing / blockage暫時性低氧可快速 reoxygenate
Chronic hypoxia距血管太遠，或 permanent vessel closure常伴 necrosis，較 radioresistant慢速 reoxygenate

Hypoxia 會限制 RT success，因為 hypoxic cells 對 low LET radiation 較 resistant。

類別例子意義

Oxygen diffuses only about 70–200 μm, so tumor cells near blood vessels have better oxygenation. Regions far from vessels are prone to hypoxia or necrosis.

LET

LET，Linear Energy Transfer

LET = ΔE / ΔL

LET 是 particle track 每單位長度沉積的平均能量，單位為 keV/μm。

概念意義
Low LET能量沉積稀疏，較依賴 indirect DNA damage 和 oxygen
High LET能量沉積密集，造成 clustered DNA damage，較不依賴 oxygen

不同 radiation 的 LET

高 LET radiation 包括 α-particles、neutrons；一般來說 α-particles / neutrons > protons > x-rays / γ-rays。對同一種 charged particle，能量越低，LET 越高。

臨床轉譯

Radiobiology clinical translation

一、為什麼 hypoxia 讓 RT 失效？

Low LET photons 很依賴 indirect free radical damage；oxygen 會把 radical-induced damage 固定成 permanent lesion。Hypoxia 時這些 lesion 較可能被 repair，因此 cell kill 下降。Photon OER 約 2.5–3，代表 hypoxic cells 可能需要約 2.5–3 倍 dose 才達到相同效果。

二、為什麼高 LET radiation 對 hypoxic tumors 有吸引力？

High LET radiation 造成 dense ionization 和 clustered DNA damage，較不依賴 oxygen，所以 OER 較低。α-particles 的 OER 約 1，代表幾乎沒有 oxygen enhancement effect。因此 hypoxic tumors 理論上較適合 high LET approaches，例如 carbon ions 或 alpha-emitter radiopharmaceutical therapy。

三、為什麼 fractionation 可保護正常組織？

Fractionation 讓正常組織有時間修復 sublethal damage，尤其是 late-responding tissues。Late tissues 低 α/β，對 fraction size 敏感，所以減小 dose per fraction 可以大幅降低 late toxicity BED。

四、為什麼 SBRT late toxicity 特別需要小心？

SBRT dose per fraction 高，對低 α/β tissues 的 BED 增幅很大。Serial organs 如 spinal cord、brainstem、optic apparatus 只要小 volume hot spot 過高就可能造成嚴重 toxicity，因此看 Dmax / D0.03cc 特別重要。

1. Why does hypoxia reduce RT effectiveness?

Low-LET photons depend heavily on indirect free-radical damage. Oxygen fixes radical-induced damage into permanent lesions. Under hypoxia, these lesions are more likely to be repaired, reducing cell kill. Photon OER is about 2.5–3, meaning hypoxic cells may need 2.5–3 times the dose to achieve the same effect.

2. Why is high-LET radiation attractive for hypoxic tumors?

High-LET radiation creates dense ionization and clustered DNA damage, making it less dependent on oxygen. α-particles have OER around 1, meaning almost no oxygen enhancement effect. Hypoxic tumors may therefore be theoretically suited to high-LET approaches such as carbon ions or alpha-emitter radiopharmaceutical therapy.

3. Why does fractionation protect normal tissue?

Fractionation allows normal tissues to repair sublethal damage, especially late-responding tissues. Late tissues have low α/β and are sensitive to fraction size, so reducing dose per fraction can substantially reduce late-toxicity BED.

4. Why is SBRT late toxicity especially important?

SBRT uses high dose per fraction, which sharply increases BED in low-α/β tissues. Serial organs such as spinal cord, brainstem, and optic apparatus can be severely damaged by a small-volume hotspot, so Dmax / D0.03cc is critical.

總結

最後總結

Radiobiology 可以用一條線串起來：

Radiation → direct / indirect DNA damage → DSB → ATM / γ-H2AX / 53BP1 / BRCA signaling → NHEJ or HR repair；若 repair failed 或 misrepair → chromosome aberration → cell death。

Dose fractionation 的臨床意義是：用 BED / EQD2 把不同 fractionation 轉換成 biological dose；低 α/β late tissues 對大 fraction 特別敏感，所以 SBRT / hypofractionation 必須嚴格看 OAR constraints。

OER、LET、RBE 的核心關係是：hypoxia 讓 low LET photons 變弱；LET 越高，oxygen dependency 越低，RBE 越高，但 RBE 在約 100 keV/μm 後因 overkill 下降。

臨床最重要的一句話：好的放射治療計畫不是只把 tumor dose 開高，而是在 TCP、NTCP、fractionation biology、oxygenation、DNA repair capacity、serial/parallel organ constraints 之間取得最佳平衡。

Radiobiology can be connected as one chain:

Radiation → direct / indirect DNA damage → DSB → ATM / γ-H2AX / 53BP1 / BRCA signaling → NHEJ or HR repair. If repair fails or misrepair occurs → chromosome aberration → cell death.

The clinical meaning of dose fractionation is to convert different schedules into biological dose using BED/EQD2. Low-α/β late tissues are especially sensitive to large fractions, so SBRT/hypofractionation requires strict OAR constraints.

The core relationship among OER, LET, and RBE is: hypoxia weakens low-LET photons; as LET increases, oxygen dependency falls and RBE rises, but RBE falls after about 100 keV/μm because of overkill.

The most important clinical sentence: a good radiotherapy plan is not simply dose escalation to the tumor; it is the optimal balance among TCP, NTCP, fractionation biology, oxygenation, DNA repair capacity, and serial/parallel organ constraints.
